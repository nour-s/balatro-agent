# Telescope

card for your most played poker hand ([[Planet Cards|]] cards in your [[Consumables|]] area give for their specified [[Poker Hands|]] Use a total of [[Planet|]] )

Telescope is a voucher that causes Celestial Packs to contain the Planet Card for the player's currently most played Poker Hand during the run. In case of a tie, Telescope picks the higher-tier hand.

## Usage notes

- Telescope will still function normally if the player already has the designated Planet Card in their Consumables slot, even without Showman ([[Jokers|]], [[Tarot Cards|]], [[Planet Cards|]], and [[Spectral Cards|]] cards may appear multiple times Reach Ante level Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ).

- Telescope picks the higher-tier hand in case of multiple most played hands.

- Purchasing Telescope enables Observatory ([[Planet Cards|]] cards in your [[Consumables|]] area give for their specified [[Poker Hands|]] Use a total of [[Planet|]] ) to appear that run (provided that Observatory is unlocked).

- The Nebula Deck ( Start run with the voucher <br> consumable slot Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) starts with a copy of Telescope.

- Telescope may unintentionally become a hindrance if the player's most played hand is not the hand they plan to score most of their chips with. For example, if the player is scoring with Flushes but is using hands as discards, then the player's most played hand may end up being High Card instead, and all Celestial Packs will have Pluto (Increases hand value by and Available from start. ) instead of Jupiter (Increases hand value by and Available from start. ).

## Synergies

### Decks

- Red Deck ( ): The extra discard can make it easier to get specific hands.

- Yellow Deck ( Start with extra Discover at least items from your [[Collection|collection]] ): The extra starting cash makes it easier to buy more packs early on.

- Painted Deck ( hand size, <br> Joker slot Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ): The +2 hand size makes it easier to get specific hands.

### Poker Hands

Telescope can synergize with any poker hand because it can be upgraded from every Celestial Pack. But it's more difficult to synergize well with multiple hands, since it only guarantees the most played hand.

### Jokers

- Constellation (This Joker gains every time a [[Planet_Cards|]] card is used <br> }} ): Opening Celestial Packs is further rewarded as Constellation gains X0.1 Mult for each planet used.

- Astronomer (All cards and in the shop are Discover every card Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ): Celestial Packs become free.

## Anti-Synergies

### Jokers

- Red Card (This Joker gains when any [[Booster Packs|]] is skipped <br> }} ) has anti-synergy because it incentivizes skipping Booster Packs, while taking Telescope incentivizes not skipping Planet Packs.

- Obelisk (This Joker gains per hand played without playing your most played <br> }} ) encourages playing multiple different hands, which does not work well with Telescope focusing on the player's most played hand.

- Satellite (Earn at end of round per unique card used this run Have at least . Uncommon Economy N/A (Passive) ) will be harder to scale with Telescope.

## Trivia

- Telescope is associated with space, similar to most other planet-related or poker hand level-related objects such as Constellation (This Joker gains every time a [[Planet_Cards|]] card is used <br> }} ) or Space Joker ( chance to upgrade level of played [[Poker Hands|]] ) respectively.

- The reason of Telescope's effect to guarantee the planet card for your most played hand may be a reference to how telescopes are used to watch planets from far away.

- In the demo version of Balatro, Telescope had a completely different effect, where it unlocked a feature called "Planetarium," which allowed you to select one of up to two random Planet Cards when a Blind is selected.

 - Also in the demo, the Crystal Ball ( ) voucher unlocked "Tarot Reading," which had the same effect except for tarot cards.
