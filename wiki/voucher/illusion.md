# Illusion

, and/or a Seal ( can be purchased from the [[The Shop|]] )

Illusion is a voucher that allows playing cards in the shop to have enhancements or editions. Playing cards in the shop will be enhanced 40% of the time, and editioned 20% of the time.

Illusion is currently (v1.0.1o-FULL) bugged, and cards in the shop cannot have seals like the effect states, only enhancements and/or editions. The chance for playing cards to have an edition are unaffected by Hone (, , ) and Glow Up (, , and cards appear more often Have at least 5 Joker cards with Foil, Holographic, or Polychrome (or Negative) ).

## Usage notes

- The player must buy a total of 20 Playing Cards from the shop across runs to unlock Illusion. This is only possible with Magic Trick ( can be purchased from the [[The Shop|]] ). Since buying unhelpful cards will worsen the player's deck, the player may wish buy the required cards after winning a run as the deck doesn't matter anymore.
