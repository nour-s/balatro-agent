# Glow Up

more often (, , )

Glow Up is a Voucher that causes Foil ( Edition Joker (Foil).png), Holographic ( Edition Joker (Holographic).png), and Polychrome ( Edition Joker (Polychrome).png) cards of all types to appear even more often. Polychrome actually appears 7x more often with Glow Up purchased.

## Usage notes

- Glow Up does not increase the probability of Negative ( Joker or Consumable slot Edition Joker (Negative).png) Jokers. Therefore, due to the edition generation mechanism in game, Polychrome ( Edition Joker (Polychrome).png) Jokers actually appear 7x more often with Glow Up. More information can be found on Appearance Rates.

- Hone (, , ) must be purchased in the same run for Glow Up to appear.

- Only playing cards in Standard Packs will be affected by Glow Up. Those that appear in shop by Illusion ( in shop may have an [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]], [[Card Modifiers#Edition|]], and/or a [[Card Modifiers#Seal|]] ) will not.

- The player must have at least 5 Jokers with Foil, Holographic, Polychrome, or Negative at the same time in a run to unlock Glow Up. Note that the unlock isn't given immediately upon acquiring the 5th Joker, at least one further action is needed.

## Trivia

- "Glow up" is an informal term used to describe a thing a person which has become a more attractive and accomplished version of themselves, which refers to editions being the best versions of joker cards.

- The Glow Up voucher is the only voucher in the game to have an outline behind its name that has multiple colors instead of a single color.
