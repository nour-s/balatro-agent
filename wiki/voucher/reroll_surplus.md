# Reroll Surplus

StubThis article is a stub. You can help Balatro Wiki by expanding it.
 ()

Reroll Surplus is a voucher causing rerolls to cost $2 less, making their starting cost $3. This effectively saves $ equal to Rerolls×2.

## Synergies

- Flash Card (This Joker gains per in [[The Shop|the shop]] <br> }} ): Rerolls are cheaper, allowing Flash Card to be scaled at a lower cost.

- Cheaper rerolls allows more cards to be seen at a lower cost and thus can be useful to scale Jokers such as Campfire (This Joker gains for each card , resets when [[Blinds|]] is defeated <br> }} ), Constellation (This Joker gains every time a [[Planet_Cards|]] card is used <br> }} ), and Fortune Teller ( per [[Tarot Cards|]] card used this run <br> }} ).
