# Observatory

Observatory is a tier 2 voucher that causes Planet Cards in your Consumables slots to give X1.5 Mult when their poker hand is played. This bonus is added after Jokers (see Guide: Activation Sequence for detailed information on activation order).

## Usage notes

- The player must use a total of 25 Planet Cards from booster packs across runs to unlock Observatory.

- The corresponding tier 1 voucher Telescope ([[Booster Packs|]] always contain the [[Planet Cards|]] card for your most played [[Poker Hands|]] ) must be purchased in the same run for Observatory to appear.
