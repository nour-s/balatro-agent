# Crystal Ball

( cards may appear in any of the [[Arcana Packs|]] )

Crystal Ball is a voucher that increases Consumable slots by 1.

## Usage notes

- The Magic Deck ( Start run with the voucher and copies of Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) starts with one copy of Crystal Ball.

- Crystal Ball will increase Consumables slots from 2 to 3 on most decks, and from 1 to 2 on Nebula Deck ( Start run with the voucher <br> consumable slot Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ).

## Synergies

### Decks

- Nebula Deck ( Start run with the voucher <br> consumable slot Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ): Mitigates the downside of -1 Consumable Slot.

- Zodiac Deck ( Start run with , , and Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ): Allows you to hold the more abundant Tarot Cards that appear in each shop for use in-ante.

### Jokers

- Showman ([[Jokers|]], [[Tarot Cards|]], [[Planet Cards|]], and [[Spectral Cards|]] cards may appear multiple times Reach Ante level Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ): Allows you to easily find and hold up to 3 copies of the same Consumable. This can be especially useful with multiple copies of The Fool (Creates the last [[Tarot Cards|]] or [[Planet Cards|]] card used during this run<br><small> excluded</small> Available from start. ) or with an abundance of money for rerolls to maximise available deck fixing per shop.

Any Joker that creates consumables benefits from the extra space. This includes:

- Cartomancer (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when [[Blinds|]] is selected <br> Discover every Tarot Card. ): Creates a Tarot Card when entering a blind.

- Vagabond (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] ): Creates a Tarot Card after each hand played when at $4 or less.

- Superposition (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card if poker hand contains an and a <br> ): Creates a Tarot Card after playing a Straight containing an Ace.

- Séance (If [[Poker Hands|]] is a , create a random [[Spectral_Cards|]] card <br> ): Creates a Spectral Card after playing a Straight Flush.

- Sixth Sense (If of round is a single , destroy it and create a [[Spectral Cards|]] card <br> Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ): If first hand of round is a single six, it is destroyed and a Spectral Card is created.

- Hallucination ( chance to create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when any [[Booster Packs|]] is opened <br> Common Effect N/A (Passive) ): Creates a Tarot Card 50% of the time after opening a Booster Pack.

- The additional consumable slot is especially effective with 8 Ball ( chance for each played to create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when scored<br> ), due to it generating consumables through many repetitions of low odds. Crystal Ball raises the ceiling of possible consumable payouts due to 8 Ball's Tarot generation being reliant on retriggers, which may be large enough to consistently generate more than 2 cards.

 - This applies even moreso with Oops! All 6s (Doubles all [[listed probabilities| ]] <br> -> )}} In one hand, earn at least chips Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) Oops!) increasing the odds of 8 Ball.

### Consumables

- The High Priestess (Creates up to random [[Planet Cards|]] cards<br> Available from start. ) and The Emperor (Creates up to random cards<br> Available from start. ): Having more consumable slots available allows you to hold onto an additional card (such as The Fool (Creates the last [[Tarot Cards|]] or [[Planet Cards|]] card used during this run<br><small> excluded</small> Available from start. )) while using and still gaining the full effect of these cards.

 - With Nebula Deck ( Start run with the voucher <br> consumable slot Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) Crystal Ball allows these cards to work at full effect at all, due to the reduced starting consumable slots.

### Vouchers

- Observatory ([[Planet Cards|]] cards in your [[Consumables|]] area give for their specified [[Poker Hands|]] Use a total of [[Planet|]] ): Crystal Ball allows you to gain an additional X1.5 Mult from Observatory by holding an additional Planet Card.

 - Showman ([[Jokers|]], [[Tarot Cards|]], [[Planet Cards|]], and [[Spectral Cards|]] cards may appear multiple times Reach Ante level Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ): Makes it easier to find copies of the same Planet Cards to be held for Observatory.

- Tarot Merchant ( ) and Tarot Tycoon ( ): Makes it easier to fill up all slots with tarot cards to be held for use in-ante.

### Modifiers

- Blue Seal (Creates the card for final played [[Poker Hands|]] of round if in hand<br> ): Allows for up to 3 Planet Cards to be made with blue seals per blind.

 - Mime () can easily fill up these slots by giving 2 Planet Cards per blue seal held in hand.

## Trivia

- A crystal ball is an object commonly associated with witchcraft and fortune-telling for its purported use in telling someone's future. This appears to be the influence behind its relation with other magic and esotericism themed mechanics, namely tarot cards.

- In the demo version of Balatro, Crystal Ball had a completely different effect, where it unlocked a feature called "Tarot Reading," allowing you to choose 1 of up to 2 random tarot cards before the start of each round.

 - In reality, tarot readings follow a similar principle, to which a person chooses either 3 cards for more simple readings or 5 cards for more complex readings to give more insight into their present and potential future events.

 - Also in the demo, the Telescope ([[Booster Packs|]] always contain the [[Planet Cards|]] card for your most played [[Poker Hands|]] ) voucher unlocked "Planetarium," which had the same effect except for planet cards.
