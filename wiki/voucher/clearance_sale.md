# Clearance Sale

()

Clearance Sale is a Voucher that reduces the price of cards, packs, and vouchers in the shop by 25%.

## Usage Notes

- Clearance Sale will reduce the sell value of all Jokers and Consumables, including those held before purchasing Clearance Sale.

- Prices affected by Clearance Sale are always rounded half down.

- Clearance Sale does affect the cost of Vouchers, despite not being mentioned explicitly in the in-game text. Only reroll costs are not affected by it.

- The Inflation ( Permanently raise prices by on every purchase Banned [[Vouchers]]: , ) challenge bans Clearance Sale.

As Clearance Sale's effect is percentage-based, anything purchased that costs $8 or more will have a larger absolute cost reduction of at least $2. This includes:

- All Vouchers.

- The high-cost Uncommon Jokers Joker Stencil ( for each empty [[Jokers|]] slot <br> <small>Joker Stencil included</small> <br> }} ), Fibonacci (Each played , , , , ), and Astronomer (All cards and in the shop are Discover every card Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ).

- Most Jokers with editions.

- Chaos the Clown ( free per shop Common Effect N/A (Passive) ): Allows more reduced cost cards to be seen at no cost.

- Clearance Sale effectively makes every Economy Joker more powerful, as it allows more items to be bought with the same amount of money.

## Anti-Synergies

Any Shop items that already cost the minimum of $1 (or are free) will gain no benefit from Clearance Sale. This includes:

- Base and Enhanced playing Cards purchasable with Magic Trick ( can be purchased from the [[The Shop|]] )/ Illusion ( in shop may have an [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]], [[Card Modifiers#Edition|]], and/or a [[Card Modifiers#Seal|]] ).

- Rental () Jokers.

- Credit Card (Go up to in debt Common Economy N/A (Passive) ), which always costs $1.

- Planet Cards made free with Astronomer (All cards and in the shop are Discover every card Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ).

- Coupon Tag ( ), which makes initial Jokers, Consumables, and Booster Packs free.

### Decks

The Anaglyph Deck ( After defeating each [[Blinds|]], gain a [[File:|24px]] [[Tags|]] Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ), which encourages skipping, makes Clearance Sale less valuable due to its effect only working in the Shop.

### Jokers

Since the sell value of your Jokers and Consumables decreases, any Joker or Consumable that gives a benefit from sell value has a reduced effect:

- Swashbuckler (Adds the sell value of all other owned [[Jokers|]] to Mult <br> }} ) will have less mult.

- Ceremonial Dagger (When [[Blinds|]] is selected, destroy [[Jokers|Joker]] to the right and permanently add its sell value to this <br> }} ) will gain less mult per joker destroyed.

- Temperance (Gives the total sell value of all current [[Jokers]]<br> }} Available from start. ) will give less money.

## Strategies

As Clearance Sale's effect only applies in The Shop, skipping becomes less valuable and therefore less recommended.

## Trivia

- A clearance sale is a special type of sale given out by retailers where items are sold at a heavily discounted price, encouraging people to buy them. These sales usually occur when a store needs to make room for new inventory or if the store is going out of business. The voucher reflects this, as now all items sold in the Shop will have their price reduced by 25%.
