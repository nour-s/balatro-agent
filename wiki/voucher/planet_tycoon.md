# Planet Tycoon

( )

Planet Tycoon is a Voucher that increases the rate that Planet Cards appear in the Shop by approximately 4x. This can make it harder to find important Jokers to increase scoring or earn money if picked up too early.

To increase the rate that Planet cards appear, this voucher changes the weight of planets in the shop from 9.6 to 32. If nothing else impacts the shop weights, this changes the planet chance from approximately 28.6% to 57.1%.

| Situation | Joker Weight | Tarot Weight | Planet Weight | Chance of Planet in Shop slot |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| With Planet Merchant | 20 | 4 | 9.6 | 9.620+4+9.6≈0.286 |
| With Planet Tycoon | 20 | 4 | 32 | 3220+4+32≈0.571 |

- The player must buy a total of 50 Planet Cards from the shop across runs to unlock Planet Tycoon.

## Synergies

### Jokers

- Constellation (This Joker gains every time a [[Planet_Cards|]] card is used <br> }} ) can scale faster with more Planet Cards to use.

- Astronomer (All cards and in the shop are Discover every card Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) allows the player to purchase all Planet Cards in The Shop for free.

- Satellite (Earn at end of round per unique card used this run Have at least . Uncommon Economy N/A (Passive) ) earns $1 at end of round per unique Planet card used this run, which can be easier to find with more Planet Cards in The Shop.

- Campfire (This Joker gains for each card , resets when [[Blinds|]] is defeated <br> }} ) can scale faster with more Planet Cards to buy and sell.

### Vouchers

- Reroll Surplus () and Reroll Glut () allow the player to find more Planet Cards with cheaper rerolls.

- Overstock ( ) and Overstock Plus ( ) allow the player to find even more Planet Cards by seeing more cards per reroll.

## Anti-Synergies

- Jokers that have strong synergies with another Joker, like Photograph () and Hanging Chad (), can be harder to find with more Planet Cards diluting the Shop.

- Cavendish ( <br> ) can be harder to find once Gros Michel (<br> ) goes extinct if Planet Merchant was bought, because it dilutes the Shop.

- Jokers that rely on Tarot Cards, like Fortune Teller ( per [[Tarot Cards|]] card used this run <br> }} ), can be harder to use with less Tarot Cards in the Shop.
