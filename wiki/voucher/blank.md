# Blank

( )

Blank is a voucher that has no effect. However, it is capable of unlocking the next voucher, Antimatter ( ), after it has been purchased at least 10 times in unseeded, non-Challenge runs. Antimatter will not appear before Blank has been purchased during the current run.

## Usage notes

- If the player manages to purchase all unlocked vouchers in a single run, the only voucher that will appear in The Shop is the Blank Voucher.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Yellow Deck ( Start with extra Discover at least items from your [[Collection|collection]] ): The extra cash makes it less of an investment to buy the blank voucher before Antimatter ( ).

- Green Deck ( At end of each Round: <br> per remaining <br> per remaining <br> Earn no Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ): The lack of interest means it's less costly on cash outs to buy Blank.

### Poker Hands

- Blank doesn't synergize with any specific poker hands.

### Jokers

- Blank is usually a good choice to take if you're able to afford it, since getting an extra Joker slot from Antimatter ( ) is almost always beneficial.
