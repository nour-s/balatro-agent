# Hone

Hone is a Voucher that causes Foil ( Edition Joker (Foil).png), Holographic ( Edition Joker (Holographic).png), and Polychrome ( Edition Joker (Polychrome).png) cards of all types to appear more often. Polychrome actually appears 3x more often with Hone purchased.

## Usage notes

- Hone does not increase the probability of Negative ( Joker or Consumable slot Edition Joker (Negative).png) Jokers. Therefore, due to the edition generation mechanism in game, Polychrome ( Edition Joker (Polychrome).png) Jokers actually appear 3x more often with Hone. More information can be found on Appearance Rates.

- Only playing cards in Standard Packs will be affected by Hone. Those that appear in shop by Illusion ( in shop may have an [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]], [[Card Modifiers#Edition|]], and/or a [[Card Modifiers#Seal|]] ) will not.

- Hone can be useful for helping unlock Glow Up (, , and cards appear more often Have at least 5 Joker cards with Foil, Holographic, or Polychrome (or Negative) ), since having 5 editioned Jokers is required to unlock it.
