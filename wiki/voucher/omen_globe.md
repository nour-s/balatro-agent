# Omen Globe

( )

Omen Globe is a voucher that has a 20% chance to replace each individual Tarot Card in an Arcana Pack with a Spectral Card.

## Usage notes

- The player must use a total of 25 Tarot cards from booster packs across runs to unlock Omen Globe.

- Crystal Ball ( ) must be purchased in the same run for Omen Globe to appear.

- Showman ([[Jokers|]], [[Tarot Cards|]], [[Planet Cards|]], and [[Spectral Cards|]] cards may appear multiple times Reach Ante level Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) allows multiple copies of the same Spectral Card to appear in the same pack.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Erratic Deck ( All [[Card Ranks|]] and [[Suits|]] in deck are randomized Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ): The unbalanced starting ranks can give you direction of where to target deck fixing (e.g. taking Grim (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Aces|]] to your hand Available from start. ) when starting with an irregular amount of Aces).

- Abandoned Deck ( Start run with no [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] in your deck Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ): With fewer starting cards, the effect of deck-altering Spectral Cards like Immolate (Destroys random cards in hand, gain Available from start. ) and Grim (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Aces|]] to your hand Available from start. ) is increased.

- Zodiac Deck ( Start run with , , and Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ): Because Zodiac deck starts with Tarot Merchant ( ) it is easier to gain Tarot Cards, which when combined with Spectral Cards gained from Omen Globe can provide strong deck fixing.

- Magic Deck ( Start run with the voucher and copies of Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ): Omen Globe appears more often in this deck since it starts with Crystal Ball ( ).

### Poker Hands

As Spectral Cards provide lots of deck fixing potential (such as card destruction via Immolate (Destroys random cards in hand, gain Available from start. ) and duplication via Cryptid (Create copies of selected card in your hand Available from start. )), it is generally much easier to form higher scoring hands like Four of a Kind and Flush Five.

### Jokers

- Hologram (This Joker gains every time a is added to your deck <br> }} ): Omen Globe makes it easier to find the Familiar (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ), Grim (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Aces|]] to your hand Available from start. ), Incantation (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card_Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Number cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ), and Cryptid (Create copies of selected card in your hand Available from start. ) Spectral Cards, all of which add playing cards into the deck and thus scale Hologram.

- Erosion ( for each card below in your full deck <br> }} ): A single Immolate (Destroys random cards in hand, gain Available from start. ) can take Erosion to +20 Mult on its own, making for a potentially large source of mult.

- Driver's License ( if you have at least Enhanced cards in your full deck <br> }} Enhance 16 cards in your deck Rare Multiplicative Mult Independent Driver'): As Grim (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Aces|]] to your hand Available from start. ), Familiar (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ), and Incantation (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card_Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Number cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ) all create enhanced cards and Cryptid (Create copies of selected card in your hand Available from start. ) can also duplicate an enhanced card, Driver's License can potentially reach 16 enhanced cards faster.

- Mime (): Can benefit heavily from the increased amount of Blue Seals (Creates the card for final played [[Poker Hands|]] of round if in hand<br> ) and Red Seals ().

- Any Joker that can retrigger played cards, such as Sock and Buskin () or Hanging Chad (), can be very powerful, as the modifications Spectral Cards like Aura (Add [[Foil|]], [[Holographic|]], or [[Polychrome|]] effect to selected card in hand Available from start. ) and Talisman (Add a [[Gold Seal|]] to selected card in your hand Available from start. ) apply can be activated multiple times.

- Jokers that create Spectral Cards or Tarot Cards such as Sixth Sense (If of round is a single , destroy it and create a [[Spectral Cards|]] card <br> Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ), Cartomancer (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when [[Blinds|]] is selected <br> Discover every Tarot Card. ), and Hallucination ( chance to create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when any [[Booster Packs|]] is opened <br> Common Effect N/A (Passive) ) pair well with the Spectral cards created by Omen Globe. This is because the deck fixing potential Spectral/Tarot cards provide are more powerful in larger quantities, potentially leading to a snowball effect.

 - Accordingly, any jokers that benefit heavily from deck fixing are also more powerful, such as The Idol (Each played [[Card Ranks|]] of [[Card Suits|]] gives when scored<br><small>Card changes every round</small> In one hand, ), Mail-In Rebate (Earn for each discarded [[Card Ranks|]], ), and Triboulet (Played Kings and Queens each give when scored Find this [[Jokers|Joker]] from the [[Spectral Cards|]] card. ).

### Vouchers

- Clearance Sale () and Liquidation (): Reduces the cost of the packs necessary for Omen Globe's effect.

- Tarot Merchant ( ) and Tarot Tycoon ( ): The deck fixing effects of the tarot cards pair well with the effects of spectral cards gained through Omen Globe.

- Paint Brush ( ) and Palette ( hand size Reduce your hand size down to five cards. ): Can supplement the hand size loss from Ouija (Converts all cards in hand to a single random [[Card Ranks|]]<br> hand size Available from start. ) and Ectoplasm (Add [[Card Modifiers#Editions|]] to a random hand size Available from start. ).

### Stakes

- Playing on Black Stake and above allows Eternal () Jokers to appear. Since Eternal Jokers can't be destroyed, the player can safely use Ankh (Create a copy of a random [[Jokers|]], destroy all other Jokers <br> from copy)}} Available from start. ) to duplicate any Jokers or Hex (Add [[Card Modifiers#Editions|]] to a random [[Jokers|]], destroy all other Jokers Available from start. ) to apply Polychrome ( Edition Joker (Polychrome).png) to any Jokers if they are Eternal. Omen Globe can help the player to find Ankh and Hex more easily.

## Anti-Synergies

### Jokers

- Fortune Teller ( per [[Tarot Cards|]] card used this run <br> }} ): Does not increment if you take a Spectral Card from an Arcana Pack, although it can still be useful since the player can find Medium (Add a [[Purple Seal|]] to selected card in your hand Available from start. ) easier.

### Consumables

- The Fool (Creates the last [[Tarot Cards|]] or [[Planet Cards|]] card used during this run<br><small> excluded</small> Available from start. )'s value decreases as it cannot give Spectral Cards.

### Blinds

- The Pillar ( ): As Spectral cards heavily favour deck fixing, this blind can be an issue if playing the same cards repeatedly.

## Strategy

As mentioned above, Spectral Cards are most useful for their deck fixing potential. With powerful spectral cards such as Deja Vu (Add a [[Red Seal|]] to selected card in your hand Available from start. ) and Immolate (Destroys random cards in hand, gain Available from start. ) able to alter or destroy cards in a more significant ways than tarot cards. It is generally recommended to pair with as many other sources of spectral or tarot cards to maximise this effect to draw powerful cards as consistently as possible. Also, it is generally better to receive Omen Globe early into the run, giving the most time to deck fix and to gain value out of Hex (Add [[Card Modifiers#Editions|]] to a random [[Jokers|]], destroy all other Jokers Available from start. ) and Ankh (Create a copy of a random [[Jokers|]], destroy all other Jokers <br> from copy)}} Available from start. ) before having multiple powerful jokers.

## Trivia

- In superstition an omen is a foretelling of the future events through phenomenon. This is the apparent thematic connection with the prior voucher, Crystal Ball ( ), which is tied to fortune-telling as both relate to non-scientific predictions of the future.
