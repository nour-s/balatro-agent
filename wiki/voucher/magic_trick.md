# Magic Trick

( in shop may have an [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]], [[Card Modifiers#Edition|]], and/or a [[Card Modifiers#Seal|]] )

Magic Trick is a voucher that allows the player to purchase Playing cards directly from the shop for $1 each. Without Illusion ( in shop may have an [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]], [[Card Modifiers#Edition|]], and/or a [[Card Modifiers#Seal|]] ), these Playing cards cannot have enhancements or editions.

## Anti-Synergies

### Decks

 Ghost Deck ( [[Spectral Cards|]] cards may appear in the shop, start with a card Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) decreases the chance of finding cards from Magic Trick, relative to other decks.

### Jokers

- Erosion ( for each card below in your full deck <br> }} ): Adding playing cards with magic trick decreases Erosion by +4 Mult.

As Magic Trick decreases the chance of finding Jokers and Consumables in the shop, any Jokers that gain value from them are weakened. This includes:

- Campfire (This Joker gains for each card , resets when [[Blinds|]] is defeated <br> }} ): Gaining X0.25 Mult per Joker or Consumable sold.

- Astronomer (All cards and in the shop are Discover every card Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ): Making Planet Cards free.

- Fortune Teller ( per [[Tarot Cards|]] card used this run <br> }} ): Gains +1 Mult per tarot card used.

- Constellation (This Joker gains every time a [[Planet_Cards|]] card is used <br> }} ): Gains X0.1 Mult per planet card used.

### Vouchers

- Hone (, , ) and Glow Up (, , and cards appear more often Have at least 5 Joker cards with Foil, Holographic, or Polychrome (or Negative) ): With fewer Jokers seen per shop there are fewer chances for Hone to give them an Edition.

- Planet Merchant ( ), Planet Tycoon ([[Planet Cards|]] ), Tarot Merchant ( ), and Tarot Tycoon ( ) all conflict with the chance to find playing cards by increasing the probabilities of Planet Cards and Tarot Cards respectively.

## Strategies

It is generally not recommended to take Magic Trick without a specific synergy in mind, as the added playing cards will dilute the shop while providing less value than the Jokers and Consumables they replace.

## Trivia

- This Voucher's sprite depicts the Top Hat magic trick, which is usually made with rabbits. In this case, the trick is made with a playing card, like if magically, playing cards start to appear in the shop.
