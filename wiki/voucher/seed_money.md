# Seed Money

()

Seed Money is a voucher that raises the cap on interest earned after defeating a Blind to $10. This means that you can earn interest with up to $50 in reserve. The extra income from Seed Money almost always makes it a worthy investment, especially as it unlocks the Money Tree () voucher.

### Jokers

## Trivia

- "Seed money" is a technical term referring to the initial investment of capital that new projects or businesses receive when they are not self-sustaining and need help covering initial expenses such as marketing, research, or office space. The voucher giving extra interest reflects this; the player is given a little extra income to help sustain their run but not enough to the point where they can carelessly spend.
