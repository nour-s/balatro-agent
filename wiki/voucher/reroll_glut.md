# Reroll Glut

StubThis article is a stub. You can help Balatro Wiki by expanding it.
 ()

Reroll Glut is a voucher causing rerolls to cost $4 less than default, making their starting cost $1. This effectively saves $ equal to Rerolls×4.

## Usage notes

- The player must reroll the shop a total of 100 times across runs to unlock Reroll Glut.

- Reroll Surplus () must be purchased in the same run for Reroll Glut to appear.

## Synergies

- Flash Card (This Joker gains per in [[The Shop|the shop]] <br> }} ): Similar to Reroll Surplus (), rerolls are cheaper, allowing Flash Card to be scaled at a lower cost.

- Cheaper rerolls allows more cards to be seen at a lower cost and thus can be useful to scale Jokers such as Campfire (This Joker gains for each card , resets when [[Blinds|]] is defeated <br> }} ), Constellation (This Joker gains every time a [[Planet_Cards|]] card is used <br> }} ), and Fortune Teller ( per [[Tarot Cards|]] card used this run <br> }} ).
