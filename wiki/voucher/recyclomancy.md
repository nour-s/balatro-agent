# Recyclomancy

()

Recyclomancy is a voucher that increases the amount of discards each round by 1.

## Usage notes

- The player must discard a total of 2500 cards across runs to unlock Recyclomancy.

- Wasteful () must be purchased in the same run for Recyclomancy to appear.

All poker hands benefit from an extra discard, but those that require more discards than usual to play, like Flushes, Full Houses, and Straights, will be grateful for Recyclomancy.

## Trivia

- Recyclomancy is a coined word composed of recycle and -mancy; the latter is a suffix derived from Greek μαντεία (manteia), meaning "divination by means of" or "prophecy through", often used to describe behaviors related to superstition, such as Necromancy and Cartomancy ( Cartomancer (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when [[Blinds|]] is selected <br> Discover every Tarot Card. ) is related to this).
