# Liquidation

()

Liquidation is a Voucher that reduces the price of cards, packs, and vouchers in the shop by 50%. This also reduces the sell value of all jokers, including your present ones. Prices are rounded half down.

## Usage Notes

- Liquidation will reduce the sell value of all Jokers and Consumables, including those held before purchasing Liquidation.

- Prices affected by Liquidation are always rounded half down.

- Liquidation does affect the cost of Vouchers, despite not being mentioned explicitly in the in-game text. Only reroll costs are not affected by it.

- The Inflation ( Permanently raise prices by on every purchase Banned [[Vouchers]]: , ) challenge bans Liquidation.

- The player must redeem at least 10 Vouchers in a single run to unlock Liquidation.

 - This can be done with the Hieroglyph ( Ante, <br> ) and Petroglyph ( Ante, <br> ) Vouchers by giving you 1 extra Ante each.

 - Voucher Tag ( ) can provide an extra Voucher in the Shop.

 - You can also go to Ante 10 or higher, and purchase the Vouchers then.

- Clearance Sale () must be purchased in the same run for Liquidation to appear.

 - This effectively makes Liquidation always cost $7, despite having a base cost of $10.

## Synergies

As Liquidation's effect is percentage based, items with higher prices will have more discounts, for example, these items costs $8 or more, and will have a cost reduction of at least $4:

- All Vouchers.

- The high-cost Uncommon Jokers Joker Stencil ( for each empty [[Jokers|]] slot <br> <small>Joker Stencil included</small> <br> }} ), Fibonacci (Each played , , , , ), and Astronomer (All cards and in the shop are Discover every card Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ).

- Most Rare Jokers, with the exceptions of Stuntman (,<br> hand size Earn at least 100 million (100,000,000) Chips in a single hand. ) and Driver's License ( if you have at least Enhanced cards in your full deck <br> }} Enhance 16 cards in your deck Rare Multiplicative Mult Independent Driver').

- Most Jokers with editions.

### Jokers

- Campfire (This Joker gains for each card , resets when [[Blinds|]] is defeated <br> }} ): Cards are cheaper to buy, making Campfire cheaper to scale.

- Constellation (This Joker gains every time a [[Planet_Cards|]] card is used <br> }} ) and Fortune Teller ( per [[Tarot Cards|]] card used this run <br> }} ): Planet and Tarot cards become cheaper, making scaling these Jokers easier.

- Red Card (This Joker gains when any [[Booster Packs|]] is skipped <br> }} ) and Hallucination ( chance to create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when any [[Booster Packs|]] is opened <br> Common Effect N/A (Passive) ): Since Booster Packs are cheaper, Red Card is cheaper to scale and Hallucination is cheaper to activate.

- Bull ( for each you have <br> }} ) and Bootstraps ( for every you have <br> }} Have at least Jokers at the same time. ): More purchases can be made while still preserving enough money to score high.

- Chaos the Clown ( free per shop Common Effect N/A (Passive) ): Allows more reduced cost cards to be seen at no cost.

- Liquidation effectively makes every Economy Joker more powerful, as it allows more items to be bought with the same amount of money.

### Vouchers

- Overstock ( ) and Overstock Plus ( ): Allows you to see more reduced cost cards per reroll.

## Anti-Synergies

Any Shop items that already cost the minimum of $1 (or are free) will gain no benefit from Liquidation. This includes:

- Base and Enhanced playing Cards purchasable with Magic Trick ( can be purchased from the [[The Shop|]] )/ Illusion ( in shop may have an [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]], [[Card Modifiers#Edition|]], and/or a [[Card Modifiers#Seal|]] ).

- Rental () Jokers.

- Credit Card (Go up to in debt Common Economy N/A (Passive) ), which always costs $1.

- Planet Cards made free with Astronomer (All cards and in the shop are Discover every card Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ).

- Coupon Tag ( ), which makes initial Jokers, Consumables, and Booster Packs free.

### Decks

The Anaglyph Deck ( After defeating each [[Blinds|]], gain a [[File:|24px]] [[Tags|]] Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ), which encourages skipping, makes Liquidation less valuable due to its effect only working in the Shop.

### Jokers

Since the sell value of your Jokers and Consumables decreases, any Joker or Consumable that gives a benefit from sell value has a reduced effect:

- Swashbuckler (Adds the sell value of all other owned [[Jokers|]] to Mult <br> }} ) will have less mult.

- Ceremonial Dagger (When [[Blinds|]] is selected, destroy [[Jokers|Joker]] to the right and permanently add its sell value to this <br> }} ) will gain less mult per joker destroyed.

- Temperance (Gives the total sell value of all current [[Jokers]]<br> }} Available from start. ) will give less money.

## Strategies

As Liquidation's effect only applies in The Shop, skipping becomes less valuable and therefore less recommended.

## Trivia

- Liquidation is the accounting process by which a company is brought to an end, where it will sometimes sell off all of its wares at greatly reduced prices.
