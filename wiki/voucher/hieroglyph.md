# Hieroglyph

( Ante, <br> )

Hieroglyph is a voucher that decreases the ante by 1, at the cost of losing 1 hand each round.

## Usage Notes

- Hieroglyph cannot reduce hands per round below 1 when it's equipped against Golden Needle ( Discards each cost <br><br> hands per round<br> discards per round<br>Start with Banned Jokers: <br>Banned [[Vouchers]]: , ) (Challenge) or The Needle ( ) blind, as players always start with 1 hand per round.

- Hieroglyph can cause certain Boss Blinds or Showdown Blinds to appear on antes they wouldn't normally be able to appear on, since the Boss Blind doesn't change after Hieroglyph is purchased. Rerolling the Blind after purchasing Hieroglyph will change it back into an expected Boss Blind.

- Ante 0 can be reached by purchasing a Hieroglyph on Ante 1.

 - Ante -1 can be reached via incredibly rare circumstances, involving a voucher tag, very fast reflexes, and the seed to roll both Hieroglyph and Petroglyph. A basic demonstration can be found here.

- Hieroglyph actually just reduces the Ante value by 1, without ending or starting Antes, so Ante-related effects (such as The Pillar ( )) will still work as usual.

- If the player obtains Hieroglyph in Ante 9, returns to Ante 8 and fails, non-Endless mode game over scenes will be used (i.e., the background turns red and Jimbo appears). This may be a bug.

## Trivia

- "Hieroglyphs" generally refer to Egyptian hieroglyphs, a writing system that used symbols or pictures to stand for sounds and words. Due to hieroglyphs being an ancient writing system from centuries ago, the effect of the voucher sending the player back an ante can be interpreted as them being sent backwards in time.

- The symbol found on the card art seems to be the Eye of Horus, an ancient Egyptian symbol.
