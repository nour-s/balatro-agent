# Tarot Merchant

( )

Tarot Merchant is a voucher that increases the rate that Tarot Cards appear in the Shop by approximately 2x. While powerful in the late game when a build is already established, this voucher can make it harder to find important Jokers to increase scoring or earn money if picked up too early.

To increase the rate that Tarot cards appear, this voucher changes the weight of tarots in the shop from 4 to 9.6. If nothing else impacts the shop weights, this changes the tarot chance from approximately 14.3% to 28.6%.

| Situation | Joker Weight | Tarot Weight | Planet Weight | Chance of Tarot in Shop slot |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Default | 20 | 4 | 4 | 420+4+4≈0.143 |
| With Tarot Merchant | 20 | 9.6 | 4 | 9.620+9.6+4≈0.286 |

### Vouchers

- Reroll Surplus () and Reroll Glut () allow the player to find more Tarot Cards with cheaper rerolls.

### Poker Hands

- High value hands like Four of a Kind, Five of a Kind, Flush Five, and Flush House are easier to deck fix for with more access to duplicating cards with Death (Select cards, convert the card into the card<br> Available from start. ), and by destroying unwanted cards with The Hanged Man (Destroys up to selected cards Available from start. ) and indirectly through Justice (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ).

## Anti-Synergies

- Jokers that have strong synergies with another Joker, like Photograph () and Hanging Chad (), can be harder to find with more Tarot Cards diluting the Shop.

- Cavendish ( <br> ) can be harder to find once Gros Michel (<br> ) goes extinct if Tarot Merchant was bought, because it dilutes the Shop.

- Jokers that rely on Planet Cards, like Constellation (This Joker gains every time a [[Planet_Cards|]] card is used <br> }} ), can be harder to use with less Planet Cards in the Shop.

## Trivia

- Planet Merchant ( ) is the matching voucher for Planet Cards.

- "X Merchant" is slang from sports that implies that a player "trades" solely on one specific thing, e.g., foul merchant, free throw merchant, etc. This means that they make their money from drawing fouls and taking free throws instead of scoring some other way. Tarot Merchant () means that the run relies on specifically Tarot Cards to win, because they show up more frequently in the Shop. It also has a double meaning, as the player also buys more Tarot Cards, as they would from a merchant.
