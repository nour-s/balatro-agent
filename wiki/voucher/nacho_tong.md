# Nacho Tong

()

Nacho Tong is a voucher that increases the number of hands per round by 1.

## Usage Notes

- The player must play a total of 2,500 cards across runs to unlock Nacho Tong.

- Grabber () must be purchased in the same run for Nacho Tong to appear.

## Trivia

- According to LocalThunk, this name comes from the joke of calling one's hands Nacho Tongs1]—in which Nacho is a type of Texas-Mexican tortilla chip topped with cheese and often additional savory toppings (as depicted in its art), and tong is a gripping device.

- Nacho Tong's art is based on the hand of LocalThunk himself.2]
