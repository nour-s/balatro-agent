Temperance is a Tarot card that grants the player the total sell value of all their Jokers, up to $50.

## Usage notes

- Using the Vouchers Clearance Sale () and Liquidation () will lower the total sell value of all Jokers and reduces the amount of money gained from Temperance.

## Trivia

- Temperance's ability to give the current sell values of all Jokers in the player's possession mirrors temperance's modern meaning (as a virtue) of abstinence and self restraint. The player is rewarded for restraining themself from selling a high value joker by getting its sell value later in a tarot card instead.

- Temperance is one of the four Tarot Cards that appears on the artwork for one of the normal Arcana Packs. The other Tarot Cards are The Fool (Creates the last [[Tarot Cards|]] or [[Planet Cards|]] card used during this run<br><small> excluded</small> Available from start. ), The Hierophant (Enhances selected cards to [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ) and The World (Converts up to selected cards to Available from start. ).
