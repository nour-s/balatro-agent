# The Moon

Clubs

The Moon is a Tarot card that allows the player to change the suit of up to three cards to  Clubs.

## Usage notes

- When using The Moon on a face-down card, the card will briefly flip over and be revealed. This can be helpful on Boss blinds such as The Fish ( ) and The Wheel ( ).

## Synergies

- Jokers that synergize with the  Club suit, like Gluttonous Joker () and Onyx Agate (), benefit from The Moon.

- The Moon can also help trigger Blackboard ( ) and Seeing Double ( if played hand has a scoring card and a scoring card of any other suit Play a hand that contains <abbr title="Other suits that count as clubs (e.g. wild suits) with rank 7 will also count."></abbr> ) easier.

## Anti-Synergies

- Checkered Deck ( ) doesn't start with any  Club Cards, which can make it very awkward to use The Moon.

- The Club ( ) debuffs  Club Cards, making them unable to be scored.

## Trivia

- The Moon is part of a group of celestial body themed Tarot Cards that change the suit of playing cards. The other Tarot Cards in this group are The World (Converts up to selected cards to Available from start. ), The Sun (Converts up to selected cards to Available from start. ), and The Star (Converts up to selected cards to Available from start. ).
