# The Hanged Man

The Hanged Man is a Tarot card that allows the player to permanently destroy two cards from their deck.

## Usage notes

- Destroying a selected card while battling Cerulean Bell ( ) will allow the player to freely select other cards for that hand.

## Strategies

- The Hanged Man is best used to cut the unwanted cards out of the player's deck in order to make their most desired cards easier to find. For example, if the player intends on playing only face cards, the player can use the Hanged Man to destroy the numbered and Ace cards.

- The Hanged Man is also useful if the player has a Joker that scales when cards are destroyed, such as Erosion ( for each card below in your full deck <br> }} ) and Canio (This Joker gains when a card is destroyed <br> }} Find this [[Jokers|Joker]] from the [[Spectral Cards|]] card. ).

## Synergies

### Decks

All decks benefit from the Hanged Man, as it is generally recommended for the player to thin out their deck as early as possible. But there are two that stand out:

- Magic Deck ( Start run with the voucher and copies of Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) starts off with 2 copies of The Fool (Creates the last [[Tarot Cards|]] or [[Planet Cards|]] card used during this run<br><small> excluded</small> Available from start. ), meaning if found early, the player can create 2 extra copies of the Hanged Man and give them a major head start in terms of deck fixing.

- Zodiac Deck ( Start run with , , and Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) has Tarot Merchant ( ), making it easier to find copies of the Hanged Man in the Shop.

### Jokers

- Erosion ( for each card below in your full deck <br> }} ) gains +4 Mult every time a card is destroyed; destroying 2 cards with the Hanged Man means that Erosion will always gain +8 Mult.

- Glass Joker (This Joker gains for every [[Glass_Cards|]] that is destroyed <br> }} Have at least [[Glass_Cards|]] ) gains X0.75 Mult for every Glass Card (<br> ) destroyed.

- Canio (This Joker gains when a card is destroyed <br> }} Find this [[Jokers|Joker]] from the [[Spectral Cards|]] card. ) gains X1 Mult for each face card destroyed.

## Anti-Synergies

### Jokers

- Blue Joker ( for each remaining card in <br> }} ) becomes weaker after using The Hanged Man.

## Trivia

- Destroyed cards will be visually burned, except for Glass Cards (<br> ), which will be visually broken as if they were broken on use.

- The Hanged Man has meanings that correlate with the concepts of "surrender" and "letting go", emphasizing the card's ability to destroy up to 2 selected cards.
