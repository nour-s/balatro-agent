# The High Priestess

(Must have room)

The High Priestess is a Tarot card that creates two random unlocked Planet Cards in the player's consumable area.

## Usage notes

The High Priestess is best used if the player has Constellation (This Joker gains every time a [[Planet_Cards|]] card is used <br> }} ) as it gains X0.1 Mult for every Planet Card used, meaning that the Joker will consistently gain X0.2 Mult for every planet spawned the High Priestess if has at least two free slots in their consumable area.

Although a more niche strategy, if the Planet Cards spawned by the High Priestess are irrelevant to the player, keeping them in the consumable area stops them from showing up again in the Shop and in Celestial Packs (assuming the player doesn't also have Showman ([[Jokers|]], [[Tarot Cards|]], [[Planet Cards|]], and [[Spectral Cards|]] cards may appear multiple times Reach Ante level Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) )). This makes it easier for the player to find their desired Planet Card.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Magic Deck ( Start run with the voucher and copies of Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) has 2 copies of The Fool (Creates the last [[Tarot Cards|]] or [[Planet Cards|]] card used during this run<br><small> excluded</small> Available from start. ), allowing the player to create extra copies of the High Priestess if found early.

- Zodiac Deck ( Start run with , , and Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) has Tarot Merchant ( ) making it easier to find copies of the High Priestess in the Shop.

### Jokers

- Campfire (This Joker gains for each card , resets when [[Blinds|]] is defeated <br> }} ) will gain X0.25 Mult for every Planet Card sold.

- Constellation (This Joker gains every time a [[Planet_Cards|]] card is used <br> }} ) will gain X0.1 Mult for every Planet Card used.

- Fortune Teller ( per [[Tarot Cards|]] card used this run <br> }} ) gains +1 Mult every time the High Priestess is used.

- Satellite (Earn at end of round per unique card used this run Have at least . Uncommon Economy N/A (Passive) ) will pay out an extra $1 per unique Planet Card used in a run. If used early the High Priestess can potentially spawn Planet Cards not yet used by the player, boosting Satellite.

### Vouchers

- Tarot Merchant ( ) and Tarot Tycoon ( ) increase the possibility of the High Priestess appearing in the Shop.

## Anti-Synergies

- Nebula Deck ( Start run with the voucher <br> consumable slot Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) will only have one consumable slot without Crystal Ball ( ), making the High Priestess only spawn one Planet Card. Additionally, Nebula Deck already starts with Telescope ([[Booster Packs|]] always contain the [[Planet Cards|]] card for your most played [[Poker Hands|]] ), meaning that the player will no longer have to try their luck with the High Priestess and can find their most wanted Planet Card by opening Celestial Packs.

- Astronomer (All cards and in the shop are Discover every card Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) makes all Planet Cards and Celestial Packs free, but not the High Priestess, making it a worse value option for generating Planet Cards.
