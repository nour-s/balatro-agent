# The World

Spades

The World is a Tarot card that allows the player to change the suit of up to three cards to  Spades.

## Usage notes

- When using The World on a face-down card, the card will briefly flip over and be revealed. This can be helpful on Boss blinds such as The Fish ( ) and The Wheel ( ).

## Synergies

- Checkered Deck ( ): The World can further increase the amount of  Spades this deck starts with.

- Jokers that synergize with the  Spade suit, like Wrathful Joker () and Arrowhead (), benefit from The World.

- The World can also help trigger Blackboard ( ) easier.

## Anti-Synergies

- The Goad ( ) debuffs  Spade Cards, making them unable to be scored.

## Trivia

- The World is part of a group of celestial body themed Tarot Cards that change the suit of playing cards.
