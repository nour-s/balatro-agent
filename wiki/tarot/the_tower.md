### Jokers

- Driver's License ( if you have at least Enhanced cards in your full deck <br> }} Enhance 16 cards in your deck Rare Multiplicative Mult Independent Driver') will gain X3 Mult when 16 enhanced cards are in the player's deck; Stone cards count towards the total.

- Fortune Teller ( per [[Tarot Cards|]] card used this run <br> }} ) gains +1 Mult every time the Tower is used.

- Stone Joker (Gives for each [[Stone Card|]] in your <br> }} ) gains +25 Chips for every Stone Card added to the player's deck.

- The Idol (Each played [[Card Ranks|]] of [[Card Suits|]] gives when scored<br><small>Card changes every round</small> In one hand, ) will provide X2 Mult when a card of a specific rank and suit is scored; Stone cards cannot be selected, and so will increase the chance of hitting your preferred card.

### Poker Hands

With the except of five-hand poker hands like Straights and Flushes*, all poker hands work with the Tower as Stone Cards can be played in any hand. However, if the player wants to only play Stone Cards, then they're better off leveling up High Card.
