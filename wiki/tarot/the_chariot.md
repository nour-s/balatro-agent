# The Chariot

The Chariot is a Tarot card that allows the player to enhance one card into a Steel Card ( ).

- When using The Chariot on a face-down card, the card will briefly flip over and be revealed. This can be helpful on Boss blinds such as The Fish ( ) and The Wheel ( ).

- When facing The Pillar ( ), using The Chariot on a debuffed card will make it a "new" card and thus no longer debuffed.
