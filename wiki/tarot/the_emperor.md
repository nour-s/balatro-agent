# The Emperor

The Emperor is a Tarot card that creates two random Tarot Cards in the player's consumable area (excluding the Emperor itself).

## Usage Notes

- The Bram Poker ( Jokers no longer appear in the [[The Shop|]] Eternal <br>, <br>, ) challenge starts the player with a copy of The Emperor.

- If the Emperor creates Tarot Cards that the player doesn't need, keeping them in the consumable slot prevents them from showing up again in the Shop or in Arcana Packs (assuming the player doesn't also have Showman ([[Jokers|]], [[Tarot Cards|]], [[Planet Cards|]], and [[Spectral Cards|]] cards may appear multiple times Reach Ante level Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) )). This makes it easier for the player to find the Tarot Cards that the player actually needs.

- The Emperor will never generate itself, unless the player has Showman ([[Jokers|]], [[Tarot Cards|]], [[Planet Cards|]], and [[Spectral Cards|]] cards may appear multiple times Reach Ante level Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ).

## Synergies

### Jokers

- Fortune Teller ( per [[Tarot Cards|]] card used this run <br> }} ) gains +1 Mult for every Tarot Card used. Given that the Emperor spawns two Tarot Cards on top of being consumed itself, that means Fortune Teller will gain +3 Mult if the player has at least two consumable slots free and uses every card spawned by the Emperor.

- Campfire (This Joker gains for each card , resets when [[Blinds|]] is defeated <br> }} ) will gain X0.25 Mult for every Tarot Card sold.

- The Fool (Creates the last [[Tarot Cards|]] or [[Planet Cards|]] card used during this run<br><small> excluded</small> Available from start. ), especially if obtained from The Emperor. If The Fool was one of the two cards created by The Emperor, The Fool (Creates the last [[Tarot Cards|]] or [[Planet Cards|]] card used during this run<br><small> excluded</small> Available from start. ) can be immediately used to create The Emperor again, while retaining the other Tarot card created by the original Emperor Tarot card. If the maximum number of slots for consumables is 2, the other Tarot card can then be used, then have the newer Emperor Tarot card used to spawn in two new Tarot cards.

### Vouchers

- The Crystal Ball ( ) voucher gives +1 consumable slot, making it easier to get both Tarot Cards from the Emperor by having more room.

- Tarot Merchant ( ) and Tarot Tycoon ( ) will make the Emperor appear in the Shop more often.

## Anti-Synergies

- Nebula Deck ( Start run with the voucher <br> consumable slot Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) will only have one consumable slot without Crystal Ball ( ), making the Emperor only spawn one Tarot Card.

## Trivia

- The Emperor can theoretically be infinitely duplicated so long as it creates The Fool (Creates the last [[Tarot Cards|]] or [[Planet Cards|]] card used during this run<br><small> excluded</small> Available from start. ) when used. The odds of this happening once is only 1 in 10, and the chance of it happening many times in a row is exponentially lower. However, if Perkeo (Creates a [[Negative|]] copy of random [[Consumables|]] card in your possession at the end of the [[The Shop|]] Find this [[Jokers|Joker]] from the [[Spectral Cards|]] card. Legendary Effect N/A (Passive) ) is used to obtain a Negative copy every other Tarot Card, this can become a guarantee, and the player can obtain infinite copies of a particular Tarot Card.

- The Emperor is one of the two Tarot Cards that appears on the artwork for one of the Jumbo Arcana Packs, the other one is Death (Select cards, convert the card into the card<br> Available from start. ).
