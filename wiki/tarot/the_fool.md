# The Fool

card used during this run
The Fool excluded

The Fool is a Tarot card that allows the player to create another copy of the last Tarot or Planet card used during the current run, excluding The Fool itself.

## Usage notes

- The Fool cannot be used if your consumable area is full.

- If The Fool was your last used Tarot or Planet card, you cannot use The Fool again until you use a different card.

- Spectral cards do not affect The Fool at all, thus the player cannot create a copy of the last Spectral card they used before The Fool.

 - This also applies to Spectral cards from Arcana and Celestial Packs ( The Soul (Creates a [[Legendary Jokers|]] Joker <br> Available from start. ) and Black Hole (Upgrade every [[Poker Hands|]] by level Available from start. )) and Spectral Cards from Omen Globe ( cards may appear in any of the [[Arcana Packs|]] ) since they are still Spectral cards.

- Editions on Tarot/Planet cards, such as the Negative ( Joker or Consumable slot Edition Joker (Negative).png) edition from Perkeo (Creates a [[Negative|]] copy of random [[Consumables|]] card in your possession at the end of the [[The Shop|]] Find this [[Jokers|Joker]] from the [[Spectral Cards|]] card. Legendary Effect N/A (Passive) ), are not copied by The Fool.

- The Magic Deck ( Start run with the voucher and copies of Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) starts the player with two copies of The Fool.

## Trivia

- The icon of the final achievement of Balatro, Completionist++, is a golden version of The Fool's drawing.

- The Fool is one of the four Tarot Cards that appears on the artwork for one of the normal Arcana Pack. The other Tarot Cards are Temperance (Gives the total sell value of all current [[Jokers]]<br> }} Available from start. ), The Hierophant (Enhances selected cards to [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ) and The World (Converts up to selected cards to Available from start. ).
