# Judgement

Judgement is a Tarot card that allows the player to create a random Joker.

## Usage notes

- Like other randomly created Jokers, the cards created by this will not be Perishable (), Eternal (), or Rental () (though they may have an Edition).

- There is a 70% chance of generating a Common Joker, a 25% chance of generating an Uncommon Joker, and a 5% chance of generating a Rare Joker when using this card. Judgement cannot generate a Legendary Joker.

- You cannot use Judgement if you have no empty Joker slots remaining (the Use button will be greyed out).

## Anti-Synergies

### Decks

- Non-Perishable ( All Jokers are Eternal Banned Jokers: , ; , , ; ; , , and <br>Banned [[Blinds]]: ) makes it very risky to use Judgement, as it spawns an Eternal () Joker on this deck.

## Strategies

- Judgement is best used in the early game when the player doesn't have a strong economy and needs Jokers to keep them afloat. In the earlier antes, sometimes it's better to simply spend the $3 and get a random Joker, rather than using re-rolls and burning through money. Even if the Joker created by Judgement isn't needed or optimal, it can always be sold.
