# The Ox

The Ox is a Boss Blind that sets the player's money to $0 if they play their most played hand during a run. In case of a tie between multiple hands, The Ox will pick one at random.

## Usage Notes

- The most played hand is determined once the player has defeated the previous boss and enters the current ante. Changing the most played hand during the Small/Big Blind will not change the chosen hand, and rerolling into The Ox using e.g. Director's Cut (Reroll [[Blinds|Boss Blind]] time per Ante, per roll Base Director') will pick the most played hand from the start of the ante.

- The Ox only applies to the scoring hand, not the hands that it contains. For example, playing a Straight Flush with the most played hand being Flush or Straight does not clear the player's money.

- Royal Flush is not considered a separate hand by the Ox.

## Strategies

- If the player plays their most played hand and has the Matador (), the boss ability will be triggered, then the Matador will provide $8.

- The boss ability can provide an advantage for players who have less than $0, since their money is technically increased.

 - With the Credit Card (Go up to in debt Common Economy N/A (Passive) ), where players can go up to -$20 in debt, players can earn up to an extra $20 with the boss ability.

- Obelisk (This Joker gains per hand played without playing your most played <br> }} ) already encourages players to not play their most played hand, making this Boss Blind irrelevant in most cases.

- Vagabond (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] ) is guaranteed to trigger if the player plays their most played hand.

## Trivia

- The Icon for The Ox is the Phoenician letter Aleph (𐤀), which represents an ox head in the hieroglyphics from which it is derived.

### In other media

- The Ox makes an appearance as a boss enemy in Vampire Survivors.
