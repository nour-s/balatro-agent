# The Fish

The Fish is a boss blind that draws cards that are face down once a hand is played. For example, if a player plays 3 cards, the 3 cards that will be drawn in their place will have their face down.

## Strategies

- The Sort feature at the bottom of the screen can narrow down what the face-down cards are. Sorting by rank sorts in descending order, with Aces being the highest rank and 2s being the lowest. Sorting by suit sorts in the order of Spades, Hearts, Clubs, then Diamonds, with each suit being sorted by rank.

- You can briefly see face-down cards when using certain tarot or spectral cards, e.g., The Empress (Enhances selected cards to [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ) or Ouija (Converts all cards in hand to a single random [[Card Ranks|]]<br> hand size Available from start. ).

- You can use your Discards to discard cards that are face down. The cards that will be drawn in their place will be face up, unlike cards drawn in the place of played cards.

## Trivia

- The Icon for The Fish is the Phoenician letter Dalet (𐤃), which represents a door or a fish in the hieroglyphics from which it is derived.
