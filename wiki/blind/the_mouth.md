# The Mouth

StubThis article is a stub. You can help Balatro Wiki by expanding it.

The Mouth is a boss blind that restricts the player to only playing hands with the hand type that they have used on the first hand of this round. For example, if the player has triggered a Full House on their first hand, they will only be able to score hands with a Full House. Attempting to score other hand types will result in no score being added -- the game says "Not allowed!" and forces Chips and Mult to 0, wasting the hand.

## Strategies

- If the player's strategy revolves mainly around a hand that is difficult to draw (such as Four of a Kind), it is recommended to use Jokers that help with looking for cards like Drunkard ( discard each round Common Effect N/A (Passive) ) to prevent getting locked into an undesirable hand due to not being able to play the correct hand as the first hand.

- Be sure to remember not to play High Card (unless you can clear the blind this way) to activate DNA (If of round has only card, ) or Sixth Sense (If of round is a single , destroy it and create a [[Spectral Cards|]] card <br> Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ).

- Consider selling Burglar (When [[Blind|]] is selected, ) as you will be forced to play a hand type that is drawn in your first hand.

- It is important to note that Ice Cream (<br> ) and Seltzer () still lose value even if the played hand isn't the hand that you played on your first hand.
