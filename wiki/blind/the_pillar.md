# The Pillar

The Pillar is a Boss Blind that debuffs all of the playing cards that were previously played in the current Ante. In other words, cards played in the current Ante's Small Blind ( ) and/or the Big Blind ( ) (if played at all) will be debuffed.

## Notes

- Changing Ante with Hieroglyph ( Ante, <br> ) or Petroglyph ( Ante, <br> ) does not affect the Pillar, and the played cards will still be debuffed.

## Strategies

- Skipping both the Small Blind ( ) and the Big Blind ( ) would result in no debuffed cards.

- If a debuffed card is modified by a tarot card, the card will now be "different" and thus no longer be debuffed. However, this does not apply to suit and rank changes, as well as the Aura (Add [[Foil|]], [[Holographic|]], or [[Polychrome|]] effect to selected card in hand Available from start. ) Spectral card and cards that add seals like Trance (Add a [[Blue Seal|]] to selected card in your hand Available from start. ).

- If the player converts a non-debuffed card into a debuffed card using Death (Select cards, convert the card into the card<br> Available from start. ), the non-debuffed card will become debuffed too, even though the player technically has not played that card that Ante. The opposite is also true; if the player converts a debuffed card into a non-debuffed card, it will remove the debuff.

- Similarly, using Cryptid (Create copies of selected card in your hand Available from start. ) on a debuffed card will spawn two additional debuffed cards.

## Trivia

- The icon for The Pillar is the Phoenician letter Samekh (𐤎), which represents a pillar in the hieroglyphics from which it is derived.
