# The Goad

StubThis article is a stub. You can help Balatro Wiki by expanding it.

The Goad is a Boss Blind that debuffs all of the cards with a  Spade suit.

## Strategies

- This boss hard counters Wrathful Joker () and Arrowhead () by debuffing  Spade cards.

 - However, debuffed  Spade cards will not prevent Blackboard ( ) or Flower Pot ( if poker hand contains a card, card, card, ) from triggering.

- Smeared Joker ( and count as the same suit, and count as the same suit Have at least [[Wild Cards|]] in your deck Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) can be harmful to your build by counting  Club cards as  Spade cards, which result in them being debuffed.

- Having consumables like The Moon (Converts up to selected cards to Available from start. ) to change your cards into a different suit for this blind can help you score cards. However, using The Lovers (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ) will result in a debuffed card since a Wild Card () counts as a  Spade.

- Checkered Deck ( ) has half of its cards debuffed since it starts with 26  Spade cards.

## Trivia

- The icon for the Goad is the Phoenician letter Lamedh (𐤋), which represents a shepherd’s crook or goad in the hieroglyphics from which it is derived.
