# The Plant

The Plant is a Boss Blind that debuffs all of the face cards. This includes cards with a "King", "Queen", or "Jack" rank, and cards affected by Pareidolia (All cards are considered cards Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ).

## Usage notes

- The Mad World ( Extra no longer earn money<br>Earn no at end of round Eternal, Negative <br>Eternal Ranks 2 through 9 only, 32 cards totalBanned Blinds: ) challenge bans The Plant.

## Strategies

- Pareidolia (All cards are considered cards Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) makes The Plant debuff every card as every card is considered to be a face card.

- Jokers that interact with face cards, such as Photograph (), Smiley Face (), and Baron () are completely unusable.

- For builds that rely on face cards, the only method to "defeat" The Plant is to avoid it entirely.

 - Luchador (Sell this card to disable the current [[Blinds|]] Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) and Chicot (Disables effect of every [[Blinds|]] Find this [[Jokers|Joker]] from the [[Spectral Cards|]] card. ) disable Boss Blinds, making face cards usable again.

 - Director's Cut (Reroll [[Blinds|Boss Blind]] time per Ante, per roll Base Director') and Retcon (Reroll [[Blinds|Boss Blind]] times, ) allow the player to pay $10 to reroll the current Boss Blind.

 - Boss Tag ( ) rerolls the Boss Blind for no cost.

- On the contrary, Ride the Bus (This Joker gains per hand played without a scoring card <br> }} ) actually benefits from The Plant. Its Mult bonus will not be reset, even if a (debuffed) face card is played.

## Trivia

- The icon for The Plant is the Phoenician letter Tsade (𐤑), which represents a papyrus plant or fish Hook in the hieroglyphics from which it is derived.

- The Plant debuffs face cards because plants do not have faces.1]

- This is the only non-showdown boss blind to be banned in a challenge.
