# The Tooth

The Tooth is a Boss Blind that makes the player lose $1 for each card they play. For example, if the player plays a Straight in one hand, which would consist of 5 cards, the player will lose $5. Unscored cards also cause the player to lose money; for example, a Pair with three unscored cards would also lose $5.

It should be noted that even if the player has $0 in possession, the boss ability will continue to trigger and push the player into debt.

## Strategies

- Picking up Credit Card (Go up to in debt Common Economy N/A (Passive) ) before the round or keeping a Wraith (Creates a random [[Rare Jokers| ]], sets money to Available from start. ) in hand can help the player recover from going into debt.

- Gold Cards ( ) and Gold Seal ()s can help offset the penalty.

 - Golden Ticket (Played [[Gold Cards|]] ) allows the player to play Gold Cards for a net increase of $1, or more if the player has Retrigger Jokers.

- Any Economy Joker can help offset The Tooth's penalty, although the player may still lose out on Interest.

 - Mail-In Rebate (Earn for each discarded [[Card Ranks|]], ), Golden Joker (Earn at end of round Common Economy N/A (Passive) ), and To Do List (Earn if [[Poker Hands|]] is a [[Poker Hands|]], ) are Common Jokers, making them relatively easy to obtain.

- Vagabond (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] ) has the potential to benefit as the Money penalty may make the played Poker Hand fulfill its condition, generating Tarot cards.

- Ideally, Poker Hands with few cards such as High Card or Pair should be used to defeat The Tooth so as to minimize the Money penalty.

- The icon for The Tooth is the Phoenician letter Shin (𐤔), which represents teeth in the hieroglyphics from which it is derived.
