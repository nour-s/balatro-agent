# The Club

StubThis article is a stub. You can help Balatro Wiki by expanding it.

The Club is a boss blind that debuffs all of the cards with a  Club suit.

## Strategies

- This boss hard counters Gluttonous Joker () and Onyx Agate () by debuffing  Club cards.

 - Seeing Double ( if played hand has a scoring card and a scoring card of any other suit Play a hand that contains <abbr title="Other suits that count as clubs (e.g. wild suits) with rank 7 will also count."></abbr> ) is also useless, since  Club cards are debuffed.

 - However, debuffed  Club cards will not prevent Blackboard ( ) or Flower Pot ( if poker hand contains a card, card, card, ) from triggering.

- Smeared Joker ( and count as the same suit, and count as the same suit Have at least [[Wild Cards|]] in your deck Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) can be harmful to your build by counting  Spades cards as  Club cards, which result in them being debuffed.

- Having consumables like The Star (Converts up to selected cards to Available from start. ) to change your cards into a different suit for this blind can help you score cards. However, using The Lovers (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ) will result in a debuffed card since a Wild Card () counts as a  Club.

## Trivia

- The Icon for The Club is the Phoenician letter Gimel (𐤂), which represents a throwing stick or camel in the hieroglyphics from which it is derived.

 - Club can also refer to a type of throwing stick, especially in historical or anthropological contexts. So this naming is some kind of pun.

- This is the only blind whose name and affected playing card suit match.
