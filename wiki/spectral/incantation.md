# Incantation

numbered cards to your hand

Incantation is a Spectral card that allows the player to quickly add multiple enhanced Numbered cards to their hand at the cost of removing one random card from their hand first.

## Usage notes

- Incantation cannot add Stone Cards (<br>), similar to Grim (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Aces|]] to your hand Available from start. ) and Familiar (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ).

- Incantation only gives cards with enhancements, not editions or seals.

- Incantation cannot be used when there is only 1 card in hand, which can happen if the player's hand size is one or the player has destroyed cards until there is only 1 card left.

## Synergies

- Hologram (This Joker gains every time a is added to your deck <br> }} ) synergizes well, as using Incantation gives a free X1 Mult.

- Vampire (This Joker gains per scoring played, removes card <br> }} ) has a decent synergy, as it adds enhanced Numbered cards to scale up this card.

- Using Incantation adds enhanced cards to make progress towards Driver's License ( if you have at least Enhanced cards in your full deck <br> }} Enhance 16 cards in your deck Rare Multiplicative Mult Independent Driver')'s condition of 16 enhanced cards.

- Jokers that care about Numbered rank synergize with Incantation if it gives their respective rank. In particular:

 - Odd Todd (Played cards with [[Card Ranks|]] rank give when scored <br> ) gives +31 Chips for every scored 3, 5, 7, and 9.

 - Even Steven (Played cards with [[Card Ranks|]] rank give when scored <br> ) gives +4 Mult for every scored 2, 4, 6, 8, and 10.

 - Hack (Retrigger each played , , , ) retriggers all scored 2s, 3s, 4s, and 5s.

## Anti-synergies

### Jokers

- Erosion ( for each card below in your full deck <br> }} ): Incantation adds more cards to the deck than it destroys, reducing possible mult from Erosion.

- Any Joker reliant on a non-Numbered rank such as Baron (), Hit the Road (This Joker gains for every discarded this round<br> }} Discard at the same time. ), and Scholar () has less value with more Numbered cards added to the deck.

## Trivia

- An "incantation" is a magical spell or charm that is said to give special properties and mystical effects to an individual or an inanimate object.

- Incantation is part of a trio of spectral cards with very similar effects. The other cards are Grim (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Aces|]] to your hand Available from start. ) and Familiar (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ). These three cards share the effect of destroying one card and adding enhanced cards of specific ranks (Numbered cards for Incantation, Face cards for Familiar, and Aces for Grim).

- Incantation appears in the artwork for the Jumbo Spectral Pack.
