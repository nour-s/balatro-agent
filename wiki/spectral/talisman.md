# Talisman

Talisman is a Spectral card that allows the player to set the seal on 1 selected card in hand to a Gold Seal ().

## Usage notes

- Talisman can overwrite an existing seal on a playing card.

## Strategy

- It is recommended to put Gold Seals on cards intended to be played often, and cards that can naturally be retriggered by certain Jokers, like face cards for Sock and Buskin () or 2s, 3s, 4s, 5s for Hack (Retrigger each played , , , ).

- It is always a good idea to play a Gold Seal card, even if it doesn't contribute much to the score, if the player has spare hands. Losing $1 for playing a hand and gaining at least $3 back is almost always a good trade-off.

## Synergies

### Jokers

- Hanging Chad () can retrigger a Gold Seal 2 additional times, providing an extra $6.

- Sock and Buskin () gives an extra $3 per played face card with a Gold Seal.

- Hack (Retrigger each played , , , ) is similar to Sock and Buskin, but applies to 2s, 3s, 4s, and 5s instead.

- Dusk () gives extra money from all scored Gold Seals on the last hand of the round.

- Burglar (When [[Blind|]] is selected, ) allows for more Gold Seals to be scored in a round.

- Economy-based scalers, such as Bull ( for each you have <br> }} ), Bootstraps ( for every you have <br> }} Have at least Jokers at the same time. ), and Flash Card (This Joker gains per in [[The Shop|the shop]] <br> }} ), appreciate the extra money brought in by Gold Seals.

### Vouchers

- Grabber () and Nacho Tong (Permanently gain an additional hand per round Play a total of 2,) provide more opportunities to score Gold Seals.

### Enhancements

- Stone Cards (<br>) with a Gold Seal can be added to any less than 5 card hand to easily earn $3.

- Lucky Cards ( chance for <br> ) with a Gold Seal will give $4.3 on average when played.

## Anti-Synergies

- Vagabond (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] ) is harder to use as scoring a Gold Seal can bring the player over the $4 barrier, preventing the player from getting a Tarot card on the next hand.

## Trivia

- A "Talisman" is an ancient artifact that is said to bring magic powers, good luck, or riches to a person. This is evident in its ability to apply a Gold Seal to a card which gives $3 to the player once the card is scored.
