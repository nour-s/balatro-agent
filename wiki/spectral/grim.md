# Grim

Aces to your hand

Grim is a Spectral card that destroys one random card in hand, then adds 2 random enhanced Aces to hand.

- Grim cannot add Stone Cards (<br>), similar to Incantation (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card_Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Number cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ) and Familiar (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ).

- Abandoned Deck ( Start run with no [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] in your deck Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ): As the Abandoned deck starts with fewer cards, the added Aces will be drawn more often.

- Erratic Deck ( All [[Card Ranks|]] and [[Suits|]] in deck are randomized Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ): If the starting deck contains a larger number of Aces, Grim can further increase this.

## Trivia

- The origin behind the name 'grim' likely originates from 'grimoire' rather than the modern term 'grim', meaning somber or bleak. A grimoire is a book of magic or alchemy, usually used for containing spells or summoning, which may have influenced Grim's effect of creating cards.

- Grim is part of a trio of spectral cards with very similar effects. The other cards are Incantation (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card_Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Number cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ) and Familiar (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ). These three cards share the effect of destroying one card and adding enhanced cards of specific ranks (Numbered cards for Incantation, Face cards for Familiar, and Aces for Grim).

 - The sigil on the grimoire's cover is similar to an ace of spades, further tying into the card's effect.
