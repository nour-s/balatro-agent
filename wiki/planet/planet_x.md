# Planet X

Mult and +35 Chips

Planet X is a Planet card that increases the value of Five of a Kind by +3 Mult and +35 Chips (default 12 Mult x 120 Chips).

It can only appear during a run after playing at least one Five of a Kind.

## Trivia

- Planet X is labeled "Planet?" in the card description.

- Planet X is primarily a reference to a Louis Cole song of the same name.1] It is also a hypothetical planet in our solar system according to NASA.

 - Being a non-existent planet, it is the only card that does not have its location in the solar system displayed on the card.

 - Due to being a non-existent planet, Planet X is symbolic towards Five of a Kind being a non-existent hand type in traditional poker.
