# Neptune

Mult and +40 Chips

Neptune is a Planet card that increases the value of Straight Flush (and Royal Flush) by +4 Mult and +40 Chips (default 8 Mult x 100 Chips).

## Trivia

- In reality, Neptune was recently confirmed to not actually be a dark blue color like the card suggests, as it was mistaken due to images taken from the satellite project of Voyager 2. Neptune's true color is similar to Uranus, having a lighter blueish-green tint.1]
