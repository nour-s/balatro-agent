# Mercury

Mult and +15 Chips

Mercury is a Planet card that increases the value of Pair by +1 Mult and +15 Chips (default 2 Mult x 10 Chips).

## Trivia

- There is a 1-pixel gap in the upper left corner of the art of Mercury that does not appear on other planet cards. This is presumably a graphical bug.

- Mercury is one of the four Planet Cards that appears in the artwork for one of the normal Celestial Packs. The other ones are Jupiter (Increases hand value by and Available from start. ), Earth (Increases hand value by and Available from start. ) and Uranus (Increases hand value by and Available from start. ).
