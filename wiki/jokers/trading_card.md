# Trading Card

Copyable Perishable Eternal

Trading Card is an Uncommon Economy Joker that gives $3 from discarding a single card on their very first discard each round, once per Blind. The discarded card will be destroyed, allowing the player to thin their deck and find their desired cards more easily.

## Usage Notes

- If the player holds multiple copies of Trading Card, they still only apply to the first discard, and each gives $3.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Red Deck ( ) gives +1 Discard, allowing Trading Card to be used with fewer consequences.

### Poker Hands

- In the early game, Trading Card tends to work best with hands that are more likely to be drawn, since it requires "wasting" a discard.

- However, Trading Card is one of the strongest enablers for more difficult hands by the mid/late game, as removing a third to a half of the deck makes certain difficult hand types significantly easier to assemble.

### Blinds

- The Hook ( )'s discard can activate Trading Card after playing a hand if there is only one card held in hand. Because this discard does not count toward the player's discards, Trading Card will activate every time players play a hand that leaves only one card held in hand, making this the only time Trading Card can be used multiple times in a blind.

- This Joker is very easy to locate when playing against Amber Acorn ( ), as it still shakes even when flipped face-down.

## Trivia

- The effect of Trading Card references the practice of selling single trading cards for money. In this case, "selling" (discarding) a single card for $3.

 - However, it's still considered as destroying rather than selling in game, thus it has no interaction with Campfire (This Joker gains for each card , resets when [[Blinds|]] is defeated <br> }} ).

- The design of this Joker mimics the card layout typical of Trading Card Games, including design elements such as numerical stats, a text box, and a collector number.
