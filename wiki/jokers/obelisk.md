# Obelisk

hand played without playing your most played poker hand
 (Currently X1 Mult) Copyable Perishable Eternal

Obelisk is a Rare scaling Multiplicative Mult Joker that provides an additional X0.2 Mult each time a hand that is not one of the player's most-played hands is played. This Joker starts at X1 Mult and will reset to X1 Mult if the most-played hand is played. The Obelisk resets before the hand is scored.

You can safely play any hand type until it is tied to your most-played hand type. Obelisk will only reset once you break the tie between those hands (i.e., you play one of those hands one more time).

## Usage Notes

- The Monolith ( Eternal <br>Eternal Negative ) Challenge Deck starts the player with an Eternal () copy of Obelisk.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Red Deck ( )'s extra discards, Painted Deck ( hand size, <br> Joker slot Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] )'s higher hand size, and Abandoned Deck ( Start run with no [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] in your deck Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] )'s smaller deck size all allow the player to find more unique hands, which helps with keeping the XMult on Obelisk without risking playing the most played hand. Painted Deck also appreciates having such a high-value XMult Joker.

- Blue Deck ( hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] )'s extra hands allow Obelisk to scale faster per round.

- Checkered Deck ( ) makes Flush easier to play, as the deck only has Spades and Hearts. Using this deck allows another option for the most played hand type other than Pair and Two Pair to build around and then pivot out of. It also helps the player after pivoting if the build does not revolve around Flush beforehand, as having another hand type that's easy to hit reduces the chance of resetting Obelisk accidentally.

### Poker Hands

No particular poker hand is better with Obelisk. The Pair or Two Pair strategy mentioned below could be considered a synergy, but the bulk of the game will be spent playing as many different hands as possible to keep options open in the future.

### Jokers

- Jokers that produce Mult without hand-based conditions are what you always want to use with Obelisk. These can be flat + Mult Jokers, like Fibonacci (Each played , , , , ) and Half Joker ( ), but the most success comes from scaling ones, like Bootstraps ( for every you have <br> }} Have at least Jokers at the same time. ), Erosion ( for each card below in your full deck <br> }} ), Ride the Bus (This Joker gains per hand played without a scoring card <br> }} ), or Fortune Teller ( per [[Tarot Cards|]] card used this run <br> }} ). Having one of these scaling Jokers will usually allow you to play all your hands per round, since you can place Obelisk before them for all but the last hand, and the last hand will have a large amount of extra Mult.

- Spare Trousers (This Joker gains if played hand contains a [[Poker Hands|]] <br> }} ) will often naturally set up a stack of Two Pairs, making a smooth transition to other hands.

- Jokers that provide scaling + Chips like Square Joker (This Joker gains if played hand has exactly cards <br> }} ) or Bull ( for each you have <br> }} ), are very important to play Obelisk effectively, as few Chips will be provided from Planet Cards.

- Vagabond (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] ) and other Jokers that benefit from many hands being played per round work well with Obelisk, as you already want to play many hands per round to scale Obelisk. All of these kinds of Jokers in turn benefit from Acrobat ( ), as it provides a safety net for the last hand of the round.

### Boss Blinds

- When a poker hand is not allowed to be played by The Psychic ( ), The Eye ( ), or The Mouth ( ), Obelisk will not reset or gain Mult. However, the hand counter in the Run Info will increase by 1. This theoretically allows a method of always increasing Obelisk.

- Alternately, when Obelisk gets disabled by Crimson Heart ( ), the most played hands can be safely played without resetting Obelisk.

### Vouchers

- Grabber () and Nacho Tong (Permanently gain an additional hand per round Play a total of 2,) give additional hands per round, allowing Obelisk to scale even higher.

- Wasteful () and Recyclomancy () give additional discards, which allows the player to find more unique hands, which helps with keeping the XMult on Obelisk without risking playing the most played hand.

- Paint Brush ( ) and Palette ( hand size Reduce your hand size down to five cards. ) both increase hand size, which can help players to find and form more unique hands to keep the XMult on Obelisk without risking playing the most played hand.

- Black Deck ( Joker slot <br> hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ), Blast Off ( hands per round<br> discards per round<br> Joker Slots Eternal <br>Eternal <br><br>Banned [[Vouchers]]: , <br>Banned Jokers: ), and Golden Needle ( Discards each cost <br><br> hands per round<br> discards per round<br>Start with Banned Jokers: <br>Banned [[Vouchers]]: , ) all have poor synergy with Obelisk due to their reduced number of hands.

- Nebula Deck ( Start run with the voucher <br> consumable slot Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) has slight anti-synergy with Obelisk, as Telescope ([[Booster Packs|]] always contain the [[Planet Cards|]] card for your most played [[Poker Hands|]] ) encourages building around a single most-played hand type, which Obelisk discourages.

### Jokers

- Burglar (When [[Blind|]] is selected, ) removes all discards and may force the most played hand to be played, resetting Obelisk.

- All Jokers that rely on playing specific hands, including Superposition (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card if poker hand contains an and a <br> ), Crazy Joker ( ), and The Order ( if played hand contains a Win a run without playing a . ), will not be able to be used as much.

- Planet Cards and Jokers that upgrade hands, like Space Joker ( chance to upgrade level of played [[Poker Hands|]] ) and Burnt Joker (Upgrade the level of the first poker hand each round Sell 50 cards. ), are far less effective because the hand can only be used a limited number of times.

### Vouchers

- Telescope ([[Booster Packs|]] always contain the [[Planet Cards|]] card for your most played [[Poker Hands|]] ) encourages focusing on a single poker hand, which is the opposite of what Obelisk needs to scale.

## Strategies

Obelisk can become a very powerful Scaling XMult Joker, but it cannot be used in every build. It works best on builds that don't rely on playing specific hands to score, as if too many of any one hand are played, you can no longer play it without resetting the Obelisk.

The most effective strategy with Obelisk is to play Pair or Two Pair until around 15 have been played, then pivoting in the later antes to playing any other hand. It is not recommended to use Obelisk if the most played hand is High Card, as the player runs a higher risk of being forced to reset the Obelisk.

Old design of Obelisk

- During the second demo, Obelisk scaled at X0.5 Mult per played hand, making it as strong as Madness (When or is selected, gain and a random Joker <br> }} ). It was later nerfed twice, resulting in only X0.2 Mult per played hand, the second weakest among scaling Multiplicative Jokers per scaling trigger (only after Constellation (This Joker gains every time a [[Planet_Cards|]] card is used <br> }} ), at X0.1 Mult per Planet card used).
