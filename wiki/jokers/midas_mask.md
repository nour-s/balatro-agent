# Midas Mask

Copyable Perishable Eternal
Midas Mask is an Uncommon Effect Joker that enhances scoring Face Cards into Gold Cards ( ). It will overwrite other Enhancements.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Mad World ( Extra no longer earn money<br>Earn no at end of round Eternal, Negative <br>Eternal Ranks 2 through 9 only, 32 cards totalBanned Blinds: ) starts with an Eternal Pareidolia (All cards are considered cards Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ), which makes it so that all cards are upgraded to Gold Cards when scored.

### Poker Hands

- Any five-card hand with five Face Cards will benefit Midas Mask the most. This includes Flush, Full House, Five of a Kind, Flush House, and Flush Five.

- Since it is slightly more difficult to make a Straight or Straight Flush with face cards than other cards, Midas Mask is slightly less useful if you have invested in those hands.

- Late game, playing small hands like High Card means you can keep more Gold Cards in hand.

### Jokers

- Mime () means the Gold Cards make twice as much money.

- Midas Mask naturally synergizes with Smiley Face (), Scary Face (), and Business Card () when playing the face cards. However, you're not going to want to play your golden face cards later in the game.

- It has an extremely strong synergy with Pareidolia (All cards are considered cards Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ), which makes it so that all cards are upgraded to Gold Cards when scored.

 - Beware of using Stone Cards (<br>) with this strategy, as they will also be turned into regular Gold Cards, which may ruin a run based on Stone Cards.

- Golden Ticket (Played [[Gold Cards|]] ) makes Gold Cards give +$4 when played.

- Vampire (This Joker gains per scoring played, removes card <br> }} ) removes card enhancements in exchange for X0.1 Mult per enhancement removed, making it a potent combination with Midas Mask as it creates a steady stream of XMult scaling.

 - Where Midas Mask is placed relative to the Vampire can affect this. Assuming a five-card hand of only face cards is played, when placed to the left of Vampire, it guarantees that Vampire will gain X0.5 Mult, as it will apply the Gold Card effect, then Vampire's enhancement removal effect. However, when Midas Mask is placed to the right, Vampire's enhancement removal applies before Midas Mask, guaranteeing that all face cards are Gold Cards when reshuffled into the deck. This can be helpful to prepare additional face cards for future Vampire scaling.

- Midas Mask is inherently anti-synergistic with Ride the Bus (This Joker gains per hand played without a scoring card <br> }} ) and Reserved Parking (), however, if the player is able to convert most of the face cards in the deck with Midas Mask before acquiring these Jokers, then they may be able to benefit substantially.

- Finally, Driver's License ( if you have at least Enhanced cards in your full deck <br> }} Enhance 16 cards in your deck Rare Multiplicative Mult Independent Driver') becomes a nearly free X3 Mult when all face cards are gold.

## Anti-Synergies

### Decks

- This Joker is close to useless on the Abandoned Deck ( Start run with no [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] in your deck Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) and Medusa ( Eternal 52 cards, with all face cards replaced by Stone cards) since it has no face cards.

### Blinds

- The Plant ( ) debuffs all face cards, preventing Midas Mask from making any gold cards for the round.

- The Pillar ( ) may be problematic, as played face cards of the Ante will be debuffed upon entering the Boss Blind.

## Trivia

- Midas Mask's name and ability are a reference to King Midas, a king of Greek myth who was cursed to turn anything he touched to gold (often referred to as the "Midas touch"). The effect of turning only face cards gold reflects how it's a mask.
