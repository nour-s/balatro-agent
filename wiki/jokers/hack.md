# Hack

Copyable Perishable Eternal

Hack is an Uncommon Retrigger Joker that can retrigger played scoring low-rank cards, more specifically, 2s, 3s, 4s, and 5s. This can mitigate the lower chip count provided by low-rank cards, since they will be triggered twice.

Hack is similar to Sock and Buskin (), but works with specific numbered cards instead of faces. Similar to Sock and Buskin, it also does not retrigger a Red Seal (). For example, a Red Seal 3 will only activate three times: once for the initial trigger, once for the Red Seal, and then lastly, for Hack.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Abandoned Deck ( Start run with no [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] in your deck Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) and Mad World ( Extra no longer earn money<br>Earn no at end of round Eternal, Negative <br>Eternal Ranks 2 through 9 only, 32 cards totalBanned Blinds: ) only have number cards, making it much easier to find low-rank cards.

- Erratic Deck ( All [[Card Ranks|]] and [[Suits|]] in deck are randomized Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) can potentially have a high amount of low-rank cards that Hack can affect.

- Zodiac Deck ( Start run with , , and Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) can be used to find more Tarot cards to enhance low-rank cards or ways to copy them.

- Ghost Deck ( [[Spectral Cards|]] cards may appear in the shop, start with a card Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) can find powerful seal-applying Spectral cards and Aura (Add [[Foil|]], [[Holographic|]], or [[Polychrome|]] effect to selected card in hand Available from start. ), which can make your low-rank cards more impactful.

### Poker Hands

Using large hands that have multiple low-rank cards that can trigger Hack is generally the best option, allowing the player to maximize its potential. Notably, Five of a Kind and Flush Five are easily feasible after duplicating powerful and/or modified low-rank cards. This also includes Full House and Flush House, although they are slightly weaker than the initially mentioned hands. A Straight using Ace, 2, 3, 4, and 5 is also a good way to utilize Hack, although it will not retrigger the Ace.

### Jokers

- Jokers that are able to give bonuses based on the type of playing card, whether it is suit-based or rank-based, can benefit from Hack. This allows the player to double that Joker's effectiveness.

 - Suit-based Jokers like Greedy Joker (), Arrowhead (), Gluttonous Joker (), etc. can be retriggered if a low-rank card of the corresponding suit is played.

 - Rank-based Jokers like Even Steven (Played cards with [[Card Ranks|]] rank give when scored <br> ), Walkie Talkie (), or Odd Todd (Played cards with [[Card Ranks|]] rank give when scored <br> ) can benefit from a second trigger.

 - Fibonacci (Each played , , , , ) is particularly strong with Hack, as it gains +8 Mult for each scored 2, 3, and 5, and will trigger twice.

 - Wee Joker (This Joker gains when each played is scored <br> }} ) can scale twice as fast utilizing the retriggered 2s.

- Tarot generating Jokers, like Cartomancer (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when [[Blinds|]] is selected <br> Discover every Tarot Card. ) and Vagabond (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] ), are needed for deck manipulation to draw low-rank cards more reliably. It will also help modify low-rank cards to make them more valuable during the retrigger (ex. retriggering a Bonus ( ) 4).

- Jokers that give XMult from cards are very useful, as the cards that trigger them will do so twice. There aren't as many that work with low-rank cards, but some examples are Bloodstone ( ) and Ancient Joker (Each played card with [[Card Suits|]] gives when scored, <br> <small>suit changes at end of round</small> ).

- If one has enough XMult from their cards, another retrigger Joker is very useful to combine with Hack. This will usually be either Dusk () or Seltzer (), though Blueprint (Copies ability of [[Jokers|]] ) and Brainstorm (Copies the ability of leftmost [[Jokers|]] Discard a . ) can also be used to copy Hack for a less conditional retrigger. One can also use Showman ([[Jokers|]], [[Tarot Cards|]], [[Planet Cards|]], and [[Spectral Cards|]] cards may appear multiple times Reach Ante level Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) to try and find another Hack.

- Trading Card (If of round has only card, ) and DNA (If of round has only card, ) make it easier to draw your useful low-rank cards. DNA is particularly useful, as it can copy a powerful low-rank card with an enhancement, edition, and seal.

- Hiker () has incredible synergy with Hack, as not only will Hiker scale cards twice as fast, but the retriggering of the cards will also give their chips again.

- Lucky Cat (This Joker gains every time a [[Lucky Cards|]] card triggers <br> }} ) can scale twice as fast since the low-rank Lucky Cards ( chance for <br> ) it will be using to scale have twice as many chances to earn +20 Mult or $20.

- As Glass Cards (<br> ) are substantially powerful with Hack's retriggers, Glass Joker (This Joker gains for every [[Glass_Cards|]] that is destroyed <br> }} Have at least [[Glass_Cards|]] ) can be a nice addition to still get value when they break.

However, if it adds too many high-rank cards, Hack will become less valuable, so the risk and reward must be carefully assessed.

## Anti-synergies

### Decks

- 15 Minute City ( Eternal <br>Eternal Two copies of every face card;<br>no Aces, 2s, or 3s) doesn't work well with Hack due to a lack of 2s and 3s by default.

## Trivia

- A "hack" is a slang term to refer to both a mediocre comedian and/or re-used, obvious, or stolen jokes from other comedians (explaining the presence of the comedian in the card's artwork). This repetition of low-quality jokes is reflected in the retriggering of low-rank cards.

- According to LocalThunk, the outfit of this joker is loosely based on Joe Rogan.1]

- It is one of the five Jokers to have a physical card from Balatro: Special Edition. The four other jokers where that is also the case are Joker ( ), Ice Cream (<br> ), Abstract Joker ( for each [[Jokers|]] card<br>}} ), and Throwback ( for each [[Blinds|]] skipped this run <br> }} ).

- Hack is one of the two Jokers to appear on the front of a regular-sized Buffoon Pack. The other Joker is Juggler ( hand size Common Effect N/A (Passive) ).

- According to LocalThunk, the joke that Hack told is as follows:2]

I'll try ANYTHING in bed once!
