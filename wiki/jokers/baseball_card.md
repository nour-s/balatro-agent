# Baseball Card

Copyable Perishable Eternal

Baseball Card is a Rare Multiplicative Mult Joker that provides X1.5 Mult for each Uncommon Joker held by the player. The effect is multiplicative. The more Uncommon Jokers that the player holds, the more times Baseball Card will be triggered.

Importantly, Baseball Card does not trigger from a "minimum" rarity requirement of Uncommon; it only triggers specifically from Uncommon Jokers. Rare or Legendary Jokers do not grant the multiplier.

Baseball Card's effect triggers after the Uncommon Joker's effect. Therefore, placing +Mult Jokers left of Uncommon Jokers is best to maximize score.

### Jokers

Baseball Card is a bit of an odd Joker, since its effect is triggered by only Uncommon Jokers. This effect happens after each Uncommon Joker is activated. Any Uncommon Joker is inherently synergistic with Baseball Card (aside from Madness (When or is selected, gain and a random Joker <br> }} )), and having four Uncommon Jokers totals to X5.0625 Mult, a rather large amount, but this restricts you to only using Uncommon Jokers or having more Joker slots. This bonus grows exponentially with more Joker slots, making Baseball Card one of only two Jokers that scale with total Jokers, the other being Abstract Joker ( for each [[Jokers|]] card<br>}} ).

### Boss Blinds

- When playing against Crimson Heart ( ), Baseball Card will still trigger on debuffed Uncommon Jokers, as long as Baseball Card is not debuffed itself.

### Stakes

- On Orange Stake and higher, Uncommon Perishable () Jokers that have been debuffed will still be granted XMult by Baseball Card, making it potentially worthwhile to hold onto a Joker even after it perishes.

## Anti-Synergies

### Decks

- Painted Deck ( hand size, <br> Joker slot Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) is difficult to use with Baseball Card due to its -1 Joker slot, which allows fewer Uncommon Jokers to be held at once.

- Similarly, Cruelty ( give no reward money<br> give no reward money<br><br> Joker Slots ) is even more difficult to use with Baseball Card due to the -2 Joker slots, which allows even fewer Uncommon Jokers to be held at once.

## Trivia

- Baseball cards are a type of collectable trading card, with uncommon baseball cards being worth more than common cards. This Joker plays on this theme, with uncommon Jokers granting a bonus.

- The signature at the bottom of the card reads "Jim Joker".
