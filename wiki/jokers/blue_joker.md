# Blue Joker

(Currently +104 Chips) Copyable Perishable Eternal

Blue Joker is a Common Chips Joker that adds 2 Chips for every card still in the deck — cards that have not been played/discarded nor are currently held in hand. Its value starts high and decreases as the round continues and cards are drawn.

## Strategies

While Blue Joker does get stronger the more cards are in the deck and weaker the fewer cards are in the deck, this might not be the best way to think about Blue Joker. Instead, it is best to think about Blue Joker as if it gave +70 Chips on average, as it often gives this amount of chips within a normal deck. From that perspective, Blue Joker is one of the strongest and most reliable chip jokers at common, especially in the early game.
