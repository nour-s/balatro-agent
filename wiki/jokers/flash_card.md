# Flash Card

(Currently +0 Mult) Copyable Perishable Eternal

Flash Card is an Uncommon scaling Additive Mult Joker that permanently gains +2 Mult every time the player reroll in the shop. The increase is not retroactive and will only start scaling after purchase, meaning it is always +0 Mult initially.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Decks that generate more money than usual are stronger with Flash Card, mainly Yellow Deck ( Start with extra Discover at least items from your [[Collection|collection]] ) and Green Deck ( At end of each Round: <br> per remaining <br> per remaining <br> Earn no Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ). Green Deck in particular is good with it early, as one can spend as much money on rerolls as they want without worrying about losing interest.

- Zodiac Deck ( Start run with , , and Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) may work well with Flash Card, as the player may want to reroll frequently to see more Tarot and Planet cards.

### Poker Hands

No poker hands inherently work better than others with Flash Card. All potential benefits come from other synergies.

### Jokers

- Chaos the Clown ( free per shop Common Effect N/A (Passive) ) grants 1 free reroll per shop, effectively giving Flash Card +2 Mult gain per shop visit on top of paid rerolls.

- Economy Jokers are critical to Flash Card's success. Being able to get more money translates directly into more Mult. Particularly helpful are scaling Economy Jokers, like Cloud 9 (Earn for each in your at end of round <br> }} Uncommon Economy N/A (Passive) ) and Rocket (Earn at end of round. - Campfire (This Joker gains for each card , resets when [[Blinds|]] is defeated <br> }} ) is very good with Flash Card, as the large amount of rerolls being performed and strong economy allows many cards to be sold, scaling Campfire very high.

- Buying and selling planets from Astronomer (All cards and in the shop are Discover every card Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) found with rerolls will allow you to recuperate some of the money spent from rerolling.

- Other Jokers that give XMult are helpful, as they allow Flash Card's scaling to be more effective. Particularly, Constellation (This Joker gains every time a [[Planet_Cards|]] card is used <br> }} ) can scale very quickly, as every reroll can create a planet card.

## Anti-Synergies

### Decks

- Black Deck ( Joker slot <br> hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ) starts with -1 Hand per round, making Economy more difficult to set up early game. This directly limits Flash Card's scaling opportunities as less Hands per round earns the player less Money overall when defeating Blinds.

### Jokers

- Bull ( for each you have <br> }} ) and Bootstraps ( for every you have <br> }} Have at least Jokers at the same time. ) respectively gain Chips and Mult relative to the player's current Money. Spending Money on rerolls to scale Flash Card will therefore weaken the scoring power of these Jokers.

### Blinds

- The Ox ( ) sets the player's Money to 0 upon playing the most played Poker Hand of the run, making it potentially difficult to recover Economy for Flash Card scaling.

## Trivia

Old design of Flash Card

- Flash cards, or flashcards, are cards normally used as memory aids for learning new topics, often by repeatedly cycling through them to be effective, similar to how rerolling improves this card's effect.

- In early demo versions, the color of Flash Card was blue. This is changed later to match the color of the reroll button in shop.
