# Shortcut

(ex: 10 8 6 5 3) Copyable Perishable Eternal

Shortcut is an Uncommon Effect-type Joker that allows Straights to be made with one or more gaps of 1 rank between cards (ex: 2 4 5 7 9). Like normal, Shortcut still does not allow "wrapping" Straights, so Aces remain either high or low and not both.

## Synergies

- Black Deck ( Joker slot <br> hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ): The +1 Joker Slot allows Shortcut to function as a utility Joker while still providing space for 5 other Jokers to score.

- Abandoned Deck ( Start run with no [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] in your deck Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) lowers the range of card values from A-K to A-10, making it significantly more likely to draw a Straight, especially with Shortcut.

## Strategies

Mathematically, Shortcut is slightly better than Four Fingers (All and can be made with 4 cards Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) in creating Straights.1] However, Four Fingers is better at helping create Flushes and Straight Flushes. The player can consider choosing between the two, or combining the two to gain more Straights (in which case, Straights are almost always present in the 8 cards), but at the risk of losing two scoring Jokers.

## Trivia

- A shortcut is a quicker route from one place to another than the usual path, often taken by skipping across roads through side paths. Shortcut's effect is inspired by this; it allows players to 'skip' a number while still preserving the Straight.
