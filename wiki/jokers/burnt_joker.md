# Burnt Joker

Copyable Perishable Eternal

Burnt Joker is a Rare Effect Joker that upgrades the level of the first discarded Poker Hand each round.

## Usage notes

- Burnt Joker can be triggered as normal by debuffed cards.

- The Hook ( )'s forced discards will not activate Burnt Joker.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Red Deck ( ) - The +1 discard makes the Burnt Joker have less of a downside.

- Checkered Deck ( ) - Only having two suits makes it significantly more likely to have a Flush in hand at all times.

### Poker Hands

Simple hands such as Pairs or High Cards are more likely to naturally occur, thus making them easier to discard and level up.

### Jokers

- Blueprint (Copies ability of [[Jokers|]] ) or Brainstorm (Copies the ability of leftmost [[Jokers|]] Discard a . ) - Synergizes incredibly well, as after the desired hand is discarded it can be moved to copy a different Joker before a hand is played.

- Drunkard ( discard each round Common Effect N/A (Passive) ) and Merry Andy ( discards each round, <br> hand size Win a run in or fewer rounds Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) increase the number of Discards, making the first Discard for Burnt Joker inconsequential.

- Jokers that make a particular hand easier to produce, such as Four Fingers (All and can be made with 4 cards Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ), Shortcut (Allows to be made with gaps of <br> )}} Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ), and Smeared Joker ( and count as the same suit, and count as the same suit Have at least [[Wild Cards|]] in your deck Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ), can make it easier to discard their related hands, especially if you then want to be able to play the same hand immediately afterwards; Four Fingers and Shortcut also allow you to hit the relevant hand when discarding fewer cards, for situations when there are cards in your hand you want to keep.

### Vouchers

- Wasteful () and Recyclomancy () both increase discards, making the first discards for Burnt Joker inconsequential.

### Poker Hands

Five-card hands that are hard to draw naturally like Straight, Full House, Straight Flush can make it hard to benefit from Burnt Joker.

### Jokers

- Jokers that discourage Discarding such as Green Joker ( per hand played <br> per discard <br> }} ), Delayed Gratification (Earn per if no discards are used by end of the round Common Economy N/A (Passive) ) or Ramen (, ) can make it harder to benefit from Burnt Joker.

- Burglar (When [[Blind|]] is selected, ) removes all Discards, nullifying Burnt Joker.

- May or may not have anti-synergy with Trading Card (If of round has only card, ) since this Joker requires discarding a single card to benefit and thus can only level-up High Card.

### Stakes

- Blue Stake and above removes a discard, making Burnt Joker more committal to utilize.

### Blinds

- The Water ( ) will always set your discards to 0.

## Trivia

- This Joker might be a reference to a burn card, a playing card dealt from the top of a deck and discarded ("burned"), unused by the players, which is performed in card games (for example, Texas hold 'em) to prevent cheating.

- In early demo versions, the effect of Burnt Joker was "gains +1 hand size hand size per 4 tarot/planet cards sold". This is still reflected by the unlock requirement being related to selling cards, and in the 1.0.1o version of the game, Burnt Joker still gives +0 hand size internally.1]2]

- Space Joker ( chance to upgrade level of played [[Poker Hands|]] ) is similar, but it may provide an upgrade for playing hands instead of discarding them.

 - Though unlike Space Joker, Burnt Joker is not related to space.
