# Brainstorm

Copyable Perishable Eternal

Brainstorm is a Rare Effect Joker that copies the effect of the player's leftmost Joker. Unlike Blueprint (Copies ability of [[Jokers|]] ), moving Brainstorm around will not change what it copies, only when it does so. To change the Joker that is being copied, a different Joker will need to be moved to the leftmost position. There are some Jokers that cannot be copied, and putting Brainstorm itself in the leftmost position will cause it to do nothing. If Blueprint is in the left-most position, Brainstorm will copy the same joker Blueprint is copying. Brainstorm's compatibility with other Jokers can be seen in its tooltip.

In general, Brainstorm will not copy the following effects:

- Anything that triggers at the end of a round (ex. Golden Joker (Earn at end of round Common Economy N/A (Passive) ))

- Debuffed Jokers

- Card Edition effects

- Sticker effects

- Card destruction (ex. Trading Card (If of round has only card, ))

- Joker scaling (only the final result is copied)

- Changes to hands, discards, hand size or hand typing (except for Burglar (When [[Blind|]] is selected, ), which triggers after the blind starts)

- Passive effects (like Chicot (Disables effect of every [[Blinds|]] Find this [[Jokers|Joker]] from the [[Spectral Cards|]] card. ) or Pareidolia (All cards are considered cards Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ))

See Incompatible Jokers for a full list.

## Usage Notes

It should be noted that if the player presses "Play Hand", and then quickly attempts to change the leftmost Joker while the hand is scoring, the Brainstorm will copy the effect of the Joker that was on the leftmost side of before the hand is played. For example, if a player has three Jokers, positioned in this way before playing this hand: Lusty Joker (), Smiley Face (), and Brainstorm (), then attempting to move the Smiley Face to the leftmost position immediately after the "Play Hand" button is pressed, the Brainstorm will copy the effect of Lusty Joker instead of Smiley Face.

However, it is also worth noting that the Joker order reapplies once the round is complete. For example, if the player moves Mime () to the leftmost position while the player's winning hand is scoring, then the player will receive the benefits of multiple copies of Mime during the end-of-round effects (such as Gold Cards ( ) or Blue Seal (Creates the card for final played [[Poker Hands|]] of round if in hand<br> )). Players should move all of their copies of Blueprint (Copies ability of [[Jokers|]] ) and Brainstorm () onto Mime while their final hand is scoring for maximum Gold Card or Blue Seal value.

Unlocking Brainstorm can be done easily on either Red Deck ( ), Checkered Deck ( ), or Painted Deck ( hand size, <br> Joker slot Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) by simply starting a run below Blue Stake and intentionally expending Hands as Discards before drawing a Royal Flush to discard.

## Synergies

### Decks

By its nature, any deck can make effective use of Brainstorm. Its effect is highly flexible and can benefit nearly any archetype of deck, aside from Madness (When or is selected, gain and a random Joker <br> }} ) without Eternal () Stickers.

### Poker Hands

Any hand can benefit from Brainstorm in the usual ways. The only hand-specific Jokers that are incompatible with Brainstorm are independent effects such as Shortcut (Allows to be made with gaps of <br> )}} Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ).

### Jokers

Due to the difference in the way it copies a card, and the nature of Jokers triggering left to right, Brainstorm will always be in Blueprint's shadow — by a small amount. Brainstorm's disadvantage is that xMult Jokers (such as Cavendish ( <br> )) must be placed all the way to the left to be copied by Brainstorm, meaning the player cannot multiply the Mult from +Mult Jokers such as Supernova (Adds the number of times [[Poker Hands|]] ) twice. For example, Supernova + Blueprint + The Trio will score more than The Trio + Supernova + Brainstorm. However, a Polychrome ( Edition Joker (Polychrome).png) Brainstorm can copy a +Mult Joker to its left while still allowing the full benefit of its edition effect. Some notable targets for copying include Jokers that make money mid-Blind such as Mail-In Rebate (Earn for each discarded [[Card Ranks|]], ), retrigger Jokers such as Dusk (), and xMult Jokers that trigger before most +Mult jokers anyways, such as Baron (), Photograph () or Bloodstone ( ). When multiple copies are combined with Baron and Mime, usually while using Plasma Deck ( Balance [[Chips|]] and [[Mult|]] when calculating score for played hand <br> base Blind size Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ), players can achieve the elusive Naneinf score.

When copying a Blueprint, Brainstorm will copy whichever effect Blueprint is copying, not the Blueprint itself.

While it will not work with them, Jokers such as Smeared Joker ( and count as the same suit, and count as the same suit Have at least [[Wild Cards|]] in your deck Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) and Four Fingers (All and can be made with 4 cards Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) can be used to unlock Brainstorm more easily.

See the Blueprint (Copies ability of [[Jokers|]] ) page for more general copying strategies.

### Blinds

 Amber Acorn ( ): This Showdown Blind flips and shuffles Jokers around, potentially causing Brainstorm to copy the incorrect Joker, or copy nothing at all if it happens to be moved to the leftmost position or the leftmost Joker is incompatible for at least one hand.

- However, this can be easily mitigated, by holding the Joker which the player wants to copy in hand, before Amber Acorn starts shuffling. This allows for the player to put the designated Joker in the leftmost position regardless of shuffling, ensuring that Brainstorm copies the right Joker.

## Trivia

- Certain visuals upon triggering a Joker effect, like the "Level Up!" from a copied Space Joker ( chance to upgrade level of played [[Poker Hands|]] ), have a red background instead of the standard orange one.

- At $10 each, Brainstorm and its sibling Blueprint (Copies ability of [[Jokers|]] ) are the most expensive Jokers able to be purchased since the Legendary Jokers are never purchasable.

- On the artwork, the leftmost bauble on the hat, the leftmost eye, and the leftmost bauble on the bottom are highlighted in some way, referencing how Brainstorm copies the leftmost Joker.

- The card art depicts a messy "back of the napkin" sketch, a rough calculation or idea quickly sketched out during a conversation at a bar or restaurant, which could be seen as the conception of the Joker being copied. This also reflects its limitations compared to the more versatile Blueprint (Copies ability of [[Jokers|]] ), which depicts a more deliberate and detailed technical drawing.

- This is the only Joker that has a 90-degree corner (top left) in its card art, as all other Jokers have a rounded radius in all their corners. According to LocalThunk, this represents the unfolded corner of the napkin.1]

- The texture of this Joker has protruding areas on the right and down sides, slightly exceeding the boundaries of a standard Joker. This may represent the ragged edge of the napkin after being torn off. The other Joker with this similar property is  Hallucination ( chance to create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when any [[Booster Packs|]] is opened <br> Common Effect N/A (Passive) ).

- Brainstorm appears on the artwork of the Mega Buffoon Pack.
