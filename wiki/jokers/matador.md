# Matador

ability Copyable Perishable Eternal

Matador is an Uncommon Economy Joker that provides a cash boost whenever you play a hand that triggers a Boss Blind's ability (such as attempting to score debuffed cards). This effect doesn't work with all Boss Blinds due to an oversight, though, and can occasionally give results inconsistent with what one might expect. As a general rule, any hand that contains a debuffed card, or will not score, will trigger Matador.

Blinds that can trigger Matador:

- Suit-debuffing Blinds:

 - The Window ( ) ( Diamonds)

 - The Head ( ) ( Hearts)

 - The Club ( ) ( Clubs)

 - The Goad ( ) ( Spades)

- The Plant ( ) (debuffed Face cards)

- The Pillar ( ) (debuffed previously-played cards)

- The Flint ( ) (halved base chips and mult)

- The Eye ( ), The Mouth ( ), or The Psychic ( ), if hand is a non-scoring hand (repeat hand type, non-repeat hand type, less than 5 cards)

- The Arm ( ), if played hand's level could be decreased (when it is level 2 or higher)

- The Ox ( ), if hand sets money to zero (the Ox sets the money to zero, then triggers Matador's effect, allowing you to keep the money earned, provided you don't trigger it again)

- Verdant Leaf ( ), if no Joker has been sold yet

No other blinds can trigger Matador's effect.

## Synergies

Matador gains the most value out of being used during applicable Boss Blinds the most amount of times. This generally means you want more hands, and Blind reroll abilities if the boss won't trigger Matador.

### Decks

- Blue Deck ( hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ) allows you to play more hands, allowing Matador to trigger more often.

- Anaglyph Deck ( After defeating each [[Blinds|]], gain a [[File:|24px]] [[Tags|]] Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) appreciates a way to make money that isn't hindered by needing to play Blinds instead of skipping them.

- Double or Nothing ( All cards become after scoring Standard 52-card deck, all with a Red Seal) will trigger Matador when attempting to score debuffed cards, even during Small and Big Blinds.

- As with many mid-round economy Jokers, Seed Money () and Money Tree () are very helpful, as they allow more interest to be gained off of that money.

## Trivia

- Matadors, or bullfighters, are the main performers with the bull in bullfighting. The lead matador is dressed in gold, like on the Joker card, with other matadors dressed in silver. Matadors often wave a red cloth to attract or anger the bull, which is represented in the card's background by the folds of the cloth. Similarly, this Joker rewards the player for provoking the Boss Blind's ability.

- In an interview with Bloomberg, Localthunk has confirmed that he will overhaul "the confusing Matador Joker" in the upcoming gameplay update.
