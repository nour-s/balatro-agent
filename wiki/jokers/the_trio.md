# The Trio

Copyable Perishable Eternal

The Trio is a Rare Multiplicative Mult Joker that grants X3 Mult any time you play a hand that contains a Three of a Kind.

It can be unlocked by winning a run without playing a Three of a Kind (though playing other hands that contain three cards of the same rank, such as Full House, are allowed for the unlock condition).

## Usage Notes

The Three of a Kind does not need to score for The Trio to activate. The only way to have this happen is when playing a Flush with Four Fingers (All and can be made with 4 cards Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) that also has a differently suited Three of a Kind (ex: ♠️10 ♠️10 ♦️10 ♠️4 ♠️3).

### Decks

- Abandoned Deck ( Start run with no [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] in your deck Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) and Mad World ( Extra no longer earn money<br>Earn no at end of round Eternal, Negative <br>Eternal Ranks 2 through 9 only, 32 cards totalBanned Blinds: ) work well with this Joker thanks to their smaller deck, allowing assembling a Three of a Kind easier.

- Erratic Deck ( All [[Card Ranks|]] and [[Suits|]] in deck are randomized Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) can work well with this Joker if it randomizes a lot of cards with the same rank.

### Poker Hands

This Joker can trigger on any played hand containing three cards of the same rank:

- Three of a Kind, Four of a Kind, Five of a Kind

- Full House, Flush House

- Flush Five

- Flush, when a Three of a Kind is present in the played hand

### Jokers

- Wily Joker ( ) gives +100 Chips when played hand contains a Three of a Kind.

- Zany Joker ( ) gives +12 Mult when played hand contains a Three of a Kind.

Playing a Three of a Kind or above will also trigger Jokers that trigger with Pair hands such as Jolly Joker ( ) and The Duo ( if played hand contains a Win a run without playing a . ).
