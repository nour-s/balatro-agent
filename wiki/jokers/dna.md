# DNA

Copyable Perishable Eternal

DNA is a Rare Effect Joker that adds a copy of a card to the deck (including all of its modifiers and additional chips from Hiker ()), given that it is the only card played on the first hand. After scoring, the original card is discarded, and the copy is drawn to hand. On its own, DNA provides minimal benefit and uses up a hand to copy a card. However, when synergized with card duplication strategies, DNA can power a machine that pushes scores way up.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Blue Deck ( hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ) gives an additional hand to play, which can be used to trigger DNA with fewer consequences.

- Red Deck ( ) gives an additional discard, which can help in finding a specific card for DNA to copy.

- Erratic Deck ( All [[Card Ranks|]] and [[Suits|]] in deck are randomized Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) can provide an easy source of Flush Houses or Flush Fives with the use of DNA copying if you play towards your highest amount of suits in deck.

### Poker Hands

DNA works best with High Card, since it requires a High Card on the first hand to trigger.

DNA also works especially well with strong hands that use duplicate cards, like Four of a Kind, Five of a Kind, Flush Five, or Flush House.

### Jokers

- Hologram (This Joker gains every time a is added to your deck <br> }} ) increases by X0.25 Mult for every card copied by DNA. As a result, DNA can increase Hologram by X0.75 Mult per ante, on top of whatever other cards can be added to your deck.

- Blue Joker ( for each remaining card in <br> }} ) adds Chips based on the number of cards in the deck, which can be increased by DNA.

- Blueprint (Copies ability of [[Jokers|]] ) and Brainstorm (Copies the ability of leftmost [[Jokers|]] Discard a . ) can be combined with DNA to duplicate a card twice (or thrice if all three Jokers are used together), allowing the player to quickly fill the deck with high value cards. Additionally, it allows the player to immediately use more copies of the card they played. With all three jokers, playing one card will put three copies in hand, for a net of two extra copies available to use.

- Trading Card (If of round has only card, ) pairs with DNA in deck manipulation, removing unwanted cards from the deck while DNA copies the necessary cards. It also increases the chances, through thinning the deck, of a specific card being drawn for DNA to copy.

- Drunkard ( discard each round Common Effect N/A (Passive) ) and Merry Andy ( discards each round, <br> hand size Win a run in or fewer rounds Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) both give extra discards, which benefit DNA. While Merry Andy ( discards each round, <br> hand size Win a run in or fewer rounds Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) reduces hand size, which normally makes desired cards harder to find, the extra discards will make that punishment pretty much meaningless, as long as the player does not reduce their hand size much lower.

- Anything that increases hand size will make it easier to find the desired card for DNA to copy. Juggler ( hand size Common Effect N/A (Passive) ) may be the best one, as even though it only increases hand size by 1, it has no punishments or limits. Troubadour ( hand size, <br> hand each round Win consecutive rounds by <abbr title="Discards are OK to use.">playing only 1 hand</abbr> Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) and Turtle Bean ( hand size, reduces by each round Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) can be used too, but the first one removes a hand, which makes it more punishing to use DNA, and the second one can only be used temporarily as it will give less hand size until it reaches 0.

In general, DNA synergizes with any Joker that requires specific cards:

- Steel Joker (Gives for each [[Steel Cards|]] in your <br> }} ) - Through duplication of Steel Cards.

- Baron () - Through duplication of Kings.

- Stone Joker (Gives for each [[Stone Card|]] in your <br> }} ) - Through duplication of Stone Cards.

- Shoot the Moon (Each held in hand gives Play every Heart card in your deck in one round. ) - Through duplication of Queens.

- Driver's License ( if you have at least Enhanced cards in your full deck <br> }} Enhance 16 cards in your deck Rare Multiplicative Mult Independent Driver') - Through duplication of cards with enhancements.

- Lucky Cat (This Joker gains every time a [[Lucky Cards|]] card triggers <br> }} ) - Through duplication of Lucky Cards, then playing them to trigger them.

- Golden Ticket (Played [[Gold Cards|]] ) - Through duplication of Gold Cards, then playing them.

- Hit the Road (This Joker gains for every discarded this round<br> }} Discard at the same time. ) - Through duplication of Jacks, then discarding them.

Specially, for Jokers that consume cards, DNA can replicate the consumed cards, allowing them to be used multiple times:

- Sixth Sense (If of round is a single , destroy it and create a [[Spectral Cards|]] card <br> Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) also share triggering condition with DNA, so if the first hand is a single 6, DNA will replicate the 6 to be destroyed, effectively generating spectral cards with no cost. (The order of the two makes no difference. If DNA is on the right, the 6 will still be replicated after being destroyed.)

- Vampire (This Joker gains per scoring played, removes card <br> }} ) can be upgraded without cost through duplication of cards with enhanced effects. To achieve this, DNA needs to be placed on the left of Vampire, otherwise it will replicate card without enhancement. Note that the combination of the two will generally add many cards without enhancement to deck, hindering the effect of Vampire.

- Glass Joker (This Joker gains for every [[Glass_Cards|]] that is destroyed <br> }} Have at least [[Glass_Cards|]] ) - DNA can replenish broken Glass Cards.

### Vouchers

- Grabber () and Nacho Tong (Permanently gain an additional hand per round Play a total of 2,) both give additional hands to play, which can be used to trigger DNA with fewer consequences.

- Wasteful () and Recyclomancy () both give additional discards, which can help in finding a specific card for DNA to copy.

- Paint Brush ( ) and Palette ( hand size Reduce your hand size down to five cards. ) both increase hand size, which makes it easier to find the desired card for DNA to copy.

### Blinds

Any blind that disables cards in your deck ( The Plant ( ), The Club ( ), etc.) will not prevent DNA from triggering, allowing you to still copy a disabled card for that round.

- This Joker is very easy to locate when playing against Amber Acorn ( ), as it still shakes even when flipped face-down.

## Anti-Synergies

### Decks

- Black Deck ( Joker slot <br> hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ), Blast Off ( hands per round<br> discards per round<br> Joker Slots Eternal <br>Eternal <br><br>Banned [[Vouchers]]: , <br>Banned Jokers: ), and Golden Needle ( Discards each cost <br><br> hands per round<br> discards per round<br>Start with Banned Jokers: <br>Banned [[Vouchers]]: , ) all reduce the number of hands to play, which can discourage using DNA while a player's hands aren't strong enough.

 - Golden Needle ( Discards each cost <br><br> hands per round<br> discards per round<br>Start with Banned Jokers: <br>Banned [[Vouchers]]: , ) is especially bad, as it only starts with one hand, which means that without a strong High Card build, DNA is unlikely to be a useful purchase.

### Poker Hands

DNA is weakest when building for Straight or Straight Flush, because it is harder to duplicate cards into runs than it is to duplicate cards into sets.

### Jokers

- Erosion ( for each card below in your full deck <br> }} )'s effect depends on having fewer cards than you started with, and the cards added by DNA make it very difficult to get and stay under that limit.

- Unless with a build around High Cards, triggering DNA will waste one hand per round, which means Ice Cream (<br> ) and Seltzer () will be depleted more quickly.

- While Burglar (When [[Blind|]] is selected, ) gives extra hands, it also removes discards. This means that if the first drawn hand doesn't have the wanted card, DNA will miss it's effect.

- Stuntman (,<br> hand size Earn at least 100 million (100,000,000) Chips in a single hand. ) reduces hand size, which may make it harder to find the desired cards for DNA to copy.

### Vouchers

- Telescope ([[Booster Packs|]] always contain the [[Planet Cards|]] card for your most played [[Poker Hands|]] ) will likely identify High Card as the most-played hand because of all the card duplication being done, making it harder to find Planet Cards for the player's preferred hand—unless that preferred hand is High Card.

### Blinds

- The Needle ( ) limits the player to 1 hand which heavily discourages the use of DNA, unless the player can score high enough playing a single card.

- The Mouth ( ) only allows 1 hand type, so unless players build around High Cards, they must play a winning hand type first, missing DNA's effect.

- The Psychic ( ) requires 5 cards to be played, therefore disabling DNA as the illegal hand will not trigger its effect.

- The Pillar ( ) will debuff the copy of the card that DNA copied after playing the first hand, even if the card played isn't debuffed, preventing benefits from modifiers and seals.

- If Crimson Heart ( ) debuffs DNA on the first hand, it cannot duplicate any card.

### Stakes

- On Blue Stake difficulty or higher, there is a -1 Discard penalty which can make it harder to find the specific card for DNA to copy.

## Trivia

- DNA is one of only three Jokers that add cards to your deck. The others are Marble Joker (Adds one [[Stone Cards|]] card to the deck when [[Blinds|]] ) and Certificate (When round begins, add a random with a random [[Seal|]] to your hand Have a [[Gold_Cards|Gold playing card]] with a [[Gold_Seal|]] Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ).

- DNA, short for Deoxyribonucleic acid, is one of the "building blocks" of life, involved in the growth and development of all known organisms and many viruses. The card image is a typical representation of a double-helix, a section of a DNA strand. The four different colors connecting the strands together represent the four key ingredients of DNA: adenine, cytosine, thymine, and guanine.

- DNA is tied with Egg (Gains of at end of round Common Economy N/A (Passive) ) for the shortest Joker name in Balatro, containing only 3 letters.
