# Turtle Bean

Copyable Perishable Eternal

Turtle Bean is an Uncommon Effect Joker that initially provides +5 Hand Size, but decreases by 1 every blind until it destroys itself, indicated by the word "Eaten!" appearing under the Joker, after which it has bean and gone.

## Synergies

### Decks

Any deck can benefit from Turtle Bean.

### Poker Hands

Any 5-card hand can benefit from Turtle Bean. In particular, the extra hand size makes finding matching cards for high-value hands, like Straight Flush or Flush Five, much easier.

### Jokers

If your goal is huge hand sizes, then:

- Juggler ( hand size Common Effect N/A (Passive) ): +1 hand size.

- Troubadour ( hand size, <br> hand each round Win consecutive rounds by <abbr title="Discards are OK to use.">playing only 1 hand</abbr> Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ): +2 hand size, at the cost of -1 hand per round.

Turtle Bean has particular synergies with:

- Any Joker that provides held in-hand bonuses like Baron (), Shoot the Moon (Each held in hand gives Play every Heart card in your deck in one round. ), or Reserved Parking () benefits, since Turtle Bean allows larger hand sizes.

- Throwback ( for each [[Blinds|]] skipped this run <br> }} ): Since Throwback benefits from skipping blinds, this extends the life of Turtle Bean.

### Consumables

Note that the increased Hand Size from Turtle Bean also applies to Tarot and Spectral packs. Thus:

- Tarot and Spectral Cards with selected card effects have a wider selection of cards to choose from your deck.

- Ouija (Converts all cards in hand to a single random [[Card Ranks|]]<br> hand size Available from start. ) and Sigil (Converts all cards in hand to a single random [[Card Suits|]] Available from start. ) convert substantially more cards if used early in Turtle Bean's duration.

### Card Modifiers

- Because Turtle Bean lasts exactly 5 rounds, the Perishable () sticker has no effect on it, as it will get disappear immediately before it is set to be debuffed.

### Blinds

- If Crimson Heart ( ) disables Turtle Bean, the hand size will return to normal, but current cards in hand will stay intact until the next discard (ex: 13/8). By quitting to Main Menu and returning, the hand size actually permanently increases (ex: 13/13), giving players a permanent hand size upgrade.

## Anti-synergies

### Jokers

- Blackboard ( ) can be harder to trigger, as the increased hand size can make it harder to hold only  Clubs and  Spades in your hand.

- More cards in hand mean that Blue Joker ( for each remaining card in <br> }} ) will give fewer chips.

- The increased hand size will make it hard for Raised Fist () to give more score from high-ranked cards.

## Strategies

The sell value of Turtle Bean does not depend on its current Hand Size value, so it is best to sell Turtle Bean when it is at +1 Hand Size, instead of letting it get destroyed (worth $0).

## Trivia

- The black turtle bean is the only type of turtle bean. It is used in Latin American and Indian cuisine, to name a few. LocalThunk drew inspiration for this Joker from Mexican food.1]

- The turtle bean is often just referred to as a black bean in some areas.

- This Joker is part of the group of "Consumable" Jokers that can be destroyed with usage, dubbed "Food Jokers" due to their shared theme. The other Jokers in this group are Ice Cream (<br> ), Popcorn ( <br> ), Gros Michel (<br> ), Cavendish ( <br> ), Egg (Gains of at end of round Common Economy N/A (Passive) ), Seltzer (), Ramen (, ), and Diet Cola (Sell this card to create a free [[Tags|]] Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ).2]3]4]
