# Acrobat

of round Copyable Perishable Eternal

Acrobat is an Uncommon Multiplicative Mult Joker that provides X3 Mult on the final hand of a round. This means Acrobat will only come into play when the player exhausts all of their available hands against a given Blind. This Joker is unlocked once the player plays 200 hands along all runs.

### Poker Hands

This Joker does not directly synergize with any hand.

### Jokers

- Green Deck ( At end of each Round: <br> per remaining <br> per remaining <br> Earn no Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ): As expending all hands is necessary to activate Acrobat, it leaves no additional hands leftover for bonus Money, possibly less if all discards were used as well.

### Jokers

- Jokers that decrease in value when playing hands (e.g. Seltzer () and Ice Cream (<br> )) will likely be used up faster, as Acrobat requires the player to exhaust all hands to trigger.

## Strategies

Acrobat is a useful Joker to have when playing against The Needle ( ) Blind, since The Needle will reduce the available hands for the player to only 1, ensuring the X3 Mult from Acrobat will be applied. It is also useful against The Wall ( ) and the Violet Vessel ( ), since the player will often go through all of their available hands while trying to overcome the larger score thresholds from those two Blinds, and the X3 Mult may be just enough of a boost to get the player over the finish line.

## Trivia

- The acrobats in the artwork could be performing using aerial silks or aerial straps, or perhaps a variation of corde lisse. The lower acrobat seems to be holding an aerial hoop. Quite often performers doing these types of acts will have a 'big finish' trick to finish their performance, represented by the X3 Mult on the last hand.

- Acrobat's artwork is used for the "Rule Bender" achievement.
