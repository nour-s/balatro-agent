# Swashbuckler

Mult) Copyable Perishable Eternal

Swashbuckler is a Common Additive Mult Joker that provides + Mult equal to the sum of the sell value of all other owned Jokers. This Joker is unlocked once the player sells 20 Jokers.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Black Deck ( Joker slot <br> hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ) gives an extra Joker slot, meaning Swashbuckler gets more Mult.

- Similarly, Five-Card Draw ( discards per round<br> hand size<br> Joker slots <br>Banned Jokers: , , ) gives two extra Joker slots, meaning Swashbuckler gets even more Mult.

- Swashbuckler generally works well with negative Jokers, as they will increase the amount of Joker slots while also having substantial sell values of their own. Therefore, the Anaglyph Deck ( After defeating each [[Blinds|]], gain a [[File:|24px]] [[Tags|]] Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) can synergize with Swashbuckler, where many players will opt to use their accumulating Double Tag ( ) on a Negative Tag ( ).

With 4 Eggs, this Joker gains +12 Mult at the end of every Blind.

- Inflation ( Permanently raise prices by on every purchase Banned [[Vouchers]]: , ) might work with Swashbuckler, as this deck permanently increases prices by $1 on every purchase, including the sell price. This Joker can provide a high amount of + Mult since it will scale when other Jokers' prices inflate.

### Poker Hands

Any poker hand can benefit from Swashbuckler.

### Jokers

Generally, Swashbuckler gets more benefits when it is with higher-rarity Jokers due to their higher sell values. In particular, the Legendary Jokers have a massive sell value of $10, which is double that of the next highest base sell value.

Some other notable synergies are:

- Gift Card (Add of to every [[Jokers|]] and [[Consumables|]] card at end of round Uncommon Economy N/A (Passive) ) adds to the sell value for all other jokers at once, effectively scaling Swashbuckler over time.

- Egg (Gains of at end of round Common Economy N/A (Passive) ) increases its sell value by $3 per blind, effectively scaling Swashbuckler over time.

- When either of the two previous Jokers has been paired with Swashbuckler for long enough, Ceremonial Dagger (When [[Blinds|]] is selected, destroy [[Jokers|Joker]] to the right and permanently add its sell value to this <br> }} ) is a very effective pivot away from Swashbuckler; because it will multiply built-up sell values by X2, it can both increase immediate scoring and open up a Joker Slot (or more) for other purposes.

- Hone (, , ) and Glow Up (, , and cards appear more often Have at least 5 Joker cards with Foil, Holographic, or Polychrome (or Negative) ) make it more likely for editioned Jokers to appear, which have a higher sell value, helping Swashbuckler gain +Mult.

- Antimatter ( ) gives an extra Joker slot, meaning Swashbuckler gets more Mult.

### Jokers

- Credit Card (Go up to in debt Common Economy N/A (Passive) ). Joker ( ), Jolly Joker ( ), Sly Joker ( ) and Splash (Every counts in scoring Common Effect N/A (Passive) ) all have a sell value of $1, which is not good for Swashbuckler.

### Vouchers

- Clearance Sale () and Liquidation () are not recommended, as they reduce the sell price of all Jokers and thus reduce the +Mult of Swashbuckler.

### Blinds

- Verdant Leaf ( ) requires selling a Joker, which means that unless the player does not rely on enhanced cards to win, Swashbuckler will lose +Mult.

### Stakes

- Gold Stake introduces Rental Stickers (), which only have a sell value of $1.

## Trivia

- Swashbucklers in fiction tend to be heroic types skilled in swordplay, acrobatics, and charm, ready to rescue princesses and with a strong sense of honour. Famous swashbucklers include Robin Hood, The Three Musketeers, and the Princess Bride's Dread Pirate Roberts.

- Swashbuckler's default Mult value is +1 Mult, which can be seen when checking it in the collection of Main Menu. It still gives +0 Mult during a run if there are no other Jokers.
