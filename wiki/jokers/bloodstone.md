# Bloodstone

Heart suit to give X1.5 Mult when scored Copyable Perishable Eternal

Bloodstone is an Uncommon Hearts-oriented Multiplicative Mult Joker which has a 1 in 2, or a 50% chance to immediately provide X1.5 Mult for each  Heart scored.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Checkered Deck ( ) is the easiest Deck to pair with Bloodstone, as it starts with 26 Heart cards, which makes playing Heart Flushes consistently an easy task.

- Blue Deck ( hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] )'s extra hand makes it easier to get a winning amount of Bloodstone triggers.

### Poker Hands

Any Flush hand with the Heart suit works with Bloodstone, such as:

- Flush

- Straight Flush

- Flush House

- Flush Five

Although, any 5-card hand that includes 2-3 Heart cards can benefit greatly as well.

### Jokers

 - It may be suitable to hunt for Planet cards and/or scaling Mult Jokers if Four Fingers must remain in conjunction with Bloodstone.

### Blinds

- The Head ( ) will debuff all Heart cards, meaning Bloodstone cannot activate.

- The Pillar ( ) debuffs all previously played cards of the Ante, which may debuff a substantial amount of the player's Heart cards if Bloodstone is vital to the current Joker Build.

## Usage Notes

- As previously mentioned, Checkered Deck ( ) starts with 26 Heart cards, making it substantially easier to reach 30 Heart cards total to unlock Bloodstone.

## Trivia

- Bloodstone is one of four stone/mineral-themed jokers unlocked by having 30 cards of that suit in your deck. The others are Arrowhead () (spades), Rough Gem () (diamonds), and Onyx Agate () (clubs).

- A bloodstone (also known as a Heliotrope) is a typically green stone with distinctive red spots resembling drops of blood, hence the name.

- Bloodstone could be connected to Lusty Joker, with the name relating to the term "bloodlust" or "lust for blood".

- Before Update 1.0.1f, this was the only chance-based Joker to have a 1 in 3 chance, and therefore required two Oops! All 6s (Doubles all [[listed probabilities| ]] <br> -> )}} In one hand, earn at least chips Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) Oops!) to have a 100% activation chance. It has since been changed to a 1 in 2 chance of triggering.
