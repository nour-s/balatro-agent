# Chaos Theory

Copyable Perishable Eternal

Chaos Theory is an unused Joker. The name "Chaos Theory" is shown in the video Lumpy Touch made about this Joker.1] It never had a name or effect in the game files.2]

## Trivia

- This Joker is drawn by Lumpy Touch1] who also created the art for Vagabond (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] ).

- The art for this Joker was found in the files of the 0.9.2 version of Balatro.3]
