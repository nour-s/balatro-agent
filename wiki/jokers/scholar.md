# Scholar

Mult when scored Copyable Perishable Eternal

Scholar is a Common Chips and Additive Mult Joker which gives +20 Chips and +4 Mult per Ace scored.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Abandoned Deck ( Start run with no [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] in your deck Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) can reliably find Aces more often than the typical 52-card decks.

- Red Deck ( ) gives +1 discard, allowing for more opportunities to dig for Aces.

- Certain Erratic Deck ( All [[Card Ranks|]] and [[Suits|]] in deck are randomized Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) seeds can have more Aces.

### Poker Hands

- Pair of Aces

- Three of a Kind with Aces

- Four of a Kind with Aces

- Five of a Kind with Aces

- Flush Five with Aces

- Straight or Straight Flush with 5-4-3-2-A or A-K-Q-J-10

- Full House with 2 or 3 Aces

- Flush House with 2 or 3 Aces

- In general, the more Aces you play in a hand, the more valuable Scholar will be.

### Consumables

Tarot Cards:

- The Hanged Man (Destroys up to selected cards Available from start. ) can be used to thin out the deck and make Aces easier to find.

- Death (Select cards, convert the card into the card<br> Available from start. ) can be used to turn other cards into Aces.

- Grim (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Aces|]] to your hand Available from start. ) adds two enhanced Aces to the deck in exchange for destroying a random card.

## Trivia

- The A+ mark is reminiscent of a teacher marking their student's homework with a red ballpoint pen.

- Aces give you +4 Mult and +20 chips (4/20), because aces are high.

- Scholar's sprite is missing a pixel on the "E" in the Top Left "Joker" label and on the "K" in the Bottom Right label.

- There are two Scholar sprites in the game files: one with shading on the hair and one without.

- Scholar is one of only two Jokers to provide both Chips and Mult buff to scored cards, the other being Walkie Talkie ().
