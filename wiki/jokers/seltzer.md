# Seltzer

Copyable Perishable Eternal

Seltzer is an Uncommon Retrigger Joker that temporarily boosts played hands' effect by re-triggering each scored card. It lasts for a total of 10 hands and will disappear after the 10th hand is played and retriggered (denoted by the text "Drank!").

## Usage Notes

- Seltzer does not retrigger a Red Seal (). A card with a Red Seal will only activate three times: once for the initial trigger, once for the Red Seal, and then lastly, for Seltzer.

- The Non-Perishable ( All Jokers are Eternal Banned Jokers: , ; , , ; ; , , and <br>Banned [[Blinds]]: ) challenge bans Seltzer.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Zodiac Deck ( Start run with , , and Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) is helpful for finding tarot cards to make the enhanced cards that make Seltzer work effectively.

- Red Deck ( ) is able to find better hands on average, so Seltzer will last longer, as less hands need to be played per round.

- Black Deck ( Joker slot <br> hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ) is able to stack more Jokers that benefit from Seltzer's effect. Adding even a single XMult Joker like Ancient Joker (Each played card with [[Card Suits|]] gives when scored, <br> <small>suit changes at end of round</small> ) can be an extra X7.5 Mult normally, but with Seltzer it becomes an extra X57 Mult.

- Plasma Deck ( Balance [[Chips|]] and [[Mult|]] when calculating score for played hand <br> base Blind size Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) gains a huge boost from stacking XMult modifiers, which Seltzer enables very well.

### Poker Hands

Any 5-card hands like Flush, Straight, or Full House will provide more retriggers from Seltzer. In particular, Flush Fives, with five of the same very strong duplicated card, work very well.

### Jokers

- Any Joker that triggers on scoring will have its effect doubled, for instance Scary Face () for Chips, Fibonacci (Each played , , , , ) for +Mult, The Idol (Each played [[Card Ranks|]] of [[Card Suits|]] gives when scored<br><small>Card changes every round</small> In one hand, ) for XMult, or Rough Gem () for money.

- Jokers that scale based on cards played, like Lucky Cat (This Joker gains every time a [[Lucky Cards|]] card triggers <br> }} ) and Hiker (), work very well with Seltzer, as the cards they scale with will always retrigger, and the scaling will remain after Seltzer is consumed.

- Jokers like 8 Ball ( chance for each played to create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when scored<br> ) (in particular, due to its on scoring nature) and Cartomancer (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when [[Blinds|]] is selected <br> Discover every Tarot Card. ) can provide frequent Tarot cards to buff up playing cards.

- Throwback ( for each [[Blinds|]] skipped this run <br> }} ) rewards you for skipping blinds, making Seltzer last longer and into more difficult Boss Blinds.

## Anti-Synergies

### Jokers

- Copying cards with DNA (If of round has only card, ) will quickly use up Seltzer's retriggers.

- Jokers that incentivize playing many hands per round, such as Green Joker ( per hand played <br> per discard <br> }} ) or Vagabond (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] ), will similarly cause Seltzer to be spent quickly.

### Boss Blinds

- If a hand that is not allowed is played during The Psychic ( ), The Mouth ( ) or The Eye ( ), Seltzer will still lose a retrigger.

## Strategies

In a build with many enhanced cards, Seltzer can be a powerful joker, especially in the late game. Due to Seltzer's limited lifespan, it is often used to get the highest scoring single hand possible in endless mode.

## Trivia

- The Joker's art depicts a seltzer bottle, also known as a soda siphon. These were historically used by clowns to spray soda water as a prank, which was the initial inspiration for the Joker.1]

 - Despite this, the pop-up before disappearing will say "Drank!", tying in with its status as a Food Joker instead2].

 - The center of the bottle features a small Joker/Jimbo logo similar to those on Riff-Raff (When [[Blind|]] is selected, create [[Common Jokers| ]] <br> ) and Baseball Card ([[Uncommon Jokers|]] ).

 - Seltzer's effect to retrigger all scoring cards in a played hand could be a reference to its real life function of needing to pull the bottle's trigger repeatedly to effectively spray water from the nozzle.

- In early demos of the game, Seltzer is referred to as "Selzer", which seems to be a spelling error. This is still true in internal references to the card within the game's code.

- This Joker is part of a group of "Consumable" Jokers that can be destroyed with usage, dubbed "Food Jokers" due to their shared theme. The other Jokers in this group are Ice Cream (<br> ), Popcorn ( <br> ), Gros Michel (<br> ), Cavendish ( <br> ), Egg (Gains of at end of round Common Economy N/A (Passive) ), Turtle Bean ( hand size, reduces by each round Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ), Ramen (, ), and Diet Cola (Sell this card to create a free [[Tags|]] Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ).3]4]
