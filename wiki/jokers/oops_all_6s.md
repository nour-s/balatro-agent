# Oops! All 6s

(ex: 1 in 3 -> 2 in 3) Copyable Perishable Eternal

Oops! All 6s is an Uncommon Effect Joker that doubles all listed probabilities (generally distinguished by green text in game). For example, a 2 in 5 chance would become a 4 in 5 chance.

Oops! All 6s works on almost every shown X in Y chance, including potentially undesirable effects, such as Cavendish ( <br> ) going extinct. The only listed probability Oops! All 6s does not affect is the 1 in 4 chance of flipping a card during the X-ray Vision ( cards are drawn face down ) challenge.

## Synergies

### Jokers

This Joker benefits Jokers that have probability effects:

- 8 Ball ( chance for each played to create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when scored<br> )'s effect becomes a 2 in 4 chance to generate a Tarot card when an 8 is scored.

- Space Joker ( chance to upgrade level of played [[Poker Hands|]] )'s effect becomes a 2 in 4 chance to upgrade any hand when played.

- Reserved Parking () and Business Card () become guaranteed to give money on held face cards and scored face cards respectively.

- Hallucination ( chance to create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when any [[Booster Packs|]] is opened <br> Common Effect N/A (Passive) ) guarantees a tarot card when opening a booster pack.

- Bloodstone ( ) becomes guaranteed to apply its multiplier effect.

- Gros Michel (<br> ) goes to a 2 in 6 chance of extinction, potentially causing it to go extinct sooner and allowing Cavendish ( <br> ) to be found more quickly—which then has a negligible 2 in 1000 chance of extinction, or at which point Oops! All 6s can be sold.

Oops! All 6s can also benefit Jokers that indirectly use probability effects:

- Lucky Cat (This Joker gains every time a [[Lucky Cards|]] card triggers <br> }} ) can scale faster, as Lucky Cards ( chance for <br> ) have an increased chance to trigger.

- Glass Joker (This Joker gains for every [[Glass_Cards|]] that is destroyed <br> }} Have at least [[Glass_Cards|]] ), Canio (This Joker gains when a card is destroyed <br> }} Find this [[Jokers|Joker]] from the [[Spectral Cards|]] card. ), and Erosion ( for each card below in your full deck <br> }} ) can scale faster due to the increased chance of Glass Cards (<br> ) breaking.

## Trivia

Old design of Oops! All 6s

- This Joker's name is a reference to Cap'n Crunch cereal, specifically the "Oops! All Berries" variant. It has become a popular meme online to refer to something excessive in this way.

- The Joker art depicts a unique cubic die with six pips on every visible face, the highest roll one can get on a standard 6-sided die. The six-pip pattern is also shown in the background.

- The example from the description (1 in 3 chance to 2 in 3) is now hypothetical - there are no 1 in 3 chances in the game since Bloodstone ( ) was updated in Update 1.0.1f.

- According to LocalThunk, Oops! All 6s' design used to be in red, but it was changed to keep consistent with the color of listed probabilities.1]

 - The original red design for Oops! All 6s can still be seen on the icon for the achievement "You Get What You Get".

- This Joker shares its unlock requirement with the "10K" Achievement.
