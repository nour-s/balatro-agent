# Walkie Talkie

and +4 Mult when scored Copyable Perishable Eternal
Walkie Talkie is a Common Joker that uniquely gives a boost to Chips and Additive Mult simultaneously. Whenever a 10 or a 4 is scored, this Joker will give an immediate +10 Chips and +4 Mult; if the 10 or 4 is retriggered either by a Joker effect or its own Red Seal (), the effect of Walkie Talkie will activate again on that same card.

Even without additional assistance, this Joker represents a +50 Chips and +20 Mult bonus on a hand full of scoring 10s and 4s. For reference, a Level 1 Full House (10 10 10 4 4) would normally yield 312 score; this Joker increases it to a whopping 3,072 all by itself. The score increase is even higher on hands that allow for high amounts of 10s and 4s to score while having even higher base Chips and Mult.

With the amount of Deck manipulation present in the game thanks to the presence of certain Tarot Cards and Spectral Cards, Walkie Talkie is especially appetizing for its rarity and is definitely worth building around if acquired early, as it can allow for pivots into the very strong secret hands.

## Synergies

### Decks

Any Deck synergizes well with Walkie Talkie due to its versatility. However, these have a slightly easier time using it than the others:

- Abandoned Deck ( Start run with no [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] in your deck Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ): Has the highest state of advantage with this Joker, due to having less cards in the Deck overall, thereby making it easier to find 10s and 4s.

- Painted Deck ( hand size, <br> Joker slot Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ): This Joker serves as good role compression, providing Chips and Additive Mult while taking up just 1 of the 4 Joker slots. On top of this, the increased hand size allows easier finding of higher quantities of 10s and 4s.

- Erratic Deck ( All [[Card Ranks|]] and [[Suits|]] in deck are randomized Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ): A seed favoring 10s and 4s would be most optimal, but any seed with a higher-than-average number of either can work well.

 - Notably, the seed "7LB2WVPK" starts with 52 10 of Spades and has a Walkie Talkie on the first shop.

- Ghost Deck ( [[Spectral Cards|]] cards may appear in the shop, start with a card Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ), Magic Deck ( Start run with the voucher and copies of Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ), and Zodiac Deck ( Start run with , , and Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ): All these have more overall access to the Deck fixing methods that this Joker loves, but they do not have a distinct consistency advantage right away like the aforementioned ones do.

### Poker Hands

Focus on these hands in the early game to consistently maximize this Joker's impact:

- Two Pair

- Three of a Kind

- Full House (the best choice, due to having 5 scoring cards while not being substantially harder to find)

As you manipulate your Deck to include more 10s and 4s, pivot towards these hands (note that 3 of these 4 are secret hands, meaning you will not be able to level scale them right away):

- Four of a Kind

- Five of a Kind

- Flush House

- Flush Five

## Trivia

- Walkie Talkie is one of only two Jokers to provide both Chips and Mult buff to scored cards, the other being Scholar ().

- The affected cards, and score values, are a reference to the phrase "10-4", commonly used by US law enforcement to acknowledge that they have understood a message received over their walkie-talkies. It became more common knowledge among the public around the mid-to-late-1950s, and is now sometimes used in informal conversation in Anglophone countries to show acknowledgement of a statement.

- The German version of Walkie Talkie's description erroneously states that the Joker gives "X4 Mult" instead of "+4 Mult".
