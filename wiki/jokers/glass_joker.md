# Glass Joker

that is destroyed
 (Currently X1 Mult) Copyable Perishable Eternal

Glass Joker is an Uncommon scaling Joker that provides Multiplicative Mult to all played hands. It starts at X1 Mult and gains X0.75 Mult every time a Glass Card (<br> ) is destroyed, be it breaking after being played in a hand, or the effects of Sixth Sense (If of round is a single , destroy it and create a [[Spectral Cards|]] card <br> Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ), Trading Card (If of round has only card, ), or The Hanged Man (Destroys up to selected cards Available from start. ) Tarot Card. It is important to note that Spectral Cards that can destroy Glass Cards, such as Immolate (Destroys random cards in hand, gain Available from start. ), Familiar (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ), Grim (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Aces|]] to your hand Available from start. ), and Incantation (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card_Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Number cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ), do not increase Glass Joker. This seems to be a bug.

Among the Multiplicative Mult Jokers that can have their value increased, Glass Joker is one of the fastest-scaling; after breaking just 3 Glass Cards, it will give X3.25 Mult to all played hands, making it stronger than Cavendish ( <br> ) (only Canio (This Joker gains when a card is destroyed <br> }} Find this [[Jokers|Joker]] from the [[Spectral Cards|]] card. ) and Yorick (This Joker gains every cards discarded<br>}} Find this [[Jokers|Joker]] from the [[Spectral Cards|]] card. ), both exceedingly rare Legendary Jokers, have greater per-interval scaling values).

## Usage Notes

- Glass Joker will only appear in the shop after you have at least one Glass Card (<br> ) in your deck.

- To unlock this Joker, the player must have at least 5 Glass Cards (<br> ) in the deck

## Anti-synergies

### Decks

- Bram Poker ( Jokers no longer appear in the [[The Shop|]] Eternal <br>, <br>, ) starts with an Eternal Vampire (This Joker gains per scoring played, removes card <br> }} ), which means Glass Joker will not gain anything unless you use The Hanged Man (Destroys up to selected cards Available from start. ) or Trading Card (If of round has only card, ).

### Jokers

- Vampire (This Joker gains per scoring played, removes card <br> }} ) is not recommended, as it removes any Glass enhancement when scored, and players might need to destroy Glass cards by using The Hanged Man (Destroys up to selected cards Available from start. ) or Trading Card (If of round has only card, ).

- Blue Joker ( for each remaining card in <br> }} ) will give fewer chips if a lot of Glass Cards are destroyed.

### Consumables

- Due to a bug, Immolate (Destroys random cards in hand, gain Available from start. ), Grim (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Aces|]] to your hand Available from start. ), Incantation (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card_Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Number cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ), and Familiar (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ) destroying Glass Cards (<br> ) will NOT scale Glass Joker, wasting the Enhancement while not providing any value, althought the latter 3 can be used to create more Glass Cards to destroy.
