Bootstraps is an Uncommon Additive Mult Joker that grants +2 Mult for every $5 the player has. It does nothing if the player has $4 or less.

## Usage Notes

While Bootstrap's unlock condition may appear difficult to achieve, under the right circumstances it can be surprisingly easy.

- Ghost Deck ( [[Spectral Cards|]] cards may appear in the shop, start with a card Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) starts the player with a Hex (Add [[Card Modifiers#Editions|]] to a random [[Jokers|]], destroy all other Jokers Available from start. ), allowing any of the run's first purchased Jokers to become Polychrome ( Edition Joker (Polychrome).png).

 - In addition to this, Hex cannot destroy Eternal () Jokers, making the requirement even easier on Black Stake and above.

- As long as the player uses the given Hex on an Eternal Joker, the unlock can be achieved by finding a second Hex in the shop due to Ghost Deck's innate ability.

- Alternatively, the player can use Ankh (Create a copy of a random [[Jokers|]], destroy all other Jokers <br> from copy)}} Available from start. ) or Invisible Joker (After rounds, sell this card to a random Joker <br> /2}} <br> ]] from copy)}} Win a run without ever having more than Rare Effect N/A (Passive) ) to duplicate the Polychrome ( Edition Joker (Polychrome).png) Joker to unlock the requirement.

### Decks

- Green Deck ( At end of each Round: <br> per remaining <br> per remaining <br> Earn no Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ) has poor synergy because it encourages the player to spend money as they earn it.

- Luxury Tax ( Hold cards in hand for every you have<br><br> hand size ) encourages the player to not have too much money, which can make it harder to use Bootstraps effectively.

- The Omelette ( All give no reward money<br>Extra no longer earn money<br>Earn no at end of round 5x Banned Vouchers:<br><br><br><br>Banned Jokers:<br><br><br><br>), while not being a direct counter, makes it very hard to get money, making Bootstraps weaker.

### Jokers

- Vagabond (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] ) will never generate any Tarot cards if the player has a lot of money.

- Credit Card (Go up to in debt Common Economy N/A (Passive) ) is useless as going into debt makes Bootstraps do nothing.

### Blinds

- The Ox ( ) can neutralize Bootstraps if the most played hand is played, resetting money to $0.

- The Tooth ( ) subtracts money for how many cards are played in a given hand, potentially weakening Bootstraps.

### Consumables

- Wraith (Creates a random [[Rare Jokers| ]], sets money to Available from start. ) should not be used as it sets money to $0, making Bootstraps useless.

### Stake

- On Gold Stake, each Rental () Joker owned will weaken Bootstraps.

## Trivia

- This might be a reference to the phrase "pulling yourself up by your bootstraps", or to Bootstrap funding. The phrase originally described an impossible task, first used mockingly in an 1834 newspaper.1] Over time, it evolved to mean self-reliance, despite its ironic origins.

- As of Update 1.0.1f, having $-5 or less causes Bootstraps to erroneously show negative Mult (eg. +-2 Mult). This is purely visual, as Bootstraps does not do anything while this is in effect.
