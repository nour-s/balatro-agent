# Burglar

Copyable Perishable Eternal

Burglar is an Uncommon Effect Joker that gives 3 extra hands in exchange for all of the player's discard(s), doubling up as money generation if the player defeats the blind in 3 hands or less.

## Usage Notes

- Unlike other jokers related to hands and discards (such as Merry Andy ( discards each round, <br> hand size Win a run in or fewer rounds Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) )), the effect of Burglar triggers when selecting the Blind. This mean that if Burglar gets disabled during a round (by Crimson Heart ( )) or sold during a round, the hands and discards will not be changed. In other words, the player will still have 0 discards and get to keep the 3 extra hands for the rest of the round.

- Since Ceremonial Dagger (When [[Blinds|]] is selected, destroy [[Jokers|Joker]] to the right and permanently add its sell value to this <br> }} ) destroys the Jokers on the right side, Burglar would be destroyed before taking effect. For Madness (When or is selected, gain and a random Joker <br> }} ), the order of Burglar and Madness does not matter, as Burglar always activates before Madness.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Blue Deck ( hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ): The player starts with 1 extra hand, which when paired with Burglar, greatly increases the effectiveness of Jokers scaling from playing many hands.

- Green Deck ( At end of each Round: <br> per remaining <br> per remaining <br> Earn no Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ): As leftover hands and discards act as a pseudo-Interest, Burglar essentially trades $1 from each removed discard for $2 for each of the 3 extra hands.

- Black Deck ( Joker slot <br> hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ): Burglar mitigates the -1 Hand downside while also adding 2 extra hands, but removes all discards.

### Poker Hands

Burglar's extra hands make use of poker hands that can be played at a high frequency, such as High Card, Pair or Two Pair. On a deck that concentrates on one specific rank, this can also be Three of a Kind, Five of a Kind or Flush Five.

### Jokers

- Ride the Bus (This Joker gains per hand played without a scoring card <br> }} ), Green Joker ( per hand played <br> per discard <br> }} ), Square Joker (This Joker gains if played hand has exactly cards <br> }} ), Spare Trousers (This Joker gains if played hand contains a [[Poker Hands|]] <br> }} ), or Supernova (Adds the number of times [[Poker Hands|]] ) all scale from playing hands, which is made way easier thanks to Burglar.

- Burglar removing all discards allows Mystic Summit ( ) to always trigger its +15 Mult effect.

- Burglar, unlike its discard counterpart Merry Andy ( discards each round, <br> hand size Win a run in or fewer rounds Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ), can be copied by Blueprint (Copies ability of [[Jokers|]] )/ Brainstorm (Copies the ability of leftmost [[Jokers|]] Discard a . ) for an extra 3 hands each copy. This effect also poses no downside because of how the copying effects can be used on another Joker afterwards without any consequences.

 - Additionally, because of how Burglar triggers when blind is selected, the player can abuse this by setting the game speed down and swap Burglar with other Jokers that also triggers at the start of round, such as Marble Joker (Adds one [[Stone Cards|]] card to the deck when [[Blinds|]] ) or Certificate (When round begins, add a random with a random [[Seal|]] to your hand Have a [[Gold_Cards|Gold playing card]] with a [[Gold_Seal|]] Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) for Blueprint (Copies ability of [[Jokers|]] )/ Brainstorm (Copies the ability of leftmost [[Jokers|]] Discard a . ) to copy after Burglar has done its job. Note that while the game speed being slower makes this trick much easier, it still requires decent concentration and reflexes.

- Vagabond (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] ) can generate more Tarot cards if the added hands are used.

- Card Sharp ( ) can get more triggers thanks to the fact that there can be more hands played, therefore a greater chance of the poker hand being played twice or more.

- Loyalty Card ( every hands played<br> ) (almost) always guarantees a trigger every blind.

- The aforementioned Green Joker ( per hand played <br> per discard <br> }} ) and Ramen (, ) can no longer be reduced since there's no discards to be used, except during The Hook ( )'s forced discard effect.

### Blinds

Burglar neutralizes the following Boss blinds remarkably well:

- The Needle ( ) (Play only 1 hand): Burglar only triggers after The Needle has taken effect, which lets the player gain 3 extra hands at the expense of all discards.

- The Water ( ) (Start with 0 discards): The Water functionally does nothing as Burglar already covers its effect, making it a glorified Small Blind ( ) with a higher score requirement.

- Verdant Leaf ( ) (All playing cards debuffed until 1 Joker sold): On Ante 8 runs, selling this Joker to remove this Blind's debuff does nothing to hurt your build since its effect only goes away when the next Blind is selected.

## Anti-Synergies

### Decks

- Red Deck ( ) and Five-Card Draw ( discards per round<br> hand size<br> Joker slots <br>Banned Jokers: , , ): The extra discards provided by them are negated when a blind is selected.

### Jokers

- Any Joker that triggers off of discards like Banner ( ) and Delayed Gratification (Earn per if no discards are used by end of the round Common Economy N/A (Passive) ) will have no effect.

- Jokers that need cards to be discarded to scale like Yorick (This Joker gains every cards discarded<br>}} Find this [[Jokers|Joker]] from the [[Spectral Cards|]] card. ) and Castle (This Joker gains per discarded [[Card Suits|]] card, suit changes every round <br> }} ) can no longer scale.

- Jokers which give extra discards like Drunkard ( discard each round Common Effect N/A (Passive) ) and Merry Andy ( discards each round, <br> hand size Win a run in or fewer rounds Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) will have no effect.

 - The Merry Andy, in particular, with the Burglar, will only decrease hand size by 1.

- Jokers that activate on discard like Mail-In Rebate (Earn for each discarded [[Card Ranks|]], ), Trading Card (If of round has only card, ) and Burnt Joker (Upgrade the level of the first poker hand each round Sell 50 cards. ) can no longer function.

- Jokers that decrease in value when playing hands like Seltzer () and Ice Cream (<br> ) will likely be used up faster.

- Jokers that activate on the final hand such as the Acrobat ( ) and Dusk (). Given that Burglar provides more hands, it would delay the chances for these jokers to come into effect.

- DNA (If of round has only card, ) and Sixth Sense (If of round is a single , destroy it and create a [[Spectral Cards|]] card <br> Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) may have anti-synergy with Burglar if the first hand does not have the wanted card to be played. This is specially worse with a small hand size.

### Blinds

Some bosses affect the player in such ways that makes not having discards highly detrimental, crippling the run's potential by a wide margin:

- The Hook ( )'s forced discard effect makes building specific poker hands significantly harder if the played hands are also throwaway hands.

 - Using a high amount of hands on this Boss blind, with or without a small deck can lead to an instant game over when the playing deck runs out of cards.

- The Mouth ( ): The player is locked to only playing the poker hands that can be formed from the starting hand. If there are no ways of altering the playing cards first drawn, like Death (Select cards, convert the card into the card<br> Available from start. ) or Strength (Increases rank of up to selected cards by Available from start. ), this may be a dead-end on builds that only functions when playing a specific poker hand.

- The Fish ( ): After drawing the starting cards, all playing cards will be drawn face down, effectively making the player fight while blind.

- The Tooth ( ): Since the player will often play throwaway hands as pseudo-discards, this Boss blind can potentially be a deficit of $3 - 15.

As mentioned earlier, the effect of Burglar on the current round cannot be relieved by selling it. Therefore, any method that can avoid specific Boss Blinds (e.g. Luchador (Sell this card to disable the current [[Blinds|]] Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ), Chicot (Disables effect of every [[Blinds|]] Find this [[Jokers|Joker]] from the [[Spectral Cards|]] card. ), or Director's Cut (Reroll [[Blinds|Boss Blind]] time per Ante, per roll Base Director')) should be considered. Otherwise, Burglar should be sold before choosing Boss Blind if it would be difficult to deal with.

### Vouchers

- The +1 discard effect given by Wasteful () and Recyclomancy () is negated when a blind is selected.

## Strategies

- Burglar allows Jokers scaling off of played hands to scale much faster, such as Ride the Bus (This Joker gains per hand played without a scoring card <br> }} ), Green Joker ( per hand played <br> per discard <br> }} ), Square Joker (This Joker gains if played hand has exactly cards <br> }} ), Spare Trousers (This Joker gains if played hand contains a [[Poker Hands|]] <br> }} ), or Supernova (Adds the number of times [[Poker Hands|]] ).

- Burglar lets the player generate much more resources with Jokers that generate value off of played hands, such as Reserved Parking (), Space Joker ( chance to upgrade level of played [[Poker Hands|]] ) or Vagabond (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] ).

- A build capable of scoring the elusive Naneinf score typically consists of playing on the Plasma Deck ( Balance [[Chips|]] and [[Mult|]] when calculating score for played hand <br> base Blind size Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ), while on the Boss blind The Serpent ( ), after copying Burglar with Blueprint (Copies ability of [[Jokers|]] ) and Brainstorm (Copies the ability of leftmost [[Jokers|]] Discard a . ), and holding both Baron () and Mime ().

## Trivia

- The Burglar wears the classic black-and-white stripes of an American prison uniform, first introduced in the New York prison system in the 1820s. This suggests the Burglar is also currently an inmate of a jail, or perhaps an escapee.
