# Hanging Chad

Copyable Perishable Eternal

Hanging Chad is a Common Retrigger Joker that can retrigger the first scoring played card in a Poker Hand twice. This includes any Card Modifiers and/or extra+ Chips from Hiker (), making it three times more effective.

## Usage Notes

- Hanging Chad will not retrigger a Red Seal (). A card with a Red Seal that is played as the first scoring card will only trigger for a total of four times (once for the initial trigger, twice for Hanging Chad, and once more for the Red Seal).

- DNA (If of round has only card, ) will not be triggered another two times by Hanging Chad and will only produce one copy of the played card.

- To unlock this Joker, the player must beat a Boss Blind with a High Card.

### Poker Hands

- Cards in a Poker Hand can be rearranged in a player's hand before it is played, such that their highest scoring or most Modified card gets retriggered by Hanging Chad (i.e. the left-most card in a played hand). It does not matter how the cards are re-ordered in a player's Poker Hand. For example, a Straight will still count as one even if its played as Queen, Ace, King, Jack, 10. The Queen, in this case, will have triple its effect this way (i.e. it was a Polychrome Bonus Card).

- Smaller hands like High Card or Three of a Kind can help mitigate their lack of played scoring cards, by using Hanging Chad. A singular especially powerful card can make triple its impact with this Joker.

## Trivia

Ted Mosby's "Hanging Chad" costume

- The dot and line markings along the left edge of the card are binary numbers, where a dot represents 0 and a dash represents 1. The four visible numbers are, from top to bottom, 1, 2, 4, and 5. The hanging chad presumably obscures the symbols "••||" or "0011", which is binary for 3.

- A "chad" is the fragment of paper created when a hole is made in paper or similar substances, such as with a hole punch. A "hanging" chad is one that hasn't fully separated, remaining attached at a single corner. This became a major issue during the 2000 U.S. presidential election, where hanging chads on punch card ballots led to controversy and multiple recounts, ultimately requiring a court decision on whether such ballots should be counted.

 - A visual reference to this appears in "How I Met Your Mother", where Ted Mosby wears a "Hanging Chad" costume in Season 1, Episode 6. This specific depiction of a "Hanging Chad" closely resembles the design of the card.

- Despite being see through from the front, this Joker has the default face down texture.

- This is the only retrigger joker that is not uncommon.
