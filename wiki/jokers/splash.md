# Splash

Copyable Perishable Eternal

Splash is a Common Effect Joker that makes all the cards you play count when scoring, not just the ones that make up the formal Poker Hand. This includes triggering seals and enhancements on each of the additional cards, and these will also trigger any Joker effects.

If the player is very desperate for chips, Splash can also provide an extra 20-40 chips for low-scoring hands like High Card.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Blue Deck ( hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ) may see some more value out of this card due to its extra hand each round.

- While Black Deck ( Joker slot <br> hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ) removes a hand, it also adds an extra Joker Slot, making it easier to keep Splash alongside other scoring Jokers.

### Poker Hands

This Joker especially helps High Card and Pair builds which otherwise lose out on the ability to stack effects from enhanced cards or triggering jokers such as Fibonacci (Each played , , , , ) multiple times in a hand.

### Poker Hands

The following hands are already made up of five cards, and thus get no benefit from Splash:

- Straight*

- Flush*

- Full House

- Straight Flush (and Royal Flush)*

- Five of a Kind

- Flush House

- Flush Five

*Unless holding Four Fingers (All and can be made with 4 cards Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ).

### Jokers

- Ride the Bus (This Joker gains per hand played without a scoring card <br> }} ) is anti-synergistic, as it can be reset by a face card that would have otherwise had no effect.

- Marble Joker (Adds one [[Stone Cards|]] card to the deck when [[Blinds|]] ) adds Stone Cards (<br>), which count to the score regardless of the hand, making Splash unnecessary.

## Trivia

- This joker was available since the first demo of the game and has never been changed.

- In poker, splashing is the act of carelessly dropping or throwing chips into the pot, causing them to scatter, rather than neatly moving or piling them. This joker similarly allows you to carelessly play cards, since they will all score regardless of poker hands.

- In collectible card games such as Magic: The Gathering, a "Splash" is a term for the practice of using only a very small amount of cards of a color in a deck which is filled with cards of other colors. In a similar vein, this Joker allows the player to "Splash" a small amount of cards that wouldn't normally score into their deck.
