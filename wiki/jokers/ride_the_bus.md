# Ride the Bus

card
 (Currently +0 Mult) Copyable Perishable Eternal

Ride the Bus is a Common Joker that provides a slow but steady source of scaling Mult. It starts at +0 Mult and gains +1 Mult added for every hand played without a scoring Face card, and it resets to +0 Mult if a Face card is scored.

Acquiring it early and carefully avoiding scoring face cards will lead to a high amount of Mult in the late game, especially if an Edition like Polychrome can be applied to it.

Face cards can be played safely as long as they do not score. For example, playing an Ace and a Queen will not count as playing a face card (because it is an Ace High Card). Playing a hand with debuffed face cards also does not reset Ride the Bus.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Abandoned Deck ( Start run with no [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] in your deck Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) is perfect for this Joker, as it starts without face cards.

 - Similarly, Medusa ( Eternal 52 cards, with all face cards replaced by Stone cards) is also perfect for this Joker, as it also starts without face cards.

- Erratic Deck ( All [[Card Ranks|]] and [[Suits|]] in deck are randomized Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) might have more or less face cards than usual, making it harder or easier to play around Ride the Bus.

- Blue Deck ( hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ) gives +1 hand every round, allowing for more scaling.

### Poker Hands

Any hand will work with this Joker. However, the smaller the hand type, the better Ride the Bus is. This makes it ideal for High Card or Pair builds, while Straights and Flushes builds can be more difficult.

### Jokers

- Jokers whose effects trigger based on numbered cards can be more useful, such as Even Steven (Played cards with [[Card Ranks|]] rank give when scored <br> ), Odd Todd (Played cards with [[Card Ranks|]] rank give when scored <br> ), Fibonacci (Each played , , , , ), and Hack (Retrigger each played , , , ).

- Canio (This Joker gains when a card is destroyed <br> }} Find this [[Jokers|Joker]] from the [[Spectral Cards|]] card. ) scales greatly from destroying face cards, thus both thinning the deck and providing a significant XMult to complement the additive Mult of Ride the Bus.

 - Though, Canio encourages adding face cards to the deck in order to destroy them, which can make it harder to work around Ride the Bus.

- Trading Card (If of round has only card, ) can destroy face cards, making it easier for the player to avoid resetting Ride the Bus.

- Burglar (When [[Blind|]] is selected, ) adds 3 extra hands and removes all discards, allowing Ride The Bus to scale faster.

 - However, missing out on discards can be dangerous, as the player might be forced to reset Ride The Bus. It is especially important to remove face cards from the deck when running Burglar and Ride the Bus.

- Death (Select cards, convert the card into the card<br> Available from start. ) and The Hanged Man (Destroys up to selected cards Available from start. ) can help remove some face cards in the deck.

## Trivia

The Regina bus stop sign that the card art is based on.

- According to LocalThunk, the name of the card is a reference to the drinking game Ride the Bus.1]

- A bus usually keeps driving until it has to stop to pick up passengers. In Balatro, the face cards — Jacks, Queens, and Kings — represent people, and the "Route 210-A" could be read as allowing cards 2-10 and Ace to continue onward.

- The design of this Joker is based on a bus stop sign near LocalThunk's apartment in Regina, Saskatchewan, Canada.2]

- During the first demo, Ride the Bus provided +2 Mult per consecutive hand played without scoring Face Cards, making it as strong as Spare Trousers (This Joker gains if played hand contains a [[Poker Hands|]] <br> }} ) without any hand requirement. Upon release, it was nerfed to only provide +1 Mult for balancing purposes.
