# Cavendish

1 in 1000 chance this card is destroyed at the end of round (<br> ) has gone "extinct" during the current run. Copyable Perishable Eternal

Cavendish is a Common Multiplicative Mult Joker that provides an immediate and unconditional X3 Mult to all hands played. With many other similar cards having an activation requirement or warranting scaling, this Mult bonus makes Cavendish among the best in its class, arguably the best Common Joker, and potentially one of the best in the game.

Obtaining this joker is not particularly straightforward, however: in order for Cavendish to appear anywhere, Gros Michel (<br> ) needs to destroy itself through its own effect first during the same run. It also comes with a minuscule, but potentially devastating risk inherited from its more fragile ancestor, in the form of a 1 in 1000 chance to destroy itself at the end of the round (denoted by the word "Extinct!" displaying as it vanishes), which can deprive the player of most of their scoring power. While it can potentially be rediscovered more easily, this is hard to guarantee unless the player has a huge amount of money they can justify cutting into.

## Synergies

### Decks

This Joker works well with any of the Decks, as it gives a strong X3 Mult to every hand played. Since the chance of Cavendish destroying itself is only 0.1%, one can safely ignore this possibility with 99.9% confidence.

### Poker Hands

Any hand can benefit from this Joker for the same reasons that any of the Decks can, although a stronger hand with a higher base Mult will generally work better. One should focus on their most Levelled poker hands, if possible, and/or their most played hand while also using Supernova (Adds the number of times [[Poker Hands|]] ).

### Jokers

- Additive Mult Jokers capable of scaling really shine with the help of a Joker like this one. However, the ones dependent on playing repeated numbers of hands are less ideal due to its extreme and universal power. For this reason, Jokers that depend on non-hand actions, like Fortune Teller ( per [[Tarot Cards|]] card used this run <br> }} ) and Flash Card (This Joker gains per in [[The Shop|the shop]] <br> }} ), work exceptionally well.

- Copying Cavendish with Blueprint (Copies ability of [[Jokers|]] ) and Brainstorm (Copies the ability of leftmost [[Jokers|]] Discard a . ) does not increase the probability of self-destruction of Cavendish or cause the copying Joker to self-destruct, so this can be used without additional risks.

## Anti-Synergies

- Oops! This is still so small that it would only have an effect in 1 out of ~22.73 runs, assuming Gros Michel (<br> ) was obtained from the first Shop, broke after the first round, then both these Jokers were somehow obtained in the second Shop, both Hieroglyph ( Ante, <br> ) and Petroglyph ( Ante, <br> ) were taken at some point in the run, and that no Blinds were skipped. To guarantee that Cavendish gets destroyed, the player would need 10 copies of Oops! ## Trivia

- The disease-resistant Cavendish banana cultivar makes up the vast majority of bananas consumed around the world. It was introduced after the previous cultivar, the Gros Michel, was devastated by a fungal pandemic in the 1960s. A similar disaster could occur to wipe out the Cavendish bananas because they're vulnerable to Tropical Race 4, a relative of the Tropical Race 1 fungus that wiped out the Gros Michel, but more care and preventative measures are in place to stop this from reoccurring. This is represented by the Joker's powerful bonus, the highly unlikely chance of self-destruction, and the requirement for Gros Michel (<br> ) to first go extinct.

- The waves of energy radiating from the banana in Cavendish's art may be a reference to how bananas are very faintly radioactive.

- In the unlikely chance that Cavendish is destroyed, the text "Extinct!" will flash on the screen.

- Cavendish is one of the few Jokers to be found in a recently released pin collection by Makeship, including Jimbo and a The Wheel of Fortune ( chance to add [[Foil|]], [[Holographic|]], or [[Polychrome|]] edition to a random [[Jokers|]] Available from start. )'s "Nope!"1]

- This Joker is part of the group of "Consumable" Jokers that can be destroyed with usage, dubbed "Food Jokers" due to their shared theme. The other Jokers in this group are Ice Cream (<br> ), Popcorn ( <br> ), Gros Michel (<br> ), Egg (Gains of at end of round Common Economy N/A (Passive) ), Seltzer (), Turtle Bean ( hand size, reduces by each round Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ), Ramen (, ), and Diet Cola (Sell this card to create a free [[Tags|]] Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ).2]3]4]

- Cavendish's 1 in 1000 chance to destroy itself is the least likely probability-based event in the game.
