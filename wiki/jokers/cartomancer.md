# Cartomancer

is selected
 (Must have room) Copyable Perishable Eternal

Cartomancer is an Uncommon Effect Joker that creates a random Tarot each time a Blind is selected. The wide-ranging effects of Tarot Cards allow the player to easily manipulate their deck in a variety of directions, but this same variance can also make it slightly unreliable. Because of this, Cartomancer tends to be much stronger in the early game than later on.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Black Deck ( Joker slot <br> hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ): Its inherent +1 Joker slot allows Cartomancer to be used alongside 5 other scoring Jokers. Moreover, acquiring The Hermit (Doubles money<br>)}} Available from start. ) or Temperance (Gives the total sell value of all current [[Jokers]]<br> }} Available from start. ) early from this Joker's effect can help this Deck reach its ceiling sooner by overstepping its largest weakness: its poor early-game economy.

- Magic Deck ( Start run with the voucher and copies of Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ): This 52-stack of wizardry starts the player with the Crystal Ball ( ) Voucher, giving an extra consumable slot to hold more Tarot Cards. This serves several functions when combined with this Joker: scoring-oriented Tarot cards like Justice (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ) can be stored to save a run in a pinch; the starting copies of The Fool (Creates the last [[Tarot Cards|]] or [[Planet Cards|]] card used during this run<br><small> excluded</small> Available from start. ) will have easier access to more desirable targets, and potentially unwanted Tarot Cards can be stored to prevent them from reappearing elsewhere. Obtaining earlier access to the Omen Globe ( cards may appear in any of the [[Arcana Packs|]] ) Voucher can be of potential interest, too - by allowing Spectral Cards to appear in the Arcana Packs, they can be viewed as potential mini-Spectral Packs, as there would already be a reliable source of Tarot Cards.

### Poker Hands

Cartomancer does not synergize directly with any poker hand. It provides very slight favor to the 5-card hands, however, as they provide more opportunities for Enhancements (applied by Tarot Cards) to pump up the score.

### Jokers

The following Jokers have synergy with Cartomancer:

- Fortune Teller ( per [[Tarot Cards|]] card used this run <br> }} ): Perhaps the strongest synergy that either of these two have, it will gain +1 Mult for each Tarot Card used. Skipping as few Blinds as possible, while maximizing on Arcana Packs and copies of The Fool (Creates the last [[Tarot Cards|]] or [[Planet Cards|]] card used during this run<br><small> excluded</small> Available from start. ) and The Emperor (Creates up to random cards<br> Available from start. ), can easily shoot it up to +40 Mult or more. To strengthen the case, Fortune Teller scales retroactively, meaning that it will not be any weaker if Cartomancer is acquired first than if they were simultaneously.

- Jokers that scale based on hands/cards played are a good choice, as using this Joker means wanting to see as many Blinds as possible. These include Green Joker ( per hand played <br> per discard <br> }} ), Ride the Bus (This Joker gains per hand played without a scoring card <br> }} ), Runner (Gains if played hand contains a <br> }} ), Square Joker (This Joker gains if played hand has exactly cards <br> }} ), Supernova (Adds the number of times [[Poker Hands|]] ), Spare Trousers (This Joker gains if played hand contains a [[Poker Hands|]] <br> }} ), Wee Joker (This Joker gains when each played is scored <br> }} ), and Obelisk (This Joker gains per hand played without playing your most played <br> }} ).

- The Enhancement series Jokers ( Glass Joker (This Joker gains for every [[Glass_Cards|]] that is destroyed <br> }} Have at least [[Glass_Cards|]] ), Golden Ticket (Played [[Gold Cards|]] ), Lucky Cat (This Joker gains every time a [[Lucky Cards|]] card triggers <br> }} ), Steel Joker (Gives for each [[Steel Cards|]] in your <br> }} ), and Stone Joker (Gives for each [[Stone Card|]] in your <br> }} )) all become more accessible while using this Joker. If focusing on a specific type of Enhancement, Cartomancer can more easily balloon their high power by finding the relevant Tarot Card, as well as copies of The Fool (Creates the last [[Tarot Cards|]] or [[Planet Cards|]] card used during this run<br><small> excluded</small> Available from start. ). Of these, Lucky Cat (This Joker gains every time a [[Lucky Cards|]] card triggers <br> }} ) is perhaps the most synergistic, as more Blinds played means more Tarot Cards created, and also more chances to trigger Lucky Cards.

- Blueprint (Copies ability of [[Jokers|]] ) and Brainstorm (Copies the ability of leftmost [[Jokers|]] Discard a . ): These two can copy Cartomancer's effect at the start of a Blind, meaning 2 or possibly even 3 unique Tarot Cards to choose from, or at the very worst, a bit of extra money for the next Shop. The Joker lineup can also be rearranged immediately afterward to assist with scoring.

- Campfire (This Joker gains for each card , resets when [[Blinds|]] is defeated <br> }} ): In the event the Tarot Cards are unneeded, this Joker can provide an X0.75 Mult boost to Campfire on each Ante, which can shoot even higher if The Emperor (Creates up to random cards<br> Available from start. ) or The High Priestess (Creates up to random [[Planet Cards|]] cards<br> Available from start. ) are generated.

- Driver's License ( if you have at least Enhanced cards in your full deck <br> }} Enhance 16 cards in your deck Rare Multiplicative Mult Independent Driver') and Vampire (This Joker gains per scoring played, removes card <br> }} ) are the two best jokers for converting all of the enhanced cards that Cartomancer has made into a powerful endgame strategy.

- Perkeo (Creates a [[Negative|]] copy of random [[Consumables|]] card in your possession at the end of the [[The Shop|]] Find this [[Jokers|Joker]] from the [[Spectral Cards|]] card. Legendary Effect N/A (Passive) ): An exceedingly rare Legendary Joker that creates a Negative copy of a random held consumable at the end of the Shop. This essentially doubles the effect of Cartomancer if it creates an ideal Tarot Card, which can be used in bulk when the time comes to more or less mold the deck into an ideal build. Alternatively, should their effects not be needed, this duo can be a source of incredible economy due to the higher sell value of the duplicated cards.

### Vouchers

- The aforementioned Crystal Ball ( ) pairs very nicely with this Joker, for all the same reasons (run-saving, ease of deck fixing, and Shop RNG manipulation).

- Hieroglyph ( Ante, <br> ) and its upgraded counterpart Petroglyph ( Ante, <br> ) allow the player to see up to 3 additional Blinds each, which means even more Tarot Cards to enhance Deck quality. These should be avoided when using the Black Deck ( Joker slot <br> hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ), however, as having only 2 hands and, in the case of Petroglyph, 2 discards every round (only 1 on Blue Stake and above), is a very risky position unless the other held Jokers and poker hand levels are contributing enough raw score.

## Anti-synergies

### Decks

- Nebula Deck ( Start run with the voucher <br> consumable slot Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] )'s -1 consumable slot can make it harder to hold and use Tarot Cards from Cartomancer.

- Anaglyph Deck ( After defeating each [[Blinds|]], gain a [[File:|24px]] [[Tags|]] Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ): With emphasis placed on skipping Blinds for duplication of powerful Tags, it has slight anti-synergy with this Joker. It can still be very useful early on, however, especially in the absence of particularly strong Tags.

### Jokers

- Throwback ( for each [[Blinds|]] skipped this run <br> }} ): A Joker that encourages skipping Blinds. Cartomancer will have fewer opportunities to create Tarot Cards if scaling Throwback is more of a priority.

## Trivia

- This Joker's art is stylised to mimic a Tarot Card, mirroring Astronomer (All cards and in the shop are Discover every card Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ), which has comparable unlock requirements to Cartomancer and is stylised to mimic a Planet Card.
