# Steel Joker

in your full deck
 (Currently X1 Mult) Copyable Perishable Eternal
Steel Joker is an Uncommon Multiplicative Mult Joker that gives X0.2 Mult for each Steel Card ( ) in your full deck. This Joker can only appear if you have at least one Steel Card in your full deck.

It can be a top-tier multiplier card, assuming you can put lots of Steel cards into your deck. The combination of Steel cards in hand plus the high multiplier of Steel Joker can easily create high scoring hands. Getting Steel cards into your deck is the biggest challenge with this Joker. You will need to invest heavily in booster packs.

## Synergies

To synergize with the Steel Joker, there are two approaches: one is to maximize the number of Steel cards in the deck, and the second is to maximize the scoring potential of the Steel cards that will be in your hand. In general, Steel cards benefit from having a high base multiplier after scoring the cards, since Steel cards act before any Jokers. As such, planet cards and Jokers that add multiplier upon scoring cards can help boost scores.

### Decks

Steel Joker works well with all decks, but Steel Joker is particularly well suited to the Plasma Deck ( Balance [[Chips|]] and [[Mult|]] when calculating score for played hand <br> base Blind size Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ). Since the combination of multipliers between Steel Joker and Steel cards can significantly boost mult well beyond any possible chip combinations, Plasma helps turn that high mult into a much higher score.

### Poker Hands

Steel cards tends to work best with smaller hand types (high card, pair, etc.). This allows you to keep more cards in hand, capitalizing on the many Steel cards in the deck.

### Jokers

There are only a few good direct synergies with Steel Joker:

- DNA (If of round has only card, ) is very helpful, as it can be used to duplicate Steel cards each round.

- Mime () retriggers all "in hand" cards, which should include many Steel cards.

- Tarot creating Jokers such as Vagabond (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] ), Cartomancer (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when [[Blinds|]] is selected <br> Discover every Tarot Card. ) or Hallucination ( chance to create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when any [[Booster Packs|]] is opened <br> Common Effect N/A (Passive) ) can create some of the tarot cards described in the Consumables section below.

Some other potential synergies include:

- Burnt Joker (Upgrade the level of the first poker hand each round Sell 50 cards. ) will upgrade the level of the first discarded Poker hand. This allows you to easily level up a hand without buying planet cards, increasing the base multiplier. This can be useful if you're going for a High Card build.

- Any + Mult joker that triggers before the steel multipliers, such as Fibonacci (Each played , , , , ), Onyx Agate () or Smiley Face () can help with scoring when not having a lot of Steel cards yet.

### Consumables

Steel Joker requires lots of Steel cards to maximize its value. Use Tarot and Spectral cards to boost the amount of Steel cards you have:

- The Chariot (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ) enhances one card into a Steel card.

- Death (Select cards, convert the card into the card<br> Available from start. ) can be used to duplicate a Steel card.

- Familiar (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ), Grim (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Aces|]] to your hand Available from start. ), and Incantation (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card_Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Number cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ) all have a chance to create Steel cards but might destroy a Steel card in the process.

- Cryptid (Create copies of selected card in your hand Available from start. ) can be used to create copies of a Steel card.

- All Playing Card Packs have a chance to contain Steel cards.

Apart from turning cards into steel, there are other consumable cards that pair well with the Steel enhancement:

- Deja Vu (Add a [[Red Seal|]] to selected card in your hand Available from start. ) can add a red seal to a Steel card. This will retrigger the card, adding another X1.5 Mult to the multiplier.

### Vouchers

- Illusion ( in shop may have an [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]], [[Card Modifiers#Edition|]], and/or a [[Card Modifiers#Seal|]] ) can have a chance for Steel Cards to appear in the shop, which can be bought to scale Steel Joker. This can be further increased with help from Overstock ( ) and Overstock Plus ( ) to increase the shop selection, plus Reroll Surplus () and Reroll Glut () to make rerolling cheaper.

## Trivia

- Steel Joker's image is identical to the base Joker ( ), but with a Steel card skin applied. Unlike playing cards, which retain their color when steeled, Steel Joker is entirely grayscale.

- This Joker is part of a series, along with Stone Joker (Gives for each [[Stone Card|]] in your <br> }} ), Glass Joker (This Joker gains for every [[Glass_Cards|]] that is destroyed <br> }} Have at least [[Glass_Cards|]] ), Golden Ticket (Played [[Gold Cards|]] ) and Lucky Cat (This Joker gains every time a [[Lucky Cards|]] card triggers <br> }} ), where the Joker only appears once players have at least one card with the relevant enhancement in their deck.
