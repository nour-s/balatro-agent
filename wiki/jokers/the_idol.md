# The Idol

gives X2 Mult when scored
Card changes every round Copyable Perishable Eternal

The Idol is an Uncommon Multiplicative Mult Joker that provides X2 Mult when a card with a chosen rank and suit is scored. For example, the card may be an Ace of  Spades, or 7 of  Diamonds. The chosen card is decided at the end of each round by selecting a random card from your deck.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Abandoned Deck ( Start run with no [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] in your deck Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) and Mad World ( Extra no longer earn money<br>Earn no at end of round Eternal, Negative <br>Eternal Ranks 2 through 9 only, 32 cards totalBanned Blinds: ) can make it easier to manipulate the deck into one type of card due to the reduced number of starting cards.

- Zodiac Deck ( Start run with , , and Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) starts with Tarot Merchant ( ), which can be helpful to manipulate the deck in your favor using Tarot Cards.

- Checkered Deck ( ) starts with 2 of every card rather than 1, making The Idol slightly more consistent to activate in the early game.

### Poker Hands

- Flush Five works exceptionally well with The Idol because it can trigger the effect 5 times, plus any additional retriggers from Jokers or Red Seal ().

- If using The Idol, it's best to play a Flush Five as soon as possible in order to unlock its respective Planet Card, Eris ().

- In the early to midgame, one can make do with Three of a Kind and Four of a Kind, assuming the correct card is chosen by The Idol. Due to the highly random nature of the card, you will likely want more reliable sources of score before you can fully utilize it. That said, using this Joker in the early to midgame is nearly impossible.

### Jokers

- Jokers that retrigger scored cards, such as Dusk () or Hack (Retrigger each played , , , ), will compound the multiplication immensely.

- Jokers that help manipulate the deck to fewer types of cards will make the randomly chosen card more likely to be desirable. DNA (If of round has only card, ) or Trading Card (If of round has only card, ) are the most important jokers for accomplishing this.

- Smeared Joker ( and count as the same suit, and count as the same suit Have at least [[Wild Cards|]] in your deck Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) effectively halves the number of unique cards in any given deck (aside from Checkered Deck ( )) and may be worth considering on some runs.

### Consumables

#### Tarot Cards

- The Hanged Man (Destroys up to selected cards Available from start. ) and Death (Select cards, convert the card into the card<br> Available from start. ) can be used to remove undesired card(s), making The Idol more consistent. Thus, Vagabond (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] ) can be one of the best ways to enable The Idol.

- The Lovers (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ) can generate Wild cards, which can trigger The Idol.

- The Tower (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ) can also be used to make The Idol more consistent, as The Idol cannot choose Stone Cards (<br>) to buff.

#### Spectral Cards

- Immolate (Destroys random cards in hand, gain Available from start. ) will destroy 5 random cards in hand, making it easier to get The Idol trigger.

## Anti-Synergies

### Jokers

- Hologram (This Joker gains every time a is added to your deck <br> }} ) requires you to add cards to your deck for joker scaling, making this Joker more inconsistent in scoring.

## Strategies

- If you have more of one card type than another, it will be proportionally likely for that card type to be chosen.

- While it can be underwhelming early game, it excels late game when the proper deck manipulation can be put into place. A Flush Five containing five of The Idol's target card yields X32 Mult, retriggers notwithstanding. However, it is one of the more difficult Jokers to extract the most use out of.

- The best cards to build around are those that can be retriggered more times by other Jokers. 2's, 3's, 4's, and 5's can be retriggered by Hack (Retrigger each played , , , ), while face cards (J, Q, K) can be retriggered by Sock and Buskin ().

## Trivia

- The card art depicts a colorful wood carving of a head, also known as a tiki idol.

- If the deck has no card with any rank and suit (i.e., full Stone Cards (<br>)), The Idol will default to X2 Mult per played Ace of  Spades.

- This Joker shares its unlock requirement with the "1,000K" Achievement.
