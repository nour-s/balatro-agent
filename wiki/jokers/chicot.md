# Chicot

Copyable Perishable Eternal

Chicot is one of the five Legendary Jokers that can be generated from using The Soul (Creates a [[Legendary Jokers|]] Joker <br> Available from start. ) Spectral card, which in itself has a 3 in 1000 (0.3%) chance to replace any given card in a Spectral Pack or Arcana Pack.

Chicot eliminates all Boss Blind effects, reducing their required win conditions to 2x base chips (besides The Needle ( ), which won't have its score increased).

## Synergies

### Decks

- Black Deck ( Joker slot <br> hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ) has +1 Joker slot, which leaves more room for scoring Jokers.

### Jokers

Boss Blinds can affect almost every Joker in the game, and thus be helped by Chicot, but many Jokers have notable weaknesses to particular Boss Blinds. Chicot can negate the holes in these Jokers' defenses:

- Photograph (), Smiley Face (), and other Face Card Jokers no longer fear The Plant ( ).

- Bloodstone ( ), Seeing Double ( if played hand has a scoring card and a scoring card of any other suit Play a hand that contains <abbr title="Other suits that count as clubs (e.g. wild suits) with rank 7 will also count."></abbr> ), and other Card Suit Jokers can ignore their respective Boss Blinds.

 - In particular, Flower Pot ( if poker hand contains a card, card, card, ) and Ancient Joker (Each played card with [[Card Suits|]] gives when scored, <br> <small>suit changes at end of round</small> ) can safely rely on Wild Cards with Chicot.

- Card Sharp ( ), Supernova (Adds the number of times [[Poker Hands|]] ), and other Jokers limited by Poker Hand-limiting Boss Blinds.

### Boss Blinds

Disabling a boss blind reverses its effect, and due to the timing involved, multiple copies of Chicot () will all disable the Boss Blind, resulting in a net positive effect:

- The Wall ( ): Score requirement is reduced by a factor of 2 for each copy of Chicot.

- The Water ( ): Gains the number of discards originally as extra discards for each copy of Chicot.

- The Manacle ( ): +1 extra Hand size for each copy of Chicot (permanently).

 - This can be useful for using Ectoplasm (Add [[Card Modifiers#Editions|]] to a random hand size Available from start. ) more times in the same run than usually possible.

- The Needle ( ): Gains (the number of hands originally - 1) extra hands for each copy of Chicot.

- Violet Vessel ( ): Score is reduced by a factor of 3 for each copy of Chicot.

This interaction may actually be a bug, and might be patched in the future.

### Vouchers

Because of its distinctive effect as a utility Joker, Chicot normally does not benefit from any particular voucher. However, if the player has multiple copies of Chicot, Director's Cut (Reroll [[Blinds|Boss Blind]] time per Ante, per roll Base Director') and Retcon (Reroll [[Blinds|Boss Blind]] times, ) can be considered for the positive effects mentioned above.

## Anti-Synergies

### Decks

- Painted Deck ( hand size, <br> Joker slot Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ), Blast Off ( hands per round<br> discards per round<br> Joker Slots Eternal <br>Eternal <br><br>Banned [[Vouchers]]: , <br>Banned Jokers: ) and Cruelty ( give no reward money<br> give no reward money<br><br> Joker Slots ) penalize holding non-scoring Jokers like Chicot with their reduced Joker slots.

### Jokers

- As Luchador (Sell this card to disable the current [[Blinds|]] Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) also disable Boss blinds, functionally overlapping with Chicot, Luchador becomes a redundant pickup.

- Matador () also does not work, as it activates based on Boss Blind effects.

### Vouchers

- The Director's Cut (Reroll [[Blinds|Boss Blind]] time per Ante, per roll Base Director') and Retcon (Reroll [[Blinds|Boss Blind]] times, ) vouchers are usually made obsolete with the possession of Chicot, since Boss Blind effects are no longer a concern.

### Blinds

- While normally it's not too big of a problem, do note that Amber Acorn ( ) shuffles Jokers before Chicot disables it, which can be annoying sometimes.

## Trivia

- Chicot (c. 1540–1591), real name Jean-Antoine d'Anglerais, was the jester of King Henry III of France, and later Henry IV. He was sharp-tongued, very cunning, spoke with the king without formalities, and often participated in battle. Chicot disabling Boss Blinds in the game is likely a reference to this.

- This Joker is animated, with Chicot hovering just above the red card face.
