# Astronomer

Copyable Perishable Eternal

Astronomer is an Uncommon Effect-type Joker that sets the cost of all Planet Cards and Celestial Packs to $0, making them free to purchase.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Zodiac Deck ( Start run with , , and Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) works best with Astronomer thanks to the increased Planet card frequency in the shop.

- Nebula Deck ( Start run with the voucher <br> consumable slot Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) makes Celestial Packs always give a planet card of your most played hand, which may make the free Celestial Packs more useful.

- Blast Off ( hands per round<br> discards per round<br> Joker Slots Eternal <br>Eternal <br><br>Banned [[Vouchers]]: , <br>Banned Jokers: ) starts with an Eternal Constellation (This Joker gains every time a [[Planet_Cards|]] card is used <br> }} ) and Planet Merchant ( ) and Planet Tycoon ([[Planet Cards|]] ) vouchers, which Astronomer can help proc the given Constellation, and is doubly helpful with the provided Planet Merchant and Planet Tycoon vouchers.

### Poker Hands

Any poker hand can benefit from Astronomer.

### Jokers

- Constellation (This Joker gains every time a [[Planet_Cards|]] card is used <br> }} ): The free planet cards and packs allow players to buff this Joker up very fast.

- Satellite (Earn at end of round per unique card used this run Have at least . Uncommon Economy N/A (Passive) ): The free planet cards and packs allow it to reach its cap faster.

- Campfire (This Joker gains for each card , resets when [[Blinds|]] is defeated <br> }} ): Buying and selling Planet Cards will gain you some money while also scaling Campfire.

- Red Card (This Joker gains when any [[Booster Packs|]] is skipped <br> }} ): Since celestial packs are now completely free, the player can get it scaled up quickly by buying and skipping packs without hurting their economy.

- Baseball Card ([[Uncommon Jokers|]] ): With Astronomer being an uncommon Joker, it benefits from Baseball Card, as it will now provide X1.5 Mult.

### Vouchers

- Planet Merchant ( ) and Planet Tycoon ([[Planet Cards|]] ) make Planet Cards appear more often.

- Telescope ([[Booster Packs|]] always contain the [[Planet Cards|]] card for your most played [[Poker Hands|]] ) guarantees a specific planet in every Celestial Pack, making Astronomer more useful to keep in return.

- Overstock ( ), Overstock Plus ( ), Reroll Surplus (), and Reroll Glut () all allow the player to see more cards (and thus more Planet Cards) in the shop.

## Anti-Synergies

- The High Priestess (Creates up to random [[Planet Cards|]] cards<br> Available from start. ): Although planet cards and celestial packs are free, the High Priestess isn't, making the Tarot card less valuable.

## Strategies

- As planet cards still sell for $1, Astronomer can be used to make money by buying and selling all planet cards instead of using them.

## Trivia

- During the first playtest build, Astronomer only provided free Planet Cards in the shop. Upon release, it was buffed to provide free planet cards and free Celestial Packs.

- This joker's art is stylised to mimic a Planet card, mirroring Cartomancer (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when [[Blinds|]] is selected <br> Discover every Tarot Card. ), which has comparable unlock requirements to Astronomer and is stylised to mimic a Tarot card.
