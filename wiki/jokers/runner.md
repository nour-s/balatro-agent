# Runner

(Currently +0 Chips) Copyable Perishable Eternal

Runner is a Common scaling Chips Joker that adds Chips to played hands. The amount of Chips added begins at +0 and is permanently increased by +15 when the player plays a hand that contains a Straight (including Straight Flush and Royal Flush).

## Trivia

- This is the only Chip Joker that scales by playing a hand of a specific type; in this case, Straights (which can include Straight Flushes). Its counterpart, Spare Trousers (This Joker gains if played hand contains a [[Poker Hands|]] <br> }} ), scale its +Mult by playing hands that contain Two Pairs.

- The runner's ID number, #345, is itself a (short) Straight.

- The name is a reference to how in some games, straights are called "runs".
