# Reserved Parking

Copyable Perishable Eternal

Reserved Parking is a Common Economy Joker that has a 1 in 2 chance to provide $1 for each Face Card held in hand after a hand is played. Cards which are discarded or debuffed by certain Blinds do not trigger this effect.

## Usage Notes

Reserved Parking rolls a 1 in 2 on each face card held in hand, so the expected value per face card per hand is $0.5. Mime () and Red Seals () will only attempt to retrigger this effect if the initial roll was successful. As a result, one retrigger will raise the expected value to $0.75 per card per hand, and two retriggers raises the expected value to $1.

If the player also already has another held in hand effect that triggers on every hand, like Baron (), Shoot the Moon (Each held in hand gives Play every Heart card in your deck in one round. ) or Steel Cards ( ), then Mime () and Red Seals () will always retrigger this effect, and therefore this card. As a result, Reserved Parking can be retriggered even if the initial roll was unsuccessful. In this case, one retrigger raises expected value to $1 per hard, two retriggers raises expected value to $1.5.

## Trivia

- This Joker was cut from the second Demo, being only present in a specialized version for the Jimbo Invitational Tournament. Instead of the 1 in 2 chance, it's guaranteed to give $1 per face cards held in hand.

- The artwork for this Joker seems to be inspired by this sign, or one similar to it.

- This Joker's sprite art has the least amount of unique colors out of every Joker, having a total of two unique colors.

- Reserved parking is a parking space that is reserved for someone important or to people who bought a special pass. An unpermitted person using a reserved parking space usually results in their vehicle being towed and them being given a ticket. This is reflected by the Joker's ability to give every face card held in hand a 1 in 2 chance to grant $1, as if the cards are being fined for taking up space.
