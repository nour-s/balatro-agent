# Sixth Sense

card
 (Must have room) Copyable Perishable Eternal

Sixth Sense is an Uncommon Effect Joker that will provide the player with Spectral Cards, as long as it is fed with a 6 as the only played card in the first hand of a round. It activates after all scoring is completed and therefore still works if the 6 is debuffed, and does not get influenced by retriggers. It does not activate if the hand is not allowed under the current Boss Blind (e.g. The Psychic).

Sixth Sense cannot give the Spectral Cards The Soul (Creates a [[Legendary Jokers|]] Joker <br> Available from start. ) or Black Hole (Upgrade every [[Poker Hands|]] by level Available from start. ), as both can only be found in Booster Packs.

## Strategy

As Sixth Sense requires having 6s in the deck to activate, it has limited use as the player will likely destroy all 6s in the Deck before reaching Ante 8. However, this is usually ideal, as 6s do not have any particularly strong synergies with other Jokers, and the Spectral Card given may help further enhance the player's deck and hasten the Deck manipulation process. In general, Spectral Cards to look out for are:

- Any of the Seal-adding Cards.

 - Deja Vu (Add a [[Red Seal|]] to selected card in your hand Available from start. ), Trance (Add a [[Blue Seal|]] to selected card in your hand Available from start. ), Medium (Add a [[Purple Seal|]] to selected card in your hand Available from start. ), and Talisman (Add a [[Gold Seal|]] to selected card in your hand Available from start. ).

- Aura (Add [[Foil|]], [[Holographic|]], or [[Polychrome|]] effect to selected card in hand Available from start. ) for farming the Polychrome ( Edition Joker (Polychrome).png) Edition.

- Cryptid (Create copies of selected card in your hand Available from start. ) to make copies of a useful card, which can be used in conjunction with the above Spectral Cards.

- Immolate (Destroys random cards in hand, gain Available from start. ) to further hasten Deck manipulation and for a boost in Money.

- Ectoplasm (Add [[Card Modifiers#Editions|]] to a random hand size Available from start. ) for when the player has their final Joker lineup.

Any other Spectral Card is still worth keeping so as to prevent it from appearing in the shop, either from a Spectral Booster Pack or from playing on the Ghost Deck ( [[Spectral Cards|]] cards may appear in the shop, start with a card Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ), as they are either too niche or hard to make use of. In the event that the player finds Sixth Sense in the Shop but the Deck contains no or few 6s, it could be beneficial to open Standard Card Packs to directly add 6s for later destruction.

### Decks

- Red Deck ( ) gives +1 Discard which makes it easier to find a 6.

- Blue Deck ( hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ) gives +1 Hand which allows the player more freedom to utilize Sixth Sense, as there are more remaining Hands to defeat the Blind.

- Abandoned Deck ( Start run with no [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] in your deck Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) and Mad World ( Extra no longer earn money<br>Earn no at end of round Eternal, Negative <br>Eternal Ranks 2 through 9 only, 32 cards totalBanned Blinds: ) increase the chance to draw a 6 as they have less overall cards.

- Erratic Deck ( All [[Card Ranks|]] and [[Suits|]] in deck are randomized Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) has a chance to provide more 6s to destroy, increasing Sixth Sense's value.

### Poker Hands

- High Card is the only poker hand directly relevant to Sixth Sense.

### Jokers

- Perkeo (Creates a [[Negative|]] copy of random [[Consumables|]] card in your possession at the end of the [[The Shop|]] Find this [[Jokers|Joker]] from the [[Spectral Cards|]] card. Legendary Effect N/A (Passive) ) can copy any Spectral Card created by Sixth Sense.

- DNA (If of round has only card, ) can create copies of playing cards, allowing the player to infinitely farm Spectral Cards as long as the Deck contains at least one 6. The order of the two makes no difference—If DNA is on the right, the 6 will still be replicated after being destroyed.

- Erosion ( for each card below in your full deck <br> }} ) will also scale after Sixth Sense destroys 6s.

### Consumables

- Strength (Increases rank of up to selected cards by Available from start. ) can be used to turn 5s (and possibly 4s) into 6s to be destroyed and converted into Spectral cards. This also helps remove cards in order to shape the deck.

- Death (Select cards, convert the card into the card<br> Available from start. ) can turn some cards into 6s to be destroyed and converted into Spectral cards.

### Vouchers

- Grabber () and Nacho Tong (Permanently gain an additional hand per round Play a total of 2,) both give additional hands to play, which can be used to trigger Sixth Sense with fewer consequences.

- Wasteful () and Recyclomancy () both give additional discards, which can help in finding 6s.

### Blinds

- Any blind that disables cards in deck (like The Club ( ), The Pillar ( ), etc.) will not prevent Sixth Sense from triggering.

## Anti-Synergies

### Decks

- Black Deck ( Joker slot <br> hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ), Blast Off ( hands per round<br> discards per round<br> Joker Slots Eternal <br>Eternal <br><br>Banned [[Vouchers]]: , <br>Banned Jokers: ), and Golden Needle ( Discards each cost <br><br> hands per round<br> discards per round<br>Start with Banned Jokers: <br>Banned [[Vouchers]]: , ) all reduce the number of hands to play, which can discourage using Sixth Sense while a player's hands aren't strong enough.

### Jokers

- Obtaining a second copy of Sixth Sense () does not stack with itself, creating only 1 Spectral Card when destroying 6s.

- While Burglar (When [[Blind|]] is selected, ) gives more hands to play with, it also removes all discards. If the first hand of the round does not contain a 6, Sixth Sense will miss its effect.

- Stuntman (,<br> hand size Earn at least 100 million (100,000,000) Chips in a single hand. ) reduces hand size, which makes it harder to find a 6.

### Blinds

- The Psychic ( ) forces the player to play a 5-card hand, meaning Sixth Sense cannot activate.

- The Needle ( ) sets the player's hands to 1. Therefore, to still benefit from Sixth Sense, the player would have to beat the blind with nothing but a single High Card.

- The Mouth ( ) only allows 1 hand type, so unless players build around High Cards, they must play a winning hand type first, missing Sixth Sense's effect.

- If Crimson Heart ( ) debuffs Sixth Sense on the first hand, it cannot activate, missing its effect.

### Stakes

- On Blue Stake difficulty or higher, there is a -1 Discard penalty which can make it harder to find a 6.

## Trivia

- A sixth sense, also known as extrasensory perception, is the power of perception beyond the five senses of sight, hearing, touch, smell, or taste.

- In early demo versions, the effect of Sixth Sense was "Each played 6 permanently gains +6 Chips when scored". This is changed in later versions.
