# Square Joker

(Currently 0 Chips) Copyable Perishable Eternal

Square Joker is a Common scaling Chips Joker that adds Chips to played hands. The amount of Chips added begins at +0 and is permanently increased by +4 when the player plays a hand that contains only four cards. The four cards played do not have to score, and all four can be debuffed.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Plasma Deck ( Balance [[Chips|]] and [[Mult|]] when calculating score for played hand <br> base Blind size Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ): Due to the balancing feature, Square Joker can be very beneficial if picked up early and scaled adequately throughout the run.

- Blue Deck ( hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ): Having one extra hand allows more scaling for Square Joker.

### Poker Hands

The following hands are made up of no more than four cards, and thus can scale Square Joker:

- Four of a Kind

- Three of a Kind

- Two Pair

- Pair

- High Card

Hands that require fewer than four cards can be padded with non-scoring cards to scale anyway.

Flushes, Straights, and Straight Flushes can synergize with this Joker if Four Fingers (All and can be made with 4 cards Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) is also present.

### Jokers

- Four Fingers (All and can be made with 4 cards Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ): Allows Straights and Flushes to be made with only four cards.

- Splash (Every counts in scoring Common Effect N/A (Passive) ): Allows High Cards to score with all 4 cards, providing more bonus.

- Spare Trousers (This Joker gains if played hand contains a [[Poker Hands|]] <br> }} ): Requires Two Pairs hand to scale, which is exactly 4 cards.

- Burglar (When [[Blind|]] is selected, ): Provides three extra hands, allowing Square Joker to scale more.

- Other scaling Additive mult Joker based on hands, like Green Joker ( per hand played <br> per discard <br> }} ), Ride the Bus (This Joker gains per hand played without a scoring card <br> }} ), or Supernova (Adds the number of times [[Poker Hands|]] ), can be used alongside with Square Joker.

### Voucher

- Grabber () and Nacho Tong (Permanently gain an additional hand per round Play a total of 2,) give additional hands per round, allowing Square Joker to scale even higher.

### Boss Blinds

- This Joker is easily distinguishable when played against Amber Acorn ( ), as it retains its unique shape even when flipped face-down.

In general, the only time Square Joker will not scale or score is when a hand is not allowed. Boss Blinds that can prevent specific hands are:

- The Eye ( ): No repeat hand types this round

- The Mouth ( ): Only one hand type can be played this round

- The Psychic ( ): Must play 5 cards

### Deck

- Black Deck ( Joker slot <br> hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ), Blast Off ( hands per round<br> discards per round<br> Joker Slots Eternal <br>Eternal <br><br>Banned [[Vouchers]]: , <br>Banned Jokers: ), and Golden Needle ( Discards each cost <br><br> hands per round<br> discards per round<br>Start with Banned Jokers: <br>Banned [[Vouchers]]: , ) all have poor synergy with Square Joker due to their reduced number of hands.

### Poker Hands

The following hands are made up of five cards, and thus cannot scale Square Joker:

- Straight*

- Flush*

- Full House

- Straight Flush (and Royal Flush)*

- Five of a Kind

- Flush House

- Flush Five

*Unless holding Four Fingers (All and can be made with 4 cards Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ).

### Boss Blind

- The Psychic ( ) forces the player to play five cards, which means that Square Joker cannot scale.

## Trivia

- Old version of Square JokerThe artwork of this Joker is an exaggerated version of pixel art, which is slightly ironic considering all the artwork in this game is in that style. Even the "Joker" text on the card has been squared off, with chunky letters.

- The value 4 is a square number, which are numbers made by multiplying one number with itself. 4 is 2, or 2×2.

- The Joker's sprite is also exactly a square, being 138x138 pixels.

- This Joker is part of a set that doesn't adhere to the standard joker size, along with Wee Joker (This Joker gains when each played is scored <br> }} ), Photograph (), and Half Joker ( ).

- In early versions, Square Joker had the same size as other Jokers. This was changed in 1.0.1f.

 - The Steam Trading Card of Square Joker still retains its old shape.1]
