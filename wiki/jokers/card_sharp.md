# Card Sharp

has already been played this round Copyable Perishable Eternal
Card Sharp is an Uncommon Multiplicative Mult Joker that provides X3 Mult when a hand is played again during the same round.

## Usage Notes

- Card Sharp only applies to the scoring hand, not the hands that it contains. For example, playing a Straight Flush after a Flush or Straight does not give X3 Mult.

- The Five-Card Draw ( discards per round<br> hand size<br> Joker slots <br>Banned Jokers: , , ) challenge starts the player with a copy of Card Sharp.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Checkered Deck ( ): Start run with 26  Spades and 26  Hearts, making repeated flushes easier.

- Blue Deck ( hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ): +1 hand per round, allowing more chances to play repeated hands.

- Red Deck ( ): +1 discard, allowing more chance to assemble desired hands.

### Poker Hands

Strong 5-card hands like Flush, Straight, Full House, etc... benefit the most from Card Sharp, provided they can be played at least twice per blind.

Players may opt to buff weaker hands like High Card, Pair, Two Pair, etc... with Planet Cards to make their base Mult stronger, allowing repeated play for each blind to score larger.

### Jokers

- Burglar (When [[Blind|]] is selected, ): +3 hands, allowing more chances to play repeated hands.

- Juggler ( hand size Common Effect N/A (Passive) ): +1 hand size, allowing strong hands to be assembled more easily.

- Drunkard ( discard each round Common Effect N/A (Passive) ): +1 discard, allowing more chances to assemble desired hands.

### Vouchers

- Grabber () and Nacho Tong (Permanently gain an additional hand per round Play a total of 2,) both give additional hands, giving the player more chances to play the same hand.

- Wasteful () and Recyclomancy () both give additional discards, allowing the player more opportunities to assemble the desired hand.

### Boss Blinds

- The Needle ( ): This boss hard counters Card Sharp as it only allows 1 hand to be played. Burglar (When [[Blind|]] is selected, ) is an effective safety against this, as discards are converted after the blind effect is applied.

- The Eye ( ): This boss also hard counters Card Sharp as it does not allow repeated hand types, making it impossible to play a second hand for the X3 Mult effect.

- The Arm ( ): Does not hard counter Card Sharp unlike the other two bosses, but if the player relies on the X3 Mult to win, they must decrease the level of the hand at least two times.

## Trivia

- Card sharps, sometimes known as card sharks, are people who use skill and/or deception to win poker games.

- The hoodie + sunglasses outfit derives from Phil Laak, a professional poker player nicknamed "the Unabomber" due to the outfit's resemblance to police sketches of Ted Kaczynski. Laak's use of this outfit popularized it among poker players in general; it is unclear whether LocalThunk was referencing Laak specifically or simply riffing on the outfit more broadly.
