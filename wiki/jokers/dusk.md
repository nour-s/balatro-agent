# Dusk

Copyable Perishable Eternal
Dusk is an Uncommon Retrigger Joker that triggers all played cards in the final hand twice, effectively doubling their scored chips and any potential enhancement they have, like Bonus ( ), Mult ( ), Foil ( Edition Joker (Foil).png), Holographic ( Edition Joker (Holographic).png), and Polychrome ( Edition Joker (Polychrome).png). Note that Dusk does not retrigger a Red Seal (). A card with a Red Seal will only activate three times: once for the initial trigger, once for the Red Seal, and then lastly, for Dusk.

### Poker Hands

Dusk works best with five card hands, such as Flushes and Straights, due to these giving Dusk more cards to retrigger, thus giving a higher score.

- Dusk synergizes well with Hack (Retrigger each played , , , ) when playing low-ranked cards, as they allow each card to be triggered three times.

- Similarly to Hack, Sock and Buskin () synergizes well with Dusk, as it allows face cards to be triggered more.

### Jokers

## Trivia

- Dusk is usually considered to be the time near the end of twilight, before night begins. There are technically three 'stages' of dusk and twilight, depending on the light levels being considered: civil dusk, nautical dusk, and astronomical dusk.

- The card art depicts saguaro cacti, which grow only in the Sonoran Desert, an area known for its brilliant sunsets. It is based on LocalThunk's tour in the Sonoran Desert. 1]
