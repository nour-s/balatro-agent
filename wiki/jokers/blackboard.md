# Blackboard

Spades or  Clubs Copyable Perishable Eternal

Blackboard is an Uncommon Multiplicative Mult Joker that provides a large X3 Mult boost when all drawn cards remaining (after playing a hand) are  Spades or  Clubs. The boost also activates when there are no cards left in hand.

Any Wild Cards () will also count as a  Spade/ Club. Stone Cards (<br>) have no suit and will prevent the effect from activating if left in hand.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Five-Card Draw ( discards per round<br> hand size<br> Joker slots <br>Banned Jokers: , , ) works well with Blackboard, as this deck only has a hand size of 5, making it easier to hold  Clubs or  Spades.

### Poker Hands

- Blackboard can potentially work in Flush builds if you can convert the entire deck into  Spades and  Clubs. However, having one  Heart/ Diamond card in hand will make Blackboard useless, making this strategy risky.

- Blackboard is extremely powerful for High Card and Pair builds. A small hand allows you to play leftover  Hearts and  Diamonds in your hand and easily trigger Blackboard.

### Modifiers

- Wild Cards () in hand will be treated as a  Club or  Spade by the Blackboard.

### Jokers

- Stuntman (,<br> hand size Earn at least 100 million (100,000,000) Chips in a single hand. ) greatly reduces hand size, significantly increasing the player's chances of their hand containing no  Diamond and  Heart cards.

- Similarly, Merry Andy ( discards each round, <br> hand size Win a run in or fewer rounds Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) reduces hand size and increases discards, making it easier to hold only  Spades or  Clubs.

### Spectral Packs

### Blinds

- The Manacle ( ) reduces hand size, making it easier to hold all  Spades and  Clubs.

- The Hook ( ) discards 2 cards held in hand, allowing a chance for Blackboard to trigger even if 1 or 2  Diamond or  Heart cards are held in hand.

### Consumables

- The Moon (Converts up to selected cards to Available from start. ) and The World (Converts up to selected cards to Available from start. ) can turn some  Hearts and  Diamonds into  Clubs or  Spades, allowing Blackboard to trigger easier.

- The Lovers (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ) can turn  Hearts or  Diamonds into Wild Cards (), which triggers Blackboard.

- The Hanged Man (Destroys up to selected cards Available from start. ) and Death (Select cards, convert the card into the card<br> Available from start. ) can help remove  Hearts and  Diamonds, which allows Blackboard to trigger easier.

## Anti-Synergies

### Decks

- Painted Deck ( hand size, <br> Joker slot Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] )'s +2 hand sizes makes it slightly harder to hold only  Clubs or  Spades in your hand to trigger Blackboard.

### Enhancements

- Stone Cards (<br>) have no suit and will prevent Blackboard from activating if left in hand.

### Jokers

- Troubadour ( hand size, <br> hand each round Win consecutive rounds by <abbr title="Discards are OK to use.">playing only 1 hand</abbr> Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ), Turtle Bean ( hand size, reduces by each round Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ), and Juggler ( hand size Common Effect N/A (Passive) ) have an anti-synergy with Blackboard, as the increased hand size provided by these Jokers makes it harder to hold only  Spades or  Clubs in your hand.

- Wrathful Joker (), Gluttonous Joker (), Arrowhead (), Onyx Agate () and Seeing Double ( if played hand has a scoring card and a scoring card of any other suit Play a hand that contains <abbr title="Other suits that count as clubs (e.g. wild suits) with rank 7 will also count."></abbr> ) all rely on playing  Spades or  Clubs, which reduce the probability of Blackboard to trigger.

- Notably, Mime () will not retrigger Blackboard, despite the ability being "held in hand".

### Blinds

- When playing against debuffing Boss Blinds, debuffed Wild Cards () revert to their original suit, debuffed Stone Cards (<br>) prevent Blackboard from activating, and debuffed base cards behave as normal. This means that e.g. a debuffed (wild) diamond card will prevent Blackboard from activating.

### Vouchers

- Paint Brush ( ) and Palette ( hand size Reduce your hand size down to five cards. ) have a slight anti-synergy with Blackboard, as the increased hand sizes provided by these vouchers make it harder to hold  Clubs or  Spades in your hand.

## Trivia

- The art is reminiscent of blackboards (sometimes called chalkboards) found in schools and other learning environments, including the piece of chalk used to draw and write on it. Despite the name, they are not always made of black material and can often be found in grey, green, or dark blue. In many situations, they have been replaced with whiteboards (also called dry-erase boards).
