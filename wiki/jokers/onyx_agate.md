# Onyx Agate

suit give +7 Mult when scored Copyable Perishable Eternal

Onyx Agate is an Uncommon Clubs-oriented Additive Mult Joker that gives +7 Mult per  Club card scored. For a  Club Flush, this adds up to a whopping +35 Mult, making Onyx Agate an easy choice for a source of Mult in the early to mid game, or if the player prefers playing Flush-type builds.

## Usage Notes

Unlike unlocking Arrowhead () and Bloodstone ( ), fulfilling the condition of 30 Club cards in a Deck is trickier, as it doesn't allow for an easy strategy such as using the Checkered Deck ( ) for the aforementioned Jokers.

- Magic Deck ( Start run with the voucher and copies of Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) starts the player with two copies of The Fool (Creates the last [[Tarot Cards|]] or [[Planet Cards|]] card used during this run<br><small> excluded</small> Available from start. ), allowing for easier Deck manipulation early on by finding The Moon (Converts up to selected cards to Available from start. ) or Death (Select cards, convert the card into the card<br> Available from start. ).

- Erratic Deck ( All [[Card Ranks|]] and [[Suits|]] in deck are randomized Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) may start the player with more Clubs than usual, making the unlock condition slightly easier.

- Any Jokers that synergize with Flush builds are likely to be useful during the unlock attempt, such as Droll Joker ( ), Crafty Joker ( ), and The Tribe ( if played hand contains a Win a run without playing a . ).

- Economy Jokers are likely to be beneficial to find more Tarots for Deck manipulation, such as Mail-In Rebate (Earn for each discarded [[Card Ranks|]], ) and Golden Joker (Earn at end of round Common Economy N/A (Passive) ).

## Synergies

### Decks

- The Erratic Deck ( All [[Card Ranks|]] and [[Suits|]] in deck are randomized Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) can benefit from Onyx Agate if it's randomized to have lots of Clubs.

### Poker Hands

Playing any Flush-type hand full of Clubs will benefit greatly from Onyx Agate, those being:

- Flush

- Straight Flush

- Flush House

- Flush Five

### Jokers

- Gluttonous Joker () also triggers when Club cards are played, though the bonus is strictly inferior.

- Smeared Joker ( and count as the same suit, and count as the same suit Have at least [[Wild Cards|]] in your deck Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) allows Spade suit to benefit from Onyx Agate as well.

 - In this context, Arrowhead () can be a substantial addition as the player will gain both Chips and Mult.

- Blackboard ( ) can be an easy addition, as if the player as the deck set up to have a lot of Clubs, Blackboard will benefit too.

- Retrigger Jokers work very well with Onyx Agate, as the extra triggers adds more Mult, on top of other card Enhancements that could benefit scoring power.

- For a Flush build, The Tribe ( if played hand contains a Win a run without playing a . ) will capitalize on Onyx Agate's Mult bonus.

- Photograph () can work reasonably well as long as the face card is placed to the far right, activating its XMult after Onyx Agate's Additive Mult.

### Enhancements

- Wild Cards () count as Clubs, which will activate Onyx Agate's Mult bonus.

- As Onyx Agate already provides a hefty Mult bonus, Bonus Cards ( ) fit quite nicely to double as a source of Chips.

- Glass Cards (<br> ) may be the most potent synergy as it directly synergizes with Onyx Agate's Additive Mult, however, there is always the risk of the Glass breaking.

 - This could potentially be capitalized on if the player obtains a Glass Joker (This Joker gains for every [[Glass_Cards|]] that is destroyed <br> }} Have at least [[Glass_Cards|]] ), which gains XMult on Glass breaking.

### Consumables

- The Moon (Converts up to selected cards to Available from start. ) can turn some non-Club cards into Club cards allowing the player to see Club cards easier.

- The Lovers (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ) can turn some non-Club cards into Wild Cards which trigger Onyx Agate.

- Death (Select cards, convert the card into the card<br> Available from start. ) can turn some non-Club cards into Club cards.

- The Hanged Man (Destroys up to selected cards Available from start. ) can remove some non-Club cards.

- Deja Vu (Add a [[Red Seal|]] to selected card in your hand Available from start. ) can apply Red Seals onto Club cards allowing for more Onyx Agate triggers.

## Anti-Synergies

### Decks

- The Checkered Deck ( ) cannot benefit from Onyx Agate due to lack of Clubs, unless combined with the Smeared Joker ( and count as the same suit, and count as the same suit Have at least [[Wild Cards|]] in your deck Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ).

### Jokers

- Seeing Double ( if played hand has a scoring card and a scoring card of any other suit Play a hand that contains <abbr title="Other suits that count as clubs (e.g. wild suits) with rank 7 will also count."></abbr> ) will only activate if a Club and another suit score, rendering it useless for Flush builds which is where Onyx Agate shines best.

 - Although, the bonus may still be activated if a Wild Card is used.

- While Four Fingers (All and can be made with 4 cards Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) can make Flushes easier to play, it enables the player to do so with less overall cards, therefore the scoring power of Onyx Agate is weakened if the player opts for a 4-card Club Flush.

 - It may be suitable to hunt for Planet cards and/or scaling Chip Jokers if Four Fingers must remain in conjunction with Onyx Agate.

### Blinds

- When facing The Club ( ), all Club cards are debuffed, meaning Onyx Agate cannot activate.

- The Pillar ( ) may be problematic as previously played Club cards of the Ante will be debuffed, rendering them useless for Onyx Agate.

## Trivia

- Onyx Agate is one of four stone/mineral themed jokers unlocked by having 30 cards of that suit in your deck. The others are Arrowhead () (spades), Bloodstone ( ) (hearts), and Rough Gem () (diamonds).

- In demo versions, the effects of Bloodstone ( ) (+ Mult) and Onyx Agate (XMult on probability) are swapped.

- Like Arrowhead (), this joker is inspired from an Onyx agate LocalThunk found on beach1].
