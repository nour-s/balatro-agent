# To the Moon

for every $5 you have at end of round Copyable Perishable Eternal

To the Moon is an Uncommon Economy Joker that gives an extra $1 interest for every $5 held at the end of a round. Extra interest earned by the card is based on how much money the player is holding and limited by the current interest cap ($5 by default). The interest cap can be increased by buying Seed Money () and Money Tree (). It is worth noting that the effect of To The Moon stacks with multiple copies of itself, gaining an extra $2 interest for every $5 held with two copies, an extra $3 with three copies, and so on and so forth.

## Synergies

### Decks

This Joker works best with decks that want a high amount of money, like Zodiac Deck ( Start run with , , and Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ), Nebula Deck ( Start run with the voucher <br> consumable slot Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ), and Painted Deck ( hand size, <br> Joker slot Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ), which tend to scale most through Tarot and Planet cards that are purchased in the shop. It also is powerful with Yellow Deck ( Start with extra Discover at least items from your [[Collection|collection]] ), as this deck tends to gain large amounts of interest earlier.

### Poker Hands

This card doesn't affect any hands.

### Jokers

As with many money-producing Jokers, it directly buffs the effects of Bull ( for each you have <br> }} ) and Bootstraps ( for every you have <br> }} Have at least Jokers at the same time. ). It also, indirectly, powers up Flash Card (This Joker gains per in [[The Shop|the shop]] <br> }} ), Red Card (This Joker gains when any [[Booster Packs|]] is skipped <br> }} ), Ceremonial Dagger (When [[Blinds|]] is selected, destroy [[Jokers|Joker]] to the right and permanently add its sell value to this <br> }} ), and other such economy-based scaling Jokers by producing more money to use in activating their effects.

Jokers that produce money mid-round, like Rough Gem (), Mail-In Rebate (Earn for each discarded [[Card Ranks|]], ), and Faceless Joker (), work very well with To the Moon, as the money produced counts towards interest earned at the end of the round.

Any other Joker that makes money, like Golden Joker (Earn at end of round Common Economy N/A (Passive) ), can be phased out in favor of this one, once you have enough interest going.

Being an Uncommon Joker, To the Moon also benefits from Baseball Card ([[Uncommon Jokers|]] ), as it provides X1.5 Mult alongside its economy benefits.

### Modifiers

- Gold Cards ( ) and Gold Seal () allow money generation mid-round, meaning the money they make counts toward interest earned at the end.

### Vouchers

- Seed Money () and Money Tree () have one of the strongest synergies with To The Moon, as they raise the base interest cap. With both vouchers, To the Moon can end up producing $40 at the end of every round.

### Decks

- Luxury Tax ( Hold cards in hand for every you have<br><br> hand size ) receives little benefit from To The Moon, as in order to take advantage of its high interest, the player would want high starting money, which this deck punishes.

### Jokers

- Vagabond (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] ) encourages playing without earning interest, making To The Moon useless.

- Credit Card (Go up to in debt Common Economy N/A (Passive) ) enables going $20 in debt, well below the minimum interest level.

### Blinds

- The Ox ( ) sets money to $0 when playing most played hand, which totably disables To the Moon (unless the player has Matador ())

- The Tooth ( ) deducts money for every card played, so try not to play any 5-card hand if possible.

## Trivia

- "[Going] to the moon" is a common phrase used by cryptocurrency traders to describe a stock that is rising incredibly fast. The card art depicts the price of a stock going up exponentially.

- Unfortunately, this Joker has no synergies with the tarot card The Moon (Converts up to selected cards to Available from start. ).
