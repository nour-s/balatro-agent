# Joker Stencil

slot

 (Currently X1) Copyable Perishable Eternal

Joker Stencil is an Uncommon Multiplicative Mult Joker that gives the player X1 Mult per empty Joker slot, including this Joker.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Black Deck ( Joker slot <br> hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] ) is good with this Joker, as this deck gives +1 Joker slot, which basically gives it an extra X1 Mult.

- Similarly, Five-Card Draw ( discards per round<br> hand size<br> Joker slots <br>Banned Jokers: , , ) is even better with this Joker, as this deck gives +2 Joker slots, which basically gives it an extra X2 Mult.

- Ghost Deck ( [[Spectral Cards|]] cards may appear in the shop, start with a card Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) can be potent with Joker Stencil thanks to its easier access to Spectral Cards, especially those mentioned in the Synergetic Spectral Cards section.

- Nebula Deck ( Start run with the voucher <br> consumable slot Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) is really good with this Joker, as it relies less on having many Jokers, thus freeing up slots.

- Zodiac Deck ( Start run with , , and Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) can be good with this Joker, as it gives more chances for cards other than Jokers to appear.

### Poker Hands

Strong five-card hands like Flush, Straight, or Full House synergize well with Joker Stencil thanks to their high base Mult.

### Jokers

- Any Negative ( Joker or Consumable slot Edition Joker (Negative).png) Joker works well with this, as they do not take up a Joker slot.

- Because the Joker Stencil () counts itself as an empty slot, multiple Joker Stencils combine brilliantly. If you have five slots, two Joker Stencils, and no other Jokers, they both provide X5 Mult for a combined total of X25 Mult. A negative joker stencil gives an extra X1 Mult to each Joker Stencil (the formula being XMult = Empty Joker slots + # of Joker Stencils).

- Invisible Joker (After rounds, sell this card to a random Joker <br> /2}} <br> ]] from copy)}} Win a run without ever having more than Rare Effect N/A (Passive) ) can be very good for duplicating the Joker Stencil, provided you can play around the loss of X1 Mult for two rounds before selling it.

- While Blueprint (Copies ability of [[Jokers|]] ) and Brainstorm (Copies the ability of leftmost [[Jokers|]] Discard a . ) both copy the main XMult effect of Joker Stencil, they do not take on the secondary effect of acting as an empty joker slot. This makes their synergies inadvisable if buying them takes the Joker Stencil down to X1 Mult, rendering it useless. However, as long as the Joker Stencil stays above X1 Mult, multiple copies of its XMult effect will provide a higher combined XMult than a singular copy of it, even if that means the XMult being copied is slightly lower (for example, X3 Mult < X2 Mult * X2 Mult).

- Any Joker giving Mult is good, as it's better to have a bigger base Mult to multiply off of. Jokers that can reach high levels of Mult by themselves, like Red Card (This Joker gains when any [[Booster Packs|]] is skipped <br> }} ), Flash Card (This Joker gains per in [[The Shop|the shop]] <br> }} ), or Bootstraps ( for every you have <br> }} Have at least Jokers at the same time. ), are really good, as you don't have to rely on multiple Jokers to get large amounts of Mult. Ceremonial Dagger (When [[Blinds|]] is selected, destroy [[Jokers|Joker]] to the right and permanently add its sell value to this <br> }} ) in particular always empties out a Joker slot when it gains +Mult.

## Anti-Synergies

### Decks

- Painted Deck ( hand size, <br> Joker slot Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) hamstrings Joker Stencil with its -1 Joker Slot, giving it X1 Mult less.

- Similarly, Cruelty ( give no reward money<br> give no reward money<br><br> Joker Slots ) hamstrings Joker Stencil even more with its -2 Joker Slots, giving it X2 Mult less.

- Plasma Deck ( Balance [[Chips|]] and [[Mult|]] when calculating score for played hand <br> base Blind size Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) is also generally not good with this Joker, as usually the player either wants a lot of chips for early antes or needs a lot more mult for later antes, more than what this Joker gives.

- 15 Minute City ( Eternal <br>Eternal Two copies of every face card;<br>no Aces, 2s, or 3s) and Blast Off ( hands per round<br> discards per round<br> Joker Slots Eternal <br>Eternal <br><br>Banned [[Vouchers]]: , <br>Banned Jokers: ) do not work well with Joker Stencil, as these decks both start with two Eternal () Jokers, which means Joker Stencil gives less XMult than usual. Not only that, but Blast Off ( hands per round<br> discards per round<br> Joker Slots Eternal <br>Eternal <br><br>Banned [[Vouchers]]: , <br>Banned Jokers: ) also reduces a Joker slot, which means Joker Stencil gives even less XMult than usual.

- Typecast ( When ante boss is defeated:<br>- all Jokers become <br>- set Joker slots to Banned [[Blinds]]: ) sets your Joker Slots to 0 after ante 4, which sets Joker Stencil to X0 Mult or less.

### Poker Hands

Weaker hands with low base Mult, like High Card, Pair, or Two Pair, have poor synergies with Joker Stencil, unless heavily leveled up using Planet Cards.

### Jokers

- Abstract Joker ( for each [[Jokers|]] card<br>}} ) requires filling up all Joker Slots to maximize its effect, and thus has anti-synergy with Joker Stencil.

- Riff-Raff (When [[Blind|]] is selected, create [[Common Jokers| ]] <br> ) adds two jokers per blind selected, reducing empty slots for Joker Stencil to use.

## Trivia

- A "stencil" is a thin piece of material, usually paper, metal, or plastic, that has letters or patterns cut out of it. The stencil is then used to print a design or sentence on a surface by applying ink or paint through the cut-out holes. This is reflected in the Joker's design, as the card has a joker that's been cut out of the middle of it.

- The center of the Joker Stencil texture is transparent, allowing you to see through it when moving this Joker around. However, when it is flipped face-down, it uses the same intact card back as the rest.
