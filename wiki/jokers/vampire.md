# Vampire

played, removes card Enhancement
 (Currently X1 Mult) Copyable Perishable Eternal

Vampire is an Uncommon scaling Multiplicative Mult Joker that removes Enhancements from your scored cards and permanently gains X0.1 Mult for each Enhancements when it does. It starts at X1 Mult. It removes Enhancements before their effect occurs, meaning that the enhancement will not affect your hand's score.

## Usage Notes

Vampire removes Enhancements before their effect occurs, meaning the Enhancements cannot activate. All scored cards will get their Enhancements removed. Vampire also cannot change the type of Poker Hand retroactively, for example removing Wild () would still keep the Poker Hand as Flush.

There are a few notable playing card interactions:

- Debuffed: Debuffed cards are considered not to have any Modifier including Enhancements, so Vampire cannot remove Enhancements from debuffed cards.

- Wild Cards (): wild enhancement gets removed, the card scores if it would have scored for the original hand, and the type of hand played is not affected.

- Glass Cards (<br> ): glass enhancement gets removed, no X2 Mult, and there is no chance for the card to break.

- Stone Cards (<br>): stone enhancement gets removed and card returns to what it originally was, no +50 Chips, but card will always score for this hand (the type of hand played is not affected).

- Editions: Editions are not Enhancements, will not get removed by Vampire, and work normally.

- Seals: Seals are not Enhancements, will not get removed by Vampire, and work normally.

The Joker order matters significantly when using certain other Jokers with Vampire:

- A second Vampire (): only the left-most Vampire can remove enhancements and scale at any time (regardless of how many Midas Masks are used).

- Midas Mask (All played cards become [[Gold Cards|]] ):

 - Putting Midas Mask before Vampire results in all scoring face cards turning Gold ( ), then feeding vampire, leaving them unenhanced.

 - Putting Vampire before Midas Mask results in all scoring enhanced face cards feeding vampire, then turning gold.

 - Putting a Midas Mask before Vampire, and a second Midas Mask after Vampire results in all scoring face cards turning gold, then feeding Vampire, then turning gold again.

- DNA (If of round has only card, ) copies an enhanced card if Vampire is after DNA, but copies the unenhanced card if Vampire is before DNA.

- Ride the Bus (This Joker gains per hand played without a scoring card <br> }} ) resets if Vampire is before Ride the Bus and removes the Stone enhancement from a face card. Ride the Bus does not reset if Vampire is positioned after Ride the Bus.

- Golden Ticket (Played [[Gold Cards|]] ) only works if there is a Midas Mask positioned after Vampire, with the positioning of Golden Ticket itself having no impact.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Zodiac Deck ( Start run with , , and Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ), Magic Deck ( Start run with the voucher and copies of Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) or the Ghost Deck ( [[Spectral Cards|]] cards may appear in the shop, start with a card Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) work well for the vampire, as these decks have more opportunities to obtain Tarots and Spectral Cards (see Consumables below) that can provide enhanced cards to feed the Vampire.

- Black Deck ( Joker slot <br> hand every round Discover at least items from your [[collection]] )'s additional Joker slot can be used for effect Jokers (like Midas Mask (All played cards become [[Gold Cards|]] )) to generate enhanced cards.

- Medusa ( Eternal 52 cards, with all face cards replaced by Stone cards) and Monolith ( Eternal <br>Eternal Negative ) Challenge Decks both starts with a Marble Joker (Adds one [[Stone Cards|]] card to the deck when [[Blinds|]] ), which Vampire can use it as an enhancement generator to scale easier. Not only that, Medusa also starts with 12 Stone Cards (<br>).

### Jokers

- Vampire combos well with jokers that either generate Tarot Cards, or enhancements themselves (such as Midas Mask (All played cards become [[Gold Cards|]] )) to generate a steady supply of Enhancements to eat.

- If using the Midas Mask combo, the order will affect this. If the Midas Mask is placed first, face cards will be changed to Gold Cards ( ), and the Vampire will immediately consume the effect to increase its multiplier. However, if the Midas Mask is placed after the Vampire, the cards are made golden without consuming the effect. Thus, players could create infinite enhancement supplies for Vampire either by having Midas Mask turn face cards to Gold Cards first then Vampire consumes them, or have some Gold Cards in advance and have Vampire consume them, and they immediately turns into Gold Cards again by Midas Mask. Pareidolia (All cards are considered cards Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) can be used with Midas Mask so that all scored cards turn into Gold Cards, giving Vampire even more mult.

- Jokers such as DNA (If of round has only card, ) or Marble Joker (Adds one [[Stone Cards|]] card to the deck when [[Blinds|]] ) also provide a good source of Enhancements (as you can play a single enhanced card with DNA before Vampire to copy it with its enhancement before feeding the Vampire).

- Splash (Every counts in scoring Common Effect N/A (Passive) ) is a useful supporting Joker that guarantees five enhanced cards to be scored, in the event that they can't form a five-card hand. If used with Midas Mask and Pareidolia, all 5 scored cards can become gold and give Vampire X0.5 Mult per hand.

- Since only the left-most of two held Vampire ()s can scale, it is recommended to continuously swap them around, keeping them at the same Mult to get the best score possible.

- Tarot Cards are useful to enhance cards and get Vampire scale faster, so look out for Jokers like Cartomancer (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when [[Blinds|]] is selected <br> Discover every Tarot Card. ), Vagabond (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] ) and/or Hallucination ( chance to create a [[Tarot Cards|]] card when any [[Booster Packs|]] is opened <br> Common Effect N/A (Passive) ).

- Sixth Sense (If of round is a single , destroy it and create a [[Spectral Cards|]] card <br> Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) and Séance (If [[Poker Hands|]] is a , create a random [[Spectral_Cards|]] card <br> ) can be used to create any of the 3 Spectral Cards that destroy cards but add Enhanced Cards in exchange, as well as Cryptid (Create copies of selected card in your hand Available from start. ) to scale Vampire faster. They can also create Spectral Cards that give Seals (like Talisman (Add a [[Gold Seal|]] to selected card in your hand Available from start. )) or Aura (Add [[Foil|]], [[Holographic|]], or [[Polychrome|]] effect to selected card in hand Available from start. ), since Seals and Editions are not affected by Vampire.

### Consumables

Vampire requires a large amount of enhanced cards to scale, therefore consumables that adds enhancement are important to scale this Joker fast:

- The Magician (Enhances selected cards to [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ), The Empress (Enhances selected cards to [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ) and The Hierophant (Enhances selected cards to [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ) are preferred as they enhance up to 2 cards at once.

- The Lovers (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ), The Chariot (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ), The Devil (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ), Justice (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ), and The Tower (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ) are a good alternate pick up that enhance 1 card.

- Death (Select cards, convert the card into the card<br> Available from start. ) can be used to duplicate an enhanced card as well.

- Familiar (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ), Grim (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Aces|]] to your hand Available from start. ), and Incantation (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card_Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Number cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ) are useful to increase enhanced cards number, but be wary that they could also destroy an enhanced card in your hand as well.

- Cryptid (Create copies of selected card in your hand Available from start. ) can be used on an already enhanced card.

 - Specially, with Ghost Deck or Jokers that generates Spectral Cards (like Séance (If [[Poker Hands|]] is a , create a random [[Spectral_Cards|]] card <br> )), it is possible to using above-mentioned cards during a round, which means multiple enhanced cards generated are in the hand and can be played at once to obtain lots of upgrades.

- Aura (Add [[Foil|]], [[Holographic|]], or [[Polychrome|]] effect to selected card in hand Available from start. ) also provide utility in that the edition of a card does not get eaten by the vampire: a Holographic ( Edition Joker (Holographic).png) card stays holographic after the hand is played, hence increasing the base mult of the hand.

- Talisman (Add a [[Gold Seal|]] to selected card in your hand Available from start. ), Deja Vu (Add a [[Red Seal|]] to selected card in your hand Available from start. ), Trance (Add a [[Blue Seal|]] to selected card in your hand Available from start. ) and Medium (Add a [[Purple Seal|]] to selected card in your hand Available from start. ) provide Seals to cards, which are not affected by Vampire, so they can be used without any problem.

### Vouchers

- Tarot Merchant ( ) and Tarot Tycoon ( ) may also prove useful to find more tarot cards to apply enhancement with.

- Illusion ( in shop may have an [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]], [[Card Modifiers#Edition|]], and/or a [[Card Modifiers#Seal|]] ) allows the player to buy enhancement cards in the Shop, which can also be useful to provides additional enhancement cards to feed the Vampire with. This can be further increased with help from Overstock ( ) and Overstock Plus ( ) to increase the shop selection, plus Reroll Surplus () and Reroll Glut () to make rerolling cheaper.

## Anti-Synergies

### Jokers

- Jokers that rely on specific card enhancements become harder to use when combined with Vampire. These include Lucky Cat (This Joker gains every time a [[Lucky Cards|]] card triggers <br> }} ), Glass Joker (This Joker gains for every [[Glass_Cards|]] that is destroyed <br> }} Have at least [[Glass_Cards|]] ), Golden Ticket (Played [[Gold Cards|]] ), Steel Joker (Gives for each [[Steel Cards|]] in your <br> }} ) and Stone Joker (Gives for each [[Stone Card|]] in your <br> }} ).

- Driver's License ( if you have at least Enhanced cards in your full deck <br> }} Enhance 16 cards in your deck Rare Multiplicative Mult Independent Driver') limits the players to not play any enhanced card, making a risky combo with Vampire.

### Vouchers

- Planet Merchant ( ) and Planet Tycoon ([[Planet Cards|]] ) reduce the probabilities of Tarot Cards appearing in the shop, and therefore, the XMult from Vampire. Magic Trick ( can be purchased from the [[The Shop|]] ) also applies here, but this one isn't too bad at all since it's required to get Illusion ( in shop may have an [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]], [[Card Modifiers#Edition|]], and/or a [[Card Modifiers#Seal|]] ).

## Trivia

- Vampire's effect reflects vampirism. It drains enhancements from cards, growing stronger while leaving them weaker.

- As compared to Joker ( ), the coloring of Vampire's eye and mouth makeup are swapped. This is likely to make his fangs more distinct.

- Vampire is the fourth Joker after Jimbo, Odd Todd (Played cards with [[Card Ranks|]] rank give when scored <br> ), and Even Steven (Played cards with [[Card Ranks|]] rank give when scored <br> ) to get a plush as merchandise from Makeship.1]
