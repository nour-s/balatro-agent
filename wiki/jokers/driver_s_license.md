# Driver's License

Enhanced cards in your full deck
 (Currently 0) Copyable Perishable Eternal

Driver's License is a Rare Multiplicative Mult Joker that provides X3 Mult if your full deck contains at least 16 enhanced cards. It is unlocked using this same condition.

It has little to no utility early on in a run due to its steep activation requirement, but by stacking a lot of enhancements early using other Jokers and Tarot cards, or opening lots of Standard packs, it can help secure a win by providing a significant boost in Mult, especially when stacked with other multiplicative Jokers.

It is important to note that it only provides X3 Mult once no matter how much enhanced cards you have in deck (having 32 enhanced cards will not trigger the effect twice).

### Poker Hands

Five card hands with high base Mult like Straight, Flush, Full House, etc. benefit greatly from Driver's License.

### Jokers

- Jokers that add enhanced cards to your deck or change cards into enhanced ones like Marble Joker (Adds one [[Stone Cards|]] card to the deck when [[Blinds|]] ), Midas Mask (All played cards become [[Gold Cards|]] ), or DNA (If of round has only card, ) can help activate Driver's License a lot sooner.

 - Combining Midas Mask with Pareidolia (All cards are considered cards Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) is extremely effective for this instance as it can turn all scored cards into Gold Cards ( ), activating Driver's License at a record pace.

- Vagabond (Create a [[Tarot Cards|]] ) can also create many enhanced cards very quickly.

- Be very wary when using Vampire (This Joker gains per scoring played, removes card <br> }} ) with this Joker. Because Vampire takes enhancements away to buff its own Mult, it will eventually cause Driver's License to become useless if you don't have a reliable way to supply more enhancements.

### Consumables

Driver's License requires a large amount of enhanced cards, therefore consumables that adds enhancement are important to get this Joker online fast:

- The Magician (Enhances selected cards to [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ), The Empress (Enhances selected cards to [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ), and The Hierophant (Enhances selected cards to [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ) are preferred as they enhance up to 2 cards at once.

- The Lovers (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ), The Chariot (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ), The Devil (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ), Justice (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ), and The Tower (Enhances selected card into a [[Card Modifiers|]] Available from start. ) are alternative Tarot Cards that enhance 1 card each.

- Death (Select cards, convert the card into the card<br> Available from start. ) can be used to duplicate an enhanced card as well.

- Familiar (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Face cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ), Grim (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Aces|]] to your hand Available from start. ), and Incantation (Destroy random card in your hand, add random [[Card_Modifiers#Enhancements|]] [[Card Ranks#Number cards|]] to your hand Available from start. ) are useful to increase the number enhanced cards in your deck, but be wary that they could destroy an enhanced card as well, subtracting one from the net gain.

- Cryptid (Create copies of selected card in your hand Available from start. ) can be used on an already enhanced card.

## Trivia

- The name of the driver in this Joker is "Jen Joker", with her date of birth being December 3rd, 1995. The license expired on January 24th, 2025. In the Balatro demo, the name used to read "Jeb Joker" instead (which can still be seen in the signature on the license).

- The art of this joker also displays some information that provided on common driver's licenses of the US/Canada:

 - DD number, the Document Discriminator which is a unique number on license to determine its authenticity, is 1104961341.

 - Height (HT) is 6'0".

 - Eye color (EY), usually represented by 2-3 letter abbreviations, is BRN (brown).

 - Organ donor status, displayed as a red symbol (usually a heart shape in reality) and the "donor" on it.

- Jen Joker has a degree from Lehigh University in Electrical Engineering and works in Aurora, Colorado. Jen has never met Jimbo.1]

- The number of enhanced cards needed to activate this Joker might be a reference to a common age required to be able to obtain a real driver's license.

 - In Colorado, Jen's likely state of residence, the minimum age to obtain a driver's license is 16.2]

 - Likewise, in LocalThunk's home province of Saskatchewan3], the minimum eligible driving age without an educational course is 164].
