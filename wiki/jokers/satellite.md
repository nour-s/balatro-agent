# Satellite

card used this run Copyable Perishable Eternal

Satellite is an Uncommon Economy Joker that gives $1 at the end of each round per unique Planet card used in the run. Players can earn up to $9 from it by using the normal Planet Cards, and an additional $3 can be earned in a run by playing the three secret hands.

Each planet card only needs to be used once in a run to count for Satellite. Black Hole (Upgrade every [[Poker Hands|]] by level Available from start. ) is a Spectral card, and will not increase the $ gained from Satellite, even if opened from within a Celestial booster pack.

## Usage Notes

Without a steady Economy, unlocking Satellite may prove to be a difficult task.

- The Yellow Deck ( Start with extra Discover at least items from your [[Collection|collection]] ) is a decent choice for the run to unlock Satellite as it starts the player with an extra $10, earning the player Interest from defeating the first Blind of the run, and setting up for further Economic gain.

- Economy Jokers such as Golden Joker (Earn at end of round Common Economy N/A (Passive) ), Mail-In Rebate (Earn for each discarded [[Card Ranks|]], ), etc. are directly helpful in reaching the unlock condition.

- The Bull ( for each you have <br> }} ) and Bootstraps ( for every you have <br> }} Have at least Jokers at the same time. ) Jokers both gain scoring power relative to the player's Money, which would be useful to have in a run that attempts to unlock Satellite as hoarding Money to reach $400 will also give it scoring power.

- Egg (Gains of at end of round Common Economy N/A (Passive) ) and Gift Card (Add of to every [[Jokers|]] and [[Consumables|]] card at end of round Uncommon Economy N/A (Passive) ) add sell value, making it possible to hold onto the Egg or a useless Joker/Consumable to sell later for a substantial boost in Money.

 - Swashbuckler (Adds the sell value of all other owned [[Jokers|]] to Mult <br> }} ) would quantify the sell value into Mult, which may help the player continue the run.

- To the Moon (Earn an extra of for every you have at end of round Uncommon Economy N/A (Passive) ), Seed Money (), and Money Tree () allow the player to earn more Interest, which may contribute to reaching the unlock condition faster.

 - All 3 of these combined allow for the player to earn a maximum of $40 from Interest alone, merely one-tenth of the required amount.

## Synergies

### Decks

- Checkered Deck ( ) works well with Satellite as it requires fewer Deck modifications to unlock the secret Poker Hands.

- Erratic Deck ( All [[Card Ranks|]] and [[Suits|]] in deck are randomized Win a run with any [[Decks|deck]] ) may require no Deck modifications at all to unlock the secret Poker Hands.

### Poker Hands

Playing the three secret hands (Flush House, Five of a Kind and Flush Five) to unlock their planets allows players to maximize this Joker's value.

### Jokers

- Since Satellite is maximized through the use of Planet Cards, it synergizes with Constellation (This Joker gains every time a [[Planet_Cards|]] card is used <br> }} ), which gains X0.1 Mult per planet card used.

- Satellite benefits from Astronomer (All cards and in the shop are Discover every card Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ), which makes planet cards and Celestial booster packs free.

- Smeared Joker ( and count as the same suit, and count as the same suit Have at least [[Wild Cards|]] in your deck Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) can make it much easier to unlock the secret hands, thereby maximizing Satellite's value.

### Consumables

- The High Priestess (Creates up to random [[Planet Cards|]] cards<br> Available from start. ) can potentially spawn Planet Cards not yet used by the player if found early, boosting Satellite.

### Vouchers

- Planet Merchant ( ) and Planet Tycoon ([[Planet Cards|]] ) increase the frequency of planet cards in the shop, making it easier to find different planet cards.

## Anti-Synergies

### Jokers

- Showman ([[Jokers|]], [[Tarot Cards|]], [[Planet Cards|]], and [[Spectral Cards|]] cards may appear multiple times Reach Ante level Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ) can have anti-synergy with Satellite, since it will allow duplicate planet cards to appear, and Satellite requires unique planet cards.

### Vouchers

- Telescope ([[Booster Packs|]] always contain the [[Planet Cards|]] card for your most played [[Poker Hands|]] ) can make it harder to achieve max rewards with Satellite, as it lends itself to the same planet card repeating.

 - This innately applies to the Nebula Deck ( Start run with the voucher <br> consumable slot Win a run with the on [[Stakes|any difficulty]] ) as it starts with the Telescope Voucher.

## Trivia

- In real life, satellites (also known as "artificial satellites" or "orbiters") are used to collect data and relay signals about the planet it orbits. This plays into the Joker's main effect of rewarding money for each unique planet "discovered" by obtaining their card.

- Although there have been many satellites sent to orbit other planets, most of them have been retired due to lost contact. Currently, the only planets that have active artificial satellites are Earth and Mars, which is why the art of the Joker depicts a satellite above a planet that resembles Earth.

- Satellite is a space-themed Joker, continuing the space theme of Jokers like Supernova (Adds the number of times [[Poker Hands|]] ), Constellation (This Joker gains every time a [[Planet_Cards|]] card is used <br> }} ), Astronomer (All cards and in the shop are Discover every card Uncommon Effect N/A (Passive) ), and Space Joker ( chance to upgrade level of played [[Poker Hands|]] ).
